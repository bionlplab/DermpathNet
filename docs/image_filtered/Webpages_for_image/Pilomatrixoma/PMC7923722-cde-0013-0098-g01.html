Pilomatrixoma with Atypical Features: A Case Report.

Histological features of pilomatrixoma with atypical features. a Hematoxylin and eosin (H&E) staining of paraffin-fixed tissue biopsy specimen viewed at 2× magnification. The tissue section demonstrates a nearly well-circumscribed dermal/subcutaneous nodule of basaloid cells and shadow cells. b H&E higher (10×) magnification of the tissue specimen showing focal infiltrating into subcutaneous fat at the periphery. c H&E, 20× magnification highlights comedonecrosis. d H&E, 20× magnification highlights cytological atypia and comedonecrosis. e H&E, 40× magnification highlights increase in mitotic activity in the lesion.

PMID: 33708090

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Follicular
