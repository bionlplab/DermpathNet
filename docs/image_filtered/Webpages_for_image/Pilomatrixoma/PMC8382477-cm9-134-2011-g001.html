Pilomatrixoma misdiagnosed as dermatofibrosarcoma protuberans.

Clinical and pathological images of the case: (A) An asymptomatic, firm, reddish mass on the posterior aspect of the neck of a 29-year-old woman. (B) Punch biopsy of the skin showing diffuse proliferation of spindle cells in the dermis with an unclear boundary (Hematoxylin-eosin staining, Original magnification ×10). (C, D) Postoperative pathological examination showing a mass composed of basophils, shadow cells, and calcium in the lower dermis and subcutaneous fat [Hematoxylin-eosin staining, Original magnification ×10 (C), ×100 (D)].

PMID: 33840746

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Follicular
