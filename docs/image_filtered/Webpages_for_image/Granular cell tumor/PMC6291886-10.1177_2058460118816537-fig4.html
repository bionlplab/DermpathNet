Granular cell tumor of the breast mimicking malignancy: a case report with a literature review.

(a) High-power photomicrograph (original magnification, ×100; H&E stain) shows infiltrating nests and cords of large polygonal cells, separated by prominent fibrous septa. (b) High-power photomicrograph (original magnification, ×400; H&E stain) depicts abundant granular eosinophilic cytoplasm and small nuclei within the cells, features indicative of GCT. (c) Immunohistochemistry shows diffuse reactivity for S100 protein. Nuclei are small and bland.

PMID: 30559976

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
