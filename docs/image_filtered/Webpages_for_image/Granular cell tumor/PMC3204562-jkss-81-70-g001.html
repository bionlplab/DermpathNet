Synchronous granular cell tumors in the perianus and chest wall.

Histopathological findings of the perianal mass. The lesion was characterized by polygonal cells with abundant eosinophilic granular cytoplasm, and an immunohistochemical study revealed that the cells were positive for the S-100 (A, H&E, ×1; B, H&E, ×40; C, H&E, ×400; D, S-100 protein).

PMID: 22066104

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
