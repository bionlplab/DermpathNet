Granular Cell Tumor of the Common Hepatic Duct as an Unusual Cause of Jaundice in a Hepatitis C Patient.-DermPath

Granular Cell Tumor of the Common Hepatic Duct as an Unusual Cause of Jaundice in a Hepatitis C Patient.

Photomicrographs showing a proliferation of tumor cells within the bile duct mucosa and submucosa. The cells have eosinophilic and granular cytoplasm, with no significant cytologic atypia. No mitosis is identified. H&E stain at (A) x20 magnification and (B) x200 magnification.

PMID: 26958564

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
