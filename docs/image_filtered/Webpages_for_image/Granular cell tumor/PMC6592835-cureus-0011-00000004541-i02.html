Granular Cell Pituitary Tumor in a Patient with Multiple Endocrine Neoplasia-1.

Neuropathological analysis.Morphologic and immunophenotypic characteristics are consistent with granular cell tumor of the pituitary gland. Proliferation of ovoid and spindle cells in loose nests and fascicles with indistinct cytoplasmic borders. Abundant eosinophilic granules are present in the cytoplasm while nuclei exhibit moderate atypia and are variable in size. A) Granular cell tumor 10x, B) granular cell tumor 40x, C) S100 10x, D) epithelial membrane antigen 10x.

PMID: 31275768

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
