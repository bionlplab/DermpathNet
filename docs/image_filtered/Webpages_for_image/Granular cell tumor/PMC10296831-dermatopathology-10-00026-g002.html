Expression of Connexin 43 in Granular Cell Tumors of the Skin, Tongue and Esophagus.

(a) GCT of the tongue. Poorly defined lesion composed of sheets separated by collagenous bands (H&E, ×40). (b) Immunohistochemistry for Cx43 showing diffuse positivity in the lesional cells (×40). (c) Cytoplasmatic positivity of the tumors cell by immunostaining with Cx43 (Cx43, ×400). (d) GCT of the esophagus. The tumor is well circumscribed and infiltrates the muscularis mucosae (H&E, ×40). (e) Expression of Cx43 in regular keratinocytes of the superficial epithelium and positivity of Cx43 in dermal parts of the GCT (Cx43, ×40). (f) Cytoplasmic positivity of the tumor cells observed in the immunostaining for Cx43 (×400).

PMID: 37366801

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
