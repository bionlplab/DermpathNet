Aggressive granular cell tumor of the neurohypophysis with optic tract edema and invasion into third ventricle.

Pathology findings. H & E stain demonstrated epithelioid to vaguely spindled cells with round to ovoid nuclei and granular eosinophilic cytoplasm (a, b). PAS stain highlighted the cytoplasmic granularity (c). Positive nuclear staining for thyroid transcription factor-1 (d).

PMID: 31819811

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
