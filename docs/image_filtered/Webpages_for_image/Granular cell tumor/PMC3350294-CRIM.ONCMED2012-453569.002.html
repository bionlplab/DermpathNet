A malignant granular cell tumor excised with mohs micrographic surgery.

(Hematoxylin and eosin original magnification, 400x). The classic granular cell tumor, composed of a collection of polygonal or round cells with centrally placed nuclei and coarsely granular eosinophilic cytoplasm. Note the lack of nuclear atypia and mitotic figures.

PMID: 22606454

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
