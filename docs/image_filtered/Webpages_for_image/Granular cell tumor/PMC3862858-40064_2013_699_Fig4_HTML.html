Granular cell tumor of the appendix: a new case and review of the literature.

The tumor granules are immunoreactive for S-100 protein (A, original magnification x40), NSE (B, original magnification x40) and calretinin (C, original magnification x40) with a variable range of intensity. The tumor cell nests are surrounded by plasmocytes and lymphocytes.

PMID: 24349953

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
