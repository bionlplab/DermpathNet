Rare Mesenchymal Breast Entities that Mimic Malignancy: A Single-institution Experience of a Challenging Diagnosis.

Histopathological section of GCT (patient #4). (A) H&E staining shows nests of tumor cells distributed between collagen bands with ill-defined borders and infiltrative growth into the fat (40x magnification). (B) The tumor cells are large and polygonal with finely granular eosinophilic cytoplasm and round bland nuclei (200x magnification). (C) Tumor cells exhibit immunoreactivity for CD68 by immunohistochemistry (×200 magnification). (D) S-100 staining displaying positive activity on the granular cells, highlighting cytoplasmic granularity (x100 magnification).GCT: granular cell tumor; H&E: hematoxylin and eosin

PMID: 30989009

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
