Expression of Connexin 43 in Granular Cell Tumors of the Skin, Tongue and Esophagus.

(a) Granular cell tumor with exophytic growth and central erosion. (b) Reactive hyperplasia of the epidermis (H&E, ×40) and tumor cells with trabecular growth pattern (inset, H&E, ×200). (c) Expression of Cx43 in regular keratinocytes of the epidermis and positive staining of Cx43 in dermal parts of the GCT (Cx43, ×40). Note the cytoplasmic immunostaining of tumor cells (inset, ×200). (d) Expression of Sox10 with nuclear staining of tumor cells (Sox10, ×40), (inset, ×200).

PMID: 37366801

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
