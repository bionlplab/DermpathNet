Case report of a breast granular cell tumor in a young transgender man.

Representative H&E-stained images from the resected tumor taken at 2× (A) and 20× (B) objective power. The tumor is well-circumscribed and composed of sheets of polygonal cells with granular eosinophilic cytoplasm.

PMID: 35364393

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
