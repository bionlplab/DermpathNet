Congenital Non-Neural Granular Cell Tumor Mimicking Nevus Lipomatosus Superficialis.

(A, B) Diffuse infiltrate of monomorphic tumor cells in the dermis (H&E; A: ×100, B: ×200). (C) Large, polygonal tumor cells (arrows) with abundant granular cytoplasm (×400). (D, E) Immunohistochemical stainings revealed showing strong positivity for CD68 and vimentin (×200, respectively). (F, G) Immunohistochemical stain for S-100 and SMA were negative (S-100, ×200; SMA, ×100). (H) Periodic acid Schiff (PAS) highlights cytoplasmic granules after diastase digestion (PAS, ×400).

PMID: 29200768

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
