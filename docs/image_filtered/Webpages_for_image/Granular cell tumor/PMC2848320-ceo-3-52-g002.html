Granular cell tumor on larynx.

(A) The cells contain innumerable fine cytoplasmic granules (H&E, ×200). (B) The cytoplasm of tumor cells contains periodic acid-Schiff-positive materials (H&E, ×40). (C) S-100 immunohistochemical staining shows positive granular cells possessiveness, denoting its neuroectodermal origin (H&E, ×200). (D) Neuron-specific enolase immunohistochemical staining shows positive granular cells possessiveness, denoting its neuroectodermal origin (H&E, ×100). (E) Tumor cells revealed no immunoreactivity for smooth muscle actin (H&E, ×100).

PMID: 20379404

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
