Multiple granular cell tumors with metachronous occurrence in tongue and vulva. Clinicopathological and immunohistochemical study.

The tumor cells close to the epithelium of lingual tumor were associated with a CD 105 positivity in the vascular network unlike the remaining vessels that were negative for endoglin ie CD 105 (IHC stain, x400)
