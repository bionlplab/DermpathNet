Malignant granular cell tumor in the thoracic wall: A case report.

H&E staining of tumor tissue. (A, B), some cells are spindle shaped, the nucleus of the tumor cell can be seen to be slightly enlarged, with obvious nucleoli, a high nucleus-to-cytoplasmic ratio, and nuclear fission. (red arrow) (H&E, 200 ×).

PMID: 36203431

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
