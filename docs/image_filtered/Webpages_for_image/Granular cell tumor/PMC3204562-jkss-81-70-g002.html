Synchronous granular cell tumors in the perianus and chest wall.

Histopathological findings of the chest wall mass. The findings of the chest wall mass were similar to those of the perianal mass; the cells of the perianal mass were also positive for the S-100 protein (A, H&E, ×40; B, H&E, ×400; C, S-100 protein).

PMID: 22066104

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
