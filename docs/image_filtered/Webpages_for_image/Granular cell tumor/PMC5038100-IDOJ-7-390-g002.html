Granular cell tumor in a child: An uncommon cutaneous presentation.

Hematoxylin and eosin stained sections of the excised tissue shows (a) a polypoidal growth with hyperkeratosis of the epidermis and diffusely infiltrating sheets of polygonal cells in the papillary and reticular dermis (100x). (b) High power view showing abundant eosinophilic granular cytoplasm (400x)

PMID: 27730035

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
