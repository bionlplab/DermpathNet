Eccrine Poroma with Rapid Growth during Pregnancy: A Case Report and Review of the Literature

(A) A thick epithelial mass consisted of tumor cells that deeply infiltrate into dermis (H&E, ×40). (B) The monomorphous basophilic tumor cells with clear cytoplasm in an uneven distribution (H&E, ×400). (C) The tumor cells stained focally positive for epithelial membrane antigen (×400) and (D) with periodic acid-Schiff stain which indicates glycogen (×400).
