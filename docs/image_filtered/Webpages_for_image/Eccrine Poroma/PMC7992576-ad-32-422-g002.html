A Case of Eccrine Poromatosis in a Patient with a History of Chemotherapy Due to Stomach Cancer

(A) The tumor consisted of broad anastomosing bands without peripheral palisading. The border between the epidermis and tumor was apparent (H&E, ×40). (B) The tumor cells were small and showed a uniform cuboidal appearance with basophilic, round nuclei. The tumor cells were connected by intercellular bridges (H&E, ×400).

PMID: 33911778

Type: Benign, Category: Adnexal
