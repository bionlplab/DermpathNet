Pigmented Eccrine Poroma in abdominal region, a rare presentation

Hematoxylin and eosin staining, 10X and 40 x; Instrument Olimpus. Panel A and B are 10 x amplification, C and D are 40 x amplification. note proliferation of small keratinocytes emerging from the epithelium with epithelial tracts that anastomose within the dermis, some cavities lined by a eosinophilic cuticle (A,B), Carcinoembryonic Antigen (CEA) highlights positive in the elongated cells lining the cavities (C), the S100 marker is observed in dendritic cells and keratinocytes in the thickness of the cell proliferation (D).

PMID: 24892457

Type: Benign, Category: Adnexal
