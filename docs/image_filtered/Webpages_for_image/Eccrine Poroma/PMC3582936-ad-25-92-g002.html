Eccrine Poroma on the Postauricular Area: A Rare Presentation

(A) The tumor consists of broad anastomosing bands extending from the epidermis to the dermis. (B) Small, irregular ductal lumina were found within the tumor. (C) A view at higher magnification revealed that the tumor cells are connected intercellular bridges and have a uniform small cuboidal appearance and a round, deeply basophilic nucleus (H&E stain; A: ×40, B: ×100, C: ×400).

PMID: 23467402

Type: Benign, Category: Adnexal
