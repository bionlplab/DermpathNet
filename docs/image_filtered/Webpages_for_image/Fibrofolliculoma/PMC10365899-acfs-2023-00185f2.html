Solitary fibrofolliculoma on the nasal septum: a case report.

Histopathological photograph showing a fibrofolliculoma. (A) Low-power appearance. Cystically dilated hair follicles (black bullets) with thin proliferating epithelial strands (black asterisks) emanating from them (hematoxylin and eosin stain, × 20). (B) High-power appearance. Proliferating epithelial strands (black asterisks) with little cellular atypia surrounded by dense fibrotic stroma (empty asterisks) (hematoxylin and eosin stain, × 200).

PMID: 37415472

Type: Benign, Category: Follicular
