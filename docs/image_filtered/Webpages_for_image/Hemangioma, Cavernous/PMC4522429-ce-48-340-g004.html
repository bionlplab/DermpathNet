Single Cavernous Hemangioma of the Small Bowel Diagnosed by Using Capsule Endoscopy in a Child with Chronic Iron-Deficiency Anemia.

Microphotographs of the lesion showed irregularly dilated vascular spaces in the submucosal and muscle layers (A, H&E stain, ×40) and blood-filled vascular structures lined by a single layer of endothelial cells (B, H&E stain, ×100).

PMID: 26240811

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
