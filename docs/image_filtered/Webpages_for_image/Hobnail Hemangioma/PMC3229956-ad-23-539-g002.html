Congenital and multiple hobnail hemangiomas.

(A) Wedge-shaped architecture and dilated vessels in the upper dermis (H&E, ×40). (B) Intraluminal papillary projection and hobnail endothelial cells lining superficial vessels (H&E, ×400). (C) Collagen-dissecting, rather narrow vessels and numerous extravasated erythrocytes in deeper dermis (H&E, ×400).

PMID: 22148030

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
