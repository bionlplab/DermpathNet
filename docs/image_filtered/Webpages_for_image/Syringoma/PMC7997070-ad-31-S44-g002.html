Chondroid Syringoma of the Leg with Unusual Presentations.

(A) Low power view shows nodular dermal mass with overlying epidermis of increased basal pigmentation (H&E, ×40). (B) Low power view shows abundant basophilic stroma (H&E, ×100). (C) High power histologic feature shows a nodular dermal tumor composed of branching tubular epithelial elements embedded in a myxoid stroma (H&E, ×200). No atypical cells are observed.

PMID: 33911693

Type: Benign, Category: Adnexal
