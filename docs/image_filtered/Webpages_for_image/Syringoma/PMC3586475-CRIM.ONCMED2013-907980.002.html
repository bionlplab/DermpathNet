Facial localization of malignant chondroid syringoma: a rare case report.

Histopathological examination of nasolabially excised mass. Tumor had a benign nature mostly, with an expansive growing pattern and a few pleomorphic, atypical cells, and rare mitotic activity. In addition, generalized lymph vascular invasion was present ((a) HE, ×100; (b) HE, ×200).

PMID: 23476847

Type: Benign, Category: Adnexal
