Chondroid syringoma: a diagnosis by fine needle aspiration cytology.

(a) Cellular aspirates composed of bland, monomorphic cells arranged in sheets and loose clusters with few single cells in the myxoid background (Hematoxylin–Eosin stain, × 10). Inset shows groups of monomorphic epithelial cells having round-to-oval, centrally located nuclei, evenly dispersed fine chromatin and moderate amount of cytoplasm (Hematoxylin–Eosin stain, × 40); (b) Myoepithelial cells embedded in a chondromyxoid matrix (Hematoxylin–Eosin stain, × 40)

PMID: 23112529

Type: Benign, Category: Adnexal
