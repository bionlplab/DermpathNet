Incidental Clear Cell Syringoma of the Scalp in a Patient With Lichen Planopilaris.

Clear cell syringoma and lichen planopilarisLow magnification shows a clear cell syringoma (solid black arrowhead); some of the syringoma have tails of epithelial cells (red arrow). A syringoma duct is also present (green arrow). In the adjacent dermis, there are features of lichen planopilaris: an inflamed hair follicle (black star), which has perifollicular fibrosis (black arrow) and chronic inflammation (open black arrowhead). (Hematoxylin and eosin, 100x).

PMID: 34345549

Type: Benign, Category: Adnexal
