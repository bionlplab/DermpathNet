Glomus tumor of the hand.

Images showing pathologic features of glomangiomyoma(A) The tumor is composed of proliferating spindle smooth muscle cells with dilated blood vessels as well as round glomus cells. Characteristically, the glomus cells undergo a transition to spindle smooth muscle cells (H&E, ×400). (B) The spindle smooth muscle cells and the round glomus cells are both positive for smooth muscle actin (immunohistochemistry for smooth muscle actin, ×400).

PMID: 26015884

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
