Malignant glomus tumor of the index finger.

Photomicrographs of the tumor. A – shows a well-circumscribed tumor within the dermis arranged in solid sheets and nest (H&E, x2); B – the tumor cells are round to oval, show moderate nuclear pleomorphism and eosinophilic cytoplasm. Note the necrosis (H&E, 10x); C – few bizarre cells and atypical mitoses seen. (H&E x40); D – Tumor cells are Infiltrating the adjacent soft tissue (H&E x4).

PMID: 33344314

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
