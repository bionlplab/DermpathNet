Incidental gastric glomus tumor after laparoscopic sleeve gastrectomy.

The histopathological photomicrography of the tumor. A - Solid lesion composed by sheets of uniform cells. Small blood vessels are uniformly distributed within the tumor but may not be apparent (H&E, 2X); B - Solid glomus tumor showing monomorphic round cells with eosinophilic cytoplasm and central punched-out nuclei. (H&E, 40X).

PMID: 27284541
