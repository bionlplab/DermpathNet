Glomus tumor of the stomach: a rare cause of upper gastrointestinal bleeding.

(a) Sheets of glomus cells with eosinophilic cytoplasm distributed around dilated blood vessels with stasis (H.E. ×1000). (b) Glomus tumor of the stomach. Positive staining for type IV Collagen (×1000).
