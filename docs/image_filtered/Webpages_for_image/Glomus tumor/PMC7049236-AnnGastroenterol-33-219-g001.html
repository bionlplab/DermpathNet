Laparoscopic endoscopic cooperative surgery for a gastric glomus tumor previously diagnosed by endoscopic ultrasound fine-needle biopsy.

Endoscopic ultrasound fine-needle biopsy pathology. (A) Proliferating oval-shaped cells in a small nest formation surrounded by many capillary vessels (H/E, ×10 magnification). (B) Immunohistochemistry staining for CD 34 was positive in endothelial cells, but negative in neoplastic cells (×10). (C) Positive staining for smooth muscle actin and (D) vimentin in neoplastic cells (×10)

PMID: 32127744

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
