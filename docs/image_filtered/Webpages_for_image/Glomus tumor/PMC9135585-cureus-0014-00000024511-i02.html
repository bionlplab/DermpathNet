Gastric Glomus Tumor: A Rare Cause of Acute Blood Loss Anemia.

H&E staining of GGT: (A) 2x magnification showing a well-circumscribed cellular neoplasm in muscularis propria; (B) 20x magnification; and (C) 40x magnification showing nests of small, uniform, and rounded cells surrounding capillary-sized vessels. No cytologic atypia seen. (D) Immunohistochemical study showing the tumor cells strongly positive for SMAH&E: Hematoxylin and eosin; GGT: gastric glomus tumor; SMA: smooth muscle actin

PMID: 35651378

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
