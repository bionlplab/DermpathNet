Rare gastric neoplasm: Malignant glomus tumor of the stomach. A case report.

Tumor is within gastric wall composed of solid sheets of tumor cells (A) (H&E 40×). Tumor cells are epithelioid (at upper part of the picture) as well as spindled (at lower part of the picture), and featuring moderate nuclear pleomorphism (B) (H&E 400×).

PMID: 33887847

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
