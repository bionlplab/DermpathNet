A case of familial cutaneous collagenoma.

(A) Skin biopsy from the lower abdomen revealed slightly homogenized condensed collagen and decreased elastic fibers in the dermis (H&E, ×40). (B) Excessive accumulation of collagen in the dermis (Masson's trichrome stain, ×40). (C) Decreased and fragmented elastic fibers in the dermis (Verhoeff-van Gieson stain, ×40)

PMID: 22028557

Type: Benign, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
