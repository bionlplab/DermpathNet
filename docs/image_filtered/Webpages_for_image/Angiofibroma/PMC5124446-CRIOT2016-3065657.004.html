Angiofibroma Originating outside the Nasopharynx: A Management Dilemma.

Richly vascular lesion which has variable-sized thin-walled vessels surrounded by a fibroblastic stroma. The vessels have a single endothelial cell lining without a muscularis layer (H&E 20x).

PMID: 27957369

Type: Benign, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
