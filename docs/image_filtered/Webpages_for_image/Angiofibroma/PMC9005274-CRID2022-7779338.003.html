Angiofibroma of the Mandible: Report of a Rare Case.

Hematoxylin and eosin stain (H&E) showing the proliferation of fibrous tissue with abundant variably sized vascular channels interspersed among the spindle cells in background, consistent with the diagnosis of angiofibroma (10x).
