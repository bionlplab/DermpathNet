Recurrent Pediatric Extranasopharyngeal Angiofibroma of the Epiglottis: Case Report.

Transoral endoscopic ultrasonic surgery (TOUSS) and histological findings. a) TOUSS setup with retractor and scope holder; b) Hematoxylin & Eosin (H&E) (10X): Extranasopharyngeal angiofibroma presenting as a polypoid lesion. Sharply demarcated mass containing multiple vascular profiles. A non-specific superficial zone with squamous mucosa covers the luminal aspect of the tumor. Hemangiopericytoma-like abundant vascular pattern. c) H&E (40X): A higher magnification shows spindled cells containing uniform nuclei with delicate nucleoli. A coarse collagenous matrix is a consistent finding; d) IHQ for CD34 shows positivity in vascular channels and is negative in fusocellular components

PMID: 37727818

Type: Benign, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
