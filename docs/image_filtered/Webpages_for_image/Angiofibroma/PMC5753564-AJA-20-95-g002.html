A rare cellular angiofibroma of the epididymis.

Macroscopic examination shows (a) the appearance of the tumor which was cut into 3 pieces; (b) Showing short spindle-shaped cells by hematoxylin and eosin (H & E) staining, scale bar = 50 μm; and (c) blood vessels with thick-wall as well as remarked hyalinization of the blood vessel walls in low power, scale bar = 100 μm and (d) in higher power, scale bar = 50 μm.

PMID: 28303808

Type: Benign, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
