Angiofibroma of Soft Tissue: A Newly Described Entity; A Case Report and Review of Literature.-DermPath

Angiofibroma of Soft Tissue: A Newly Described Entity; A Case Report and Review of Literature.

A: Low power view showing a well-circumscribed nodular lesion with relatively thick capsule (H/E, 40x). B: Prominent vascular pattern consisting of numerous small thin-walled vessels and occasional ectatic vessels (H/E, 200x). C: There is regional variation in cellularity with the stroma variably myxoid to collagenous (H/E, 40x). D: High power view showing uniform bland spindle cells with inconspicuous cytoplasm and ovoid or tapering nuclei. Occasional mast cells are seen, black arrow (H/E, 400x).

PMID: 31890425

Type: Benign, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
