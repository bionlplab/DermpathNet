Abdominal Kaposiform Hemangioendothelioma Associated With Lymphangiomatosis Involving Mesentery and Ileum: A Case Report of MRI, CT, and 18F-FDG PET/CT Findings.

Small bowel capsule endoscopy showed grape-shaped red nodules in the luminal wall of the ileum (A). Photomicrograph (C, hematoxylin and eosin, original magnification ×20) showed proliferation and dilation of the mucosal lymphatics of the ileum, containing a large amount of red blood cells. In the abdominal mass, there are many irregular tumor nodules (arrow) (B, hematoxylin and eosin, original magnification ×20). Marked lymphatic proliferation containing red blood cells (arrow) encircled the tumor nodules (D, hematoxylin and eosin, original magnification ×100).

PMID: 26871848

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Vascular
