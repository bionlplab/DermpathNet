Cutaneous Vascular Neoplasms of Uncertain Biological Behavior.

An example of kaposiform hemangioendothelioma. Histopathologic examination on low power demonstrates a vascular proliferation involving connective and subcutaneous tissue in a lobular configuration ((A), H&E 2×; on higher magnification the nodules are formed of spindled shaped endothelial cells with cleft-like spaces at the periphery ((B), H&E 20×).

PMID: 34827153

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Vascular
