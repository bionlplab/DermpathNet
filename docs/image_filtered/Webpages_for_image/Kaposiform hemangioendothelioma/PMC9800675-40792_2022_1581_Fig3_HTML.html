Infantile Kaposiform hemangioendothelioma in a female patient complicated with severe obstructed jaundice: a case report.

Histopathological findings of the cystic duct. A Macroscopic findings of the gallbladder. The cystic duct positioned close to the hepatoduodenal ligament is a clue to the diagnosis of Kaposiform hemangioendothelioma (white line square). B H&E stained section of the peri-cystic duct. Hypervascular and polynodular lesions were observed around the resected CD (× 40). CD, cystic duct. C H&E stained section of the peri-cystic duct. Proliferated spindle cells formed slit-like lumens filled with red blood cells (× 400)
