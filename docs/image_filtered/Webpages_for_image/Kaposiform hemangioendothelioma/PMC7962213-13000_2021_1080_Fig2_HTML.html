Kaposiform Hemangioendothelioma: clinicopathological characteristics of 8 cases of a rare vascular tumor and review of literature.

a H&E stained sections of cutaneous KHE, showing glomeruloid nodules of tumor infiltrating into subcutaneous adipose tissue. b In the dermis, the tumor nodules infiltrate through adnexal structures with entrapment of residual bi-layered eccrine glandular acini. c Tumor nodules with surrounding lymphangioma-like vascular spaces. d Tumor nodules showing few scattered multinucleated giant cells, which is a rare finding in KHE. D Inset) Microthrombi seen within capillary lumen

PMID: 33722245

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Vascular
