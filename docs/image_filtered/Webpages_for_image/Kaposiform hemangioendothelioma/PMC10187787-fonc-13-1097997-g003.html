Case Report: Pancreatic and hepatic kaposiform hemangioendothelioma presenting as consumptive coagulopathy and right hepatic atrophy.

Histological examination and immunohistochemical staining of the pancreatic mass. (A) In HE staining, the tumor showed nestlike distribution of spindle-shaped endothelial cells (black arrow) with extensive infiltration of the pancreatic mesenchym. Original magnification: × 100. (B) Spindle endothelial cells aligned to form slit-like vascular lumina containing erythrocytes (black arrow). Original magnification: × 200. (C) CD34 positivity on immunohistochemical staining; (D) CD31 positivity on immunohistochemical staining; (F) D2-4 positivity on immunohistochemical staining; (E) Glut-1 negativity on immunohistochemical staining. Original magnification: × 200.

PMID: 37205203

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Vascular
