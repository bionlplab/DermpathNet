Infantile Myofibroma: A Report of Two Cases with Differential Diagnoses.

(a) Low magnification of tumor showing central hemangiopeicytomatous areas (×100, H and E stain); inset shows low magnification of the same tumor with characteristic zonation (H and E stain, ×100) (b) High magnification of tumor showing plump oval to spindle cells arranged around one vascular space (H and E stain, ×400). (c) Low magnification showing desmin negative tumor along with immunopositive muscle fibers (×100). (d) High magnification of tumor showing patchy SMA positivity (×400)
