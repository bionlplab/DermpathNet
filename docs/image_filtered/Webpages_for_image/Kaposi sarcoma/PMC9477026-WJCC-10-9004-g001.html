Coexistent Kaposi sarcoma and post-transplant lymphoproliferative disorder in the same lymph nodes after pediatric liver transplantation: A case report.

Histopathological changes observed in this case. A: Lymph nodes exhibited multifocal hyperplasia of spindle cells around small blood vessels, mainly beneath the capsule (arrows), and many lymphoid cells in the cortical and medullary regions of the lymph node (hematoxylin and eosin staining, × 40); B: Spindle cells exhibited minimal atypia (large arrow, upper left); heavy infiltration of plasma cells (small arrow, lower right) (hematoxylin and eosin staining, × 100).

PMID: 36157646

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Vascular
