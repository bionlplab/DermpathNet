A Series of Patients with Kaposi Sarcoma (Mediterranean/Classical Type): Case Presentations and Short Update on Pathogenesis and Treatment.

Kaposi sarcoma. Histopathology (H&E); a) Under a slight acanthotic epidermis vascular tumour with partially high cellularity (x40); b) Atypical spindle cells with small slit-like vascular spaces. Preexisting vessels are in part surrounding by spindle cells, this way appearing freely floating, so-called promontory sign (x100); c) Vascular lining by atypical, in part hyperchromatic spindle cells. Extravasated red blood cells (x400); d) In part additional plasma cells in the infiltrate (x400)

PMID: 30337990

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Vascular
