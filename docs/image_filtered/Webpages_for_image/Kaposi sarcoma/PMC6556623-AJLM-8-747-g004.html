Coexistence of Kaposi sarcoma and Molluscum contagiosum on the same site in a HIV-AIDS patient: A very rare occurrence.

(a) Section showing a lobular lesion composed of enlarged keratinocytes with central eosinophilic molluscum bodies. (b) Section showing plump spindle cells with bland nuclei delimiting slit-like vascular spaces, consistent with Kaposi sarcoma. Haematoxylin and eosin staining X 200.

PMID: 31205867

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Vascular
