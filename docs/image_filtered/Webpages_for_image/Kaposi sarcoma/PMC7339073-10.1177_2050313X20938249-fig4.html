A case of classic Kaposi sarcoma in an immunocompetent human immunodeficiency virus-negative Dominican man.

Photomicrographs of the histology and immunohistochemistry of Kaposi sarcoma (KS) in the soft palate biopsy showing: (a) low power view of nodular skin lesion with atypical spindle cell proliferation (H&E, 40×), (b) atypical spindle cell proliferation with extravasation (100×), and (c) positive immunostaining with an antibody to human herpesvirus-8 (HHV-8)-positive for spindle cells (100×).
