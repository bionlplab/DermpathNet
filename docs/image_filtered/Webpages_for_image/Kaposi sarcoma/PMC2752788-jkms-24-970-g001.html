Kaposi sarcoma herpes virus-associated hemophagocytic syndrome complicated by multicentric castleman disease and kaposi sarcoma in a HIV-negative immunocompetent patient: an autopsy case.

Histologic features of initial lymph node biopsy specimen. (A) At low magnification, lymphoid follicular hyperplasia was observed with preserved nodal architecture (H&E, ×12.5). (B) Plasma cells were markedly increased in the interfollicular area (H&E, ×200).
