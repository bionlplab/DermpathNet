Colesional cutaneous talaromycosis (penicilliosis) and Kaposi sarcoma in an HIV-infected patient.

Histopathologic findings of the inflamed area. (A) Intact epidermis with dense upper dermis inflammation. Obscured spindle cell proliferation in the upper to middle dermis (H&E, ×100). (B) Multiple fungal yeasts in the background of histiocytic inflammation (H&E, ×400). (C) Round‐to‐oval yeast cells within the cytoplasm of histiocytes (H&E, ×1000). (D) Distinctive transverse septum of T. marneffei yeast cells (PAS, ×1000).

PMID: 34849222

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Vascular
