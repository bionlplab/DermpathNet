A microRNA encoded by Kaposi sarcoma-associated herpesvirus promotes B-cell expansion in vivo.

Enhanced GC development in miRNA-expressing mice.A) H&E staining and immunohistochemical detection if PNA indicate increased GC formation in miRNA-expressing mice. White arrows in H&E stained sections indicate the GC. PNA staining was used to determine the GC number and relative GC area per section. Shown are representative sections from one mouse of each cohort. B) Statistical analysis of the average number of GCs (left) and the relative total GC area (right) in the mouse cohorts. Enumeration of GCs and determination of total GC area was performed as described in the material and methods section.
