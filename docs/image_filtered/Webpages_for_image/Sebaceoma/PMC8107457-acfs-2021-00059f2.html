Sebaceous carcinoma arising from sebaceoma.

Histologic findings. (A) Sebaceous carcinoma arising in sebaceoma. The tumor consists of multiple variably-sized, discrete nodules and is centered in the superficial dermis (H&E, ×1.25). (B) The sebaceous carcinoma displays an infiltrating growth pattern (arrow) (H&E, ×20). (C) Sebaceous carcinoma is characterized by strong epithelial membrane antigen positivity (immunohistochemistry stain, ×20). (D) Mutations and nuclear accumulation of p53 tumor suppressor gene expression are also noted (immunohistochemistry stain, ×20).

PMID: 33957741

Type: Benign, Category: Sebaceous
