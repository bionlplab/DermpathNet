Dermoscopy of accessory nipples in authors' own study.

Central white scar-like area. Dermoscopy reveals a centrally located, sharply circumscribed, oval, white area surrounded by a peripheral network (network-like structures). Small dots comparable to these of the fisheye-like structures are also visible in the central part of the lesion, resembling comedo-like openings
