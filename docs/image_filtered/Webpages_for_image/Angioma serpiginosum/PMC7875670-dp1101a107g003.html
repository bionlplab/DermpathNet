Clinicopathological and Dermoscopic Features in Blashkoid Angioma Serpiginosum.

(A) Photomicrograph showing multiple dilated vessels present in a clover-leaf pattern in the upper dermis (H&E, ×10). (B) Photomicrograph showing cluster of dilated thick-walled capillaries (due to deposition of PAS-positive material) with flattened endothelial cells and red blood cells present in the center of the vessels (H&E, ×40).
