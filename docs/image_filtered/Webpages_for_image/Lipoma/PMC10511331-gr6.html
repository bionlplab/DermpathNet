Lipoma-like hibernoma of the breast: A case report and literature review.

(A and B) H&E staining, 4x and 10x: The lesion is made up of polygonal brown fat cells, accompanied by stromal cells in the background. (C and D) H&E staining, 40x: Presence of fat and pale brown cells with eosinophilic characteristics, displaying multivacuolated granular cytoplasm and a small central nucleus.
