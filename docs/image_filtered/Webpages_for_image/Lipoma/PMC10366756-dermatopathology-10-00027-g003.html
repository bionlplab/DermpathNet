Folliculosebaceous Cystic Hamartoma with Spindle Cell Lipomatous and Neural Components.

(A). Fibromyxoid stroma positive for Alcian blue (Alcian-blue stain, original magnification ×10). (B). Fibromyxoid stroma and spindle cells positive for CD34 (CD34 stain, original magnification ×200). (C,D). S100+ small nerve bundles and adipocytes (S100 stain, original magnification ×400). (E). Vascular structures positive for SMA (SMA stain, original magnification ×100).

PMID: 37489451

Type: Benign, Category: Adipocytic
