Synchronous Thyrolipoma and Papillary Thyroid Carcinoma: A Rare but Significant Event.

The nodule I: Thyrolipoma. (A,B) Histopathologically, the nodule I was well encapsulated by fibrous walls and consisted of mature adipocytes and thyroid follicles with a bland-looking morphology. (H&E, A: ×40; B: ×400). (C) The thyroid follicular cells were positive for TTF-1 (nuclear positivity, ×200). (D) The BRAF staining was negative in thyroid follicular cells (×200).

PMID: 34441269

Type: Benign, Category: Adipocytic
