Nodular Fasciitis and Myxolipoma of the Larynx: A rare case report with brief literature review.

Haematoxylin and eosin stains at (A) ×20 magnification showing spindled and plump cells exhibiting fascicular arrangement in myxofibrotic background (lesional cells demonstrate storiform architecture); at (B) ×40 magnification showing benign-looking, uniform, stellate fibroblasts resembling a tissue culture-like appearance and; at (C) ×40 and (D) ×10 magnification showing mature-appearing adipocytes with prominent myxoid areas. No evidence of atypia, plexiform vascular network or lipoblasts.

PMID: 36407715

Type: Benign, Category: Adipocytic
