Intramuscular dendritic fibromyxolipoma in a 24-year-old male: A case report and review of the literature.

Histopathology of dendritic fibromyxolipoma (DFML). (A) Low-power view showing the tumor cells focally involving skeletal muscle [hematoxylin and eosin (H&E) stain; original magnification, ×40]. (B) The tumor cells and admixed mature adipose tissue (H&E stain; original magnification, ×40). (C) Ropey collagen bundles, which were similar in morphology to solitary fibrous tumor (H&E stain; original magnification, ×200); (D) Spindle cells with dendritic cytoplasmic processes (H&E stain; original magnification, ×400).

Type: Benign, Category: Adipocytic
