Osteochondrolipoma Presenting as a Popliteal Cyst.

Histological sections of the tumor specimen show lipoma with cartilaginous and osseous differentiation. (A) Ossification in the chondroid nodule. Lamellar bone with osteoblasts and osteoclasts is being formed in the chondroid nodule (H&E, ×40). (B) Fibroblast proliferation and fibrous band is intimately apposed to the fat necrosis area of lipoma (H&E, ×100). (C) A chondroid nodule in the lipoma. Chondroid matrix and chondrocytes are arising in the fibromyxoid band (H&E, ×40).

PMID: 26217476

Type: Benign, Category: Adipocytic
