Lipoma with osteocartilaginous metaplasia in infrapatellar fat pad: A case report and review of literature.

Gross and microscopic features of tumor. The tumor is an encapsulated lobulating mass (A), The tumor consists of mature adipocytes with focal osteoid and chondroid metaplasia (arrow) (H-E stain, ×40) (B), Hyaline cartilage with ossification and micro-calcification is shown within mature adipocytes. Cytologic atypia is not shown (H-E stain, ×100) (C).

PMID: 36281105

Type: Benign, Category: Adipocytic
