Asymptomatic Giant Lipoma of the Right Ventricular Outflow Tract: A Case Report.

Microscopic evaluation of the tumor in the right ventricular outflow tract. The lipoma (shown with multiple arrows) consists of mature fat cells, with only a slight variation in cellular size and shape (hematoxylin and eosin staining, 100 X).
