Giant mesenteric lipoma as an unusual cause of abdominal pain: a case report and a review of the literature.

Microscopic findings of the mesenteric lipoma. (A) The tumor revealed mature fat cells proliferating in the subserosal layer (H&E; original magnification, ×20). (B) Mature adipocytes are relatively uniform in size and lack cytologic atypia (H&E; original magnification, ×200).
