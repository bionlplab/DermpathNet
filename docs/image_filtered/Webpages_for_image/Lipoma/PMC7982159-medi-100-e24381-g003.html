Endobronchial lipoma with tracheobronchial aspergillosis: A case report.

Photomicrograph showing fat tissue surrounded by respiratory epithelium and the submucosa. (A) There are univacuolar mature fat cells, uniform in form and size (H&E, ×100). (B) High-power magnification revealed acutely branching and septate hyphae (H&E, ×100).
