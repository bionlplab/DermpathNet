Giant lipoma in the hand: A case report.

Histopathological images of the mass (A: H&E 40×, B: CDK4 200×, C: MDM2 200×). Histopathological staining confirmed a benign lipoma with no evidence of any malignant transformation. H&E = hematoxylin and eosin; CDK4 = cyclin-dependent kinase 4; MDM2 = mouse double minute 2 homolog.
