Intraneural lipoma of the ulnar nerve: A rare case report and review.

Biopsy specimen using hematoxylin and eosin (H&E) stain shows proliferation of mature adipocytes and a mixture of collagen fibers. Nerve fibers at the periphery, completely separated from the lipoma.

PMID: 37680653

Type: Benign, Category: Adipocytic
