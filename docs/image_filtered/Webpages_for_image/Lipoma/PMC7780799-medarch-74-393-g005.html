Pedunculated Lipoma of the Caecum Causing Colocolic Intussusception in an Adult.

Histopathological features of a pedunculated lipoma. Submucosa shows well circumscribed tumor composed of benign mature adipocytes separated by thin fibrovascular septae interspersed with chronic inflammatory cell infiltrate (Magnification 10X H&E staining)

PMID: 33424098

Type: Benign, Category: Adipocytic
