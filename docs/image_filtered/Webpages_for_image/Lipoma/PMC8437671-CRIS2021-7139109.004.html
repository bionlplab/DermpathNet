Astounding Fibro Lipoma of Spermatic Cord: A Diagnostic Dilemma.

H&E, 20x magnification. Lobules of mature adipocytes present within the stroma of spermatic cord along with highlighted pampiniform plexuses (black arrows) (a). Lobules of mature fat forming a mass within the cord tissue, separated by fibrous septae of varying thicknesses (b).

Type: Benign, Category: Adipocytic
