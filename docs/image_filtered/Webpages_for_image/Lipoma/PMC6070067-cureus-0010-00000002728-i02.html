Pericallosal Lipoma and Cortical Dysplasia Masquerading as a Glioma.

Focal cortical dysplasia, Palmini Type IA.A) The cortex, H&E 100x, shows mild architectural abnormalities and the white matter. B) H&E 100x shows associated gliosis. C) GFAP, 100x, immunohistochemical staining highlights the gliosis. D) NFP, 100x, does not highlight any dysmorphic neurons in areas of gliosis. These histologic features are diagnostic of focal cortical dysplasia, Palmini Type IA.H&E: Hematoxylin and eosin; GFAP: Glial fibrillary acidic protein; NFP: Neurofilament protein.

PMID: 30083489

Type: Benign, Category: Adipocytic
