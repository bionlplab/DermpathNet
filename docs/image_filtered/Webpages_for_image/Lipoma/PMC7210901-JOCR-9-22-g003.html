Synovial Lipomatosis with Chondroblastoma in an 8-year-old Female: A Previously Unreported Entity.-DermPath

Synovial Lipomatosis with Chondroblastoma in an 8-year-old Female: A Previously Unreported Entity.

(a) Microscopically, villous and nodular projection of synovium with replacement of stroma by adipose tissue (Hematoxylin and Eosin, ×40). (b) High power view shows hyperplasia of synovial lining with proliferation of adipocytes and chronic inflammation (×400). (c) Tumor showing lobules of cartilage with chondroblasts showing chicken wire calcification (Hematoxylin and Eosin, ×100). (d) High power shows nuclear molding with grooves.

PMID: 32405481

Type: Benign, Category: Adipocytic
