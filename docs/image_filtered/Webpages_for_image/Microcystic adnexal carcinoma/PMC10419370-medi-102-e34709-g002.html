Microcystic adnexal carcinoma of the eyelid and orbit: A case report and review of literature.

Histopathologic finding of MAC invading the deep tissues. (A) Tumor cells invading the subcutaneous adipose tissue. (B) Tumor cells invading the skeletal muscle. (C) The horn cysts containing dense, laminated keratin. (D) The ductal structures lined by one or two layers of tumor cells, and containing eosinophilic materials. (E) The ductal structures, nests and strands of tumor cells embedded in a fibrosclerotic stroma (Hematoxylin-eosin, original magnifications: A × 100, B × 100; C × 100, D × 100, E × 200). MAC = microcystic adnexal carcinoma.

PMID: 37565854

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Adnexal
