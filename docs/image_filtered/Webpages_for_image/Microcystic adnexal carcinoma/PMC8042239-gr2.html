Microcystic adnexal carcinoma of the glabella in a liver transplant recipient.

The original biopsy showed subtle islands of atypical squamous epithelium infiltrating the dermis, as indicated by the arrows, without obvious ductal differentiation; inset shows epithelium marked by arrow on the right. Ductal differentiation and extensive perineural involvement were noted in the re-excision. (Original magnifications: A, ×4; inset, ×20; B, ×10.)

PMID: 33869702

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Adnexal
