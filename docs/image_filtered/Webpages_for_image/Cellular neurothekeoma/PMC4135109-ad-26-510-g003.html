Myxoid Cellular Neurothekeoma: A New Entity of S100-Negative, CD68-Positive Myxoid Neurothekeoma.

Immunohistochemical findings. (A) The amount of myxoid stroma was >50% (alcian blue, ×40). (B, C) The tumor cells were positive for CD68 and vimentin (B: CD68, ×100; C: vimentin, ×100). The tumor cells were negative for S100 protein (D, ×100), CD57 (E, ×100), and glial fibrillary acidic protein (F, ×100).

PMID: 25143683

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
