Atypical cellular neurothekeoma: Report of a case.

Pathology specimens showing a non-capsulated neoplastic proliferation with medium-large size cells with both nodular and plexiform growth patterns and clear edges, mainly located in the dermis (A: H&E 40x; B: H&E 100x). Nuclear pleomorphism and atypical mitotic figures (red arrow) were also present (C: H&E 200x). Immunoreactivity for NKI-C3 (panel D).

PMID: 35795835

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
