Brain and lung metastasis of Bartholin's gland adenoid cystic carcinoma: a case report.

Histology of the brain metastasis biopsy specimen. (A) Hematoxylin and eosine stain with magnification x 200: adenoid cystic carcinoma cells (purple) adjacent to normal brain tissue (pink) at the upper left. (B) Hematoxylin and eosine stain with magnification x 400: adenoid cystic carcinoma cells (purple) arranged in a typical cribriform pattern, forming columns around gland-like spaces. (C) Immunohistochemistry with anti-cytokeratin 7 antibodies showing a typical positive stain for adenoid cystic carcinoma cells.

PMID: 23945057
