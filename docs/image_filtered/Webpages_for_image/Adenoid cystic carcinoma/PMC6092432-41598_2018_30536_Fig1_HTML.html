Evidence Based Tailored Parotidectomy in Treating External Auditory Canal Carcinoma.

Parotid involvement in adenoid cystic carcinoma. (A) Magnetic resonance image demonstrating the findings of external auditory canal (EAC) carcinomas (white arrows). The tumor is confined in the EAC without parotid involvement. (B) Histopathologic specimen from the same patients. The tumor is directly extending through the cartilage and an invading lesion in the parotid gland (black arrow head) is shown (magnification, x100).
