Adenoid cystic carcinoma of the trachea mimicking asthma.

(A) H&E staining: malignant cells arranged in cribriform pattern with microcystic spaces containing hyaline/basophilic mucoid material. (B) CK7 positivity within ductal/luminal cells and negative staining within myoepithelial/abluminal cells. (C) CD117 positivity within ductal/luminal cells and negative staining within myoepithelial cells

PMID: 37090913

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Adnexal
