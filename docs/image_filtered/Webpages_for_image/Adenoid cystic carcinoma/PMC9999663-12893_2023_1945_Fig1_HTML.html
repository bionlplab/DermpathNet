Value of cytopathology in the diagnosis of adenoid cystic carcinoma and an analysis of misdiagnoses.

Cytomorphologic characteristics of adenoid cystic carcinoma (ACC). (A and B) Cribriform vesicle structures of different sizes (H&E stain, ×100). (C, D) Translucent spherical body surrounded by tumor cells (H&E stain, ×100). (E) Moderately atypical basal-like cells arranged in a three-dimensional pattern (H&E stain, ×100). (F) Cells arranged in stripes, like the layers of waves hitting a beach (H&E stain, ×100)
