Adenoid cystic carcinoma of buccal mucosa: A report of two rare cases and review of literature.

A, Microscopic examinations show tubular pattern of small basaloid tumoral cells (H&E staining, ×100 magnification). B, Tumoral cells demonstrate admixture of cribriform and tubular pattern (H&E staining, ×100 magnification). C, Diffuse positive C‐kit immunoreaction in tumoral cells (IHC, ×400 magnification). D, Positive immunoreaction for ki67 in about 10% of tumoral cells (IHC, ×400 magnification)

PMID: 33489129

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Adnexal
