(68)Ga-PSMA-HBED-CC PET/CT imaging for adenoid cystic carcinoma and salivary duct carcinoma: a phase 2 imaging study.

Immunohistochemical PSMA expression in primary tumors of ACC patients no. 4 (A), no. 10 (B) and SDC patients no. 17 (C) and no. 21 (D). PSMA expression in tumor cells is 10% in (A), 90% in (B), negative in (C), and 50% in (D). PSMA expression in tumor associated neovasculature is negative in the ACC patients (A and B), and positive in most SDC patients, as shown in (C and D). Magnification 200x.

PMID: 32089741
