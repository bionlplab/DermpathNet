Adenoid Cystic Carcinoma of the Breast: A Case Report.

Macro- and micro-scopic examinations of the resected specimen: The excised specimen was a 15.0 × 12.1 × 9.7 mm mass with a greyish white cut surface (a). Pathological examination of the excised specimen by H&E staining revealed a so-called adenoid cystic pattern, with various large and small cribriform mucinous tumor nests around the tumor stroma, and both true and false lumen within the tumor nests (×200) (b).

PMID: 31607886

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Adnexal
