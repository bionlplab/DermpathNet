Immediate Umbilical Reconstruction after a Mohs Micrographic Surgery for Primary Cutaneous Adenoid Cystic Carcinoma Arising in the Umbilicus.

Histopathologic features of the excised tumor. (A) Basaloid tumor cells forming nests and cords in the dermis and subcutaneous tissue without connection to the epidermis (H&E, original magnification ×100). (B) Cribriform or tubular structure of the basaloid cells with abundant mucin inside (H&E, ×400). (C) Tumor cells had hyperchromatic nuclei but did not demonstrate distinct cellular atypia (H&E, ×400).

PMID: 33911668

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Adnexal
