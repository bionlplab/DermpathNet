Strain elastography of palatal tumors in conjunction with intraoral ultrasonography, computed tomography, and magnetic resonance imaging: 2 case reports.

Specimen and histopathological exam of an adenoid cystic carcinoma of the palate in a 70-year-old woman. In the coronal segment (A) of the right maxillary first molar (real view) and the low-magnification view (B) of the surgical specimen (H&E stain ×1; scale bar, 5 mm), the tumor showed aggressive bone destruction and extension into the maxillary sinus (*) and nasal cavity (★). C. Cribriform and tubular patterns were observed (H&E stain ×40; scale bar, 50 µm).

PMID: 32206623

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Adnexal
