Sleeve pneumonectomy and carinal resection for management of primary adenoid cystic tumor of the lung with carinal extension: Report of two cases.

A, B. Hematoxylin and eosin staining (H & E), adenoid cystic carcinoma with cribiform pattern, positive immunohistochemical study (IHC) for the expression of p63 and p40. C. Benign epithelial inclusions within the lymph node, negative for malignancy. D. Expression of cytokeratin CK7 in the lymph node, confirming a benign epithelial inclusion.

PMID: 30555782

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Adnexal
