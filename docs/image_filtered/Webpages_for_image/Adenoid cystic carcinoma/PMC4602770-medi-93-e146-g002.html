Carcinoma ex pleomorphic adenoma of the palate composed of invasive micropapillary salivary duct carcinoma and adenoid cystic carcinoma components: an unusual case with immunohistochemical approach.

(A) Immunohistochemically, ductal cells of the remaining pleomorphic adenoma expressed CK7 (×100). (B) The invasive micropapillary salivary duct carcinoma cells strongly stained with CK7 and HER-2 in a membrane pattern (C), both in intratubular and invasive areas (×100). (D) Modified myoepithelial cells of the adenoid cystic carcinoma component expressed α-SMA, nuclear p63 (E), and CK7 (F) highlighted cells with luminal differentiation (×40). (G) p53 expression in IMSDC (right) and in ACC (left) (×100). (H) Ki-67 expression in malignant neoplastic cells (×100). ACC = adenoid cystic carcinoma, HER-2 = human epidermal growth factor receptor-2, IMSDC = invasive micropapillary salivary duct carcinoma, α-SMA = α-smooth mucle actin.
