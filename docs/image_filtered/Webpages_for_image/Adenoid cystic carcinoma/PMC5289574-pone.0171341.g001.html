Cytokeratin-14 contributes to collective invasion of salivary adenoid cystic carcinoma.

SACC collective cells invaded into normal tissues under light microscopy with H&E staining.(A) Arrow showed invasion into fat tissue (HE×200; ×400). (B) Arrow showed invasion into gland (HE×200; ×400). (C) Arrow showed invasion into blood vessel (HE×200; ×400). (D) Arrow showed invasion into nerve tissue (HE×200; ×400). The invasive cells were aggregated or clustered, and not single cells.
