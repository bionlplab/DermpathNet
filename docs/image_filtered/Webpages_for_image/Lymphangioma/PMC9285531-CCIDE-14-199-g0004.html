Lymphangioma Circumscriptum - A Rare Cause of Gingival Enlargement: A Case Report and Review of Literature.-DermPath

Lymphangioma Circumscriptum - A Rare Cause of Gingival Enlargement: A Case Report and Review of Literature.

(A) H&E staining X10 magnification and (B) H&E staining X40 magnification. Histopathological examination showed parakeratinized stratified squamous epithelium (Red arrow). The juxtaepithelial connective tissue showed homogeneous eosinophilic areas resembling lymph lined by endothelium (Yellow arrow) with interspersed lymphocytes, occasional plasma cells, tissue macrophages, and red blood cells (RBC). The connective tissue was densely collagenous with interlacing bundles of collagen fibres, fibroblasts, and blood vessels (Black arrow).

PMID: 35845977

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
