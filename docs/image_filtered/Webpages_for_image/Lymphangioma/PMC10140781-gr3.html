Adult upper-limb forearm lymphoma hygroma (cystic lymphangioma): A case report.

H&E (a ×100, b ×200). The endothelial-lined cyst wall was thick and surrounded by abundant smooth muscle fibers (a black arrow) and scattered lymphocytes. The dilated cystic space surrounded by various blood cells contained proliferative flat endothelial cells and fibrous connective tissue, irregular adipocytes or fatty small cysts, and enriched mature lymphocytes (b black arrow).

PMID: 37080154

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
