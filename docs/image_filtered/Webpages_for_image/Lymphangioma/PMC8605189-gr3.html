Case report: Omental lymphangioma mimicking gastric cystic tumor.

Histology of cystic lymphangioma. Cystic space with thin wall, endothelial lining, and proteinaceous content (A: H&E 20x; B: H&E 400x). Immunohistochemical staining disclosed positive for CD34 (C) and negative for calretinin (D).

PMID: 34824652

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
