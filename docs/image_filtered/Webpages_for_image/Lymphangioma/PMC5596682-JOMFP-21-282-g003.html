Hemangiolymphangioma of buccal mucosa: A rare case report.

(a and b) Photomicrograph (H & E, stain ×10) reveals stratified squamous epithelium with juxta epithelial stroma showing numerous vessels spaces which are filled with either blood cells or lymphatic fluids. (c) Photomicrograph (H & E, stain ×10) showing dilated vessels filled with RBC's along with few cross sections of muscle bundles. (d) Photomicrograph (H & E, stain ×10) showing numerous vessels spaces which are filled with lymphatic fluids

PMID: 28932041

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
