Acquired omental cystic lymphangioma after subtotal gastrectomy: a case report.

Histology of lymphangioma. (A, B) Cystic wall was consisted of fibroconnective tissue accompanied by dilated lymphatic spaces and lymphoid cell aggregations in the endothelial lining of lymphatic vessels (H&E ×100, ×400). Immunohistochemical staining. (C) Positive for CD31 in the lining cells of dilated lymphatic vessels (×400). (D) Positive for SMA in the cystic wall, and they were smooth muscle bundles surrounding lymphatic vascular endothelium (×400).

PMID: 19949686

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
