Retrobulbar Lymphangioma: A Rare Case Report.

(a) Photomicrograph shows multiple large lymphatic channels, focally lined by flattened epithelium with small scattered mature lymphoid aggregates in the wall, and filled with pink, amorphous, homogeneous proteinaceous fluid, and lymphocytes (H and E, ×20).
