Case report of solitary giant hepatic lymphangioma.

Microscopic photographs. Histologically, the cyst wall is lined with single-layered flat endothelial cells (H&E, ×200, A). Immunohistochemical findings revealed these endothelial-lined cells to be positively immunostained by CD31 (×400, B).

PMID: 27212994

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
