Lymphangioma of Colon Presenting as an Intramural Tumor.

Photomicrograph of histopathologic specimen (HE stain). (A) (x20) showing the submucosal cystic lymphangioma with the interior wall lined by flattened endothelium (arrows). (B) (x40) showing a wall that has multiple dilated spaces (asterisks), lined by a thin layer of endothelial cells (short arrow); between these lymphatic spaces are smooth muscles and foci of lymphocyte clusters (long arrow).

PMID: 35845223

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
