Radical local excision of the mons for treatment of recalcitrant lymphangioma circumscriptum.

(a) Verruciform-like epidermal hyperplastic reaction; H&E 4×. (b). Ectatic benign lymphatic channels situated in the superficial dermis; H&E 20×. (c). A deep abscess containing follicular debris, typical of hidradenitis suppurativa; H&E 20×.

PMID: 37744207

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
