Rapidly growing pleural liposarcoma masquerading as extrapleural hematoma.

Microscopic features of the pleural liposarcoma. (a) Low power view of the resected specimen showing plump spindle tumor cells with eosinophilic cytoplasm (hematoxylin and eosin [H&E] staining ×40). (b) High power view showing atypical enlarged hyperchromatic nuclei in collagenous background (H&E staining ×400). (c) Immunohistochemical analysis revealing CDK4 (×200) and (d) P16 (×200) expression.

PMID: 30511801

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Adipocytic
