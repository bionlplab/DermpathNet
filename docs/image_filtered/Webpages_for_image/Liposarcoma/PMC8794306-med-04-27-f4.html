Liposarcomas of the mediastinum.

Low- and high-grade dedifferentiated liposarcoma. (A) Low-grade DDLS shows a solid proliferation of bland spindle cells embedded in collagenous stroma (top) adjacent to area of well-differentiated lipomatous tumor (4× magnification, H&E); (B) higher magnification of low-grade DDLS shows mildly atypical non-lipogenic spindle cell proliferation (20× magnification, H&E); (C) low-grade DDLS shows distinctive whorling pattern of tumor cells resembling meningioma (20× magnification, H&E); (D) high-grade DDLS shows transitions between atypical lipomatous tumor and high-grade sarcoma (4× magnification, H&E); (E) MDM2 immunohistochemical stain in spindle cell component of low-grade DDLS shows scattered nuclear positivity for MDM2 in the tumor cells (20× magnification, MDM2 immunohistochemistry).

PMID: 35118295

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Adipocytic
