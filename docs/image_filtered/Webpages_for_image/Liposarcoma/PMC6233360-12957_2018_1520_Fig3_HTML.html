Dedifferentiated liposarcoma of the gallbladder: first reported case.

Dedifferentiated liposarcoma of the gallbladder stained with hematoxylin and eosin (H&E) (a). Foci of heterologous leiomyosarcomatous differentiation with immunohistochemical (IHC) staining for S100 Protein (b) and undifferentiated fusocellular areas of high histological grade with IHC staining for desmin (c)

PMID: 30419915

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Adipocytic
