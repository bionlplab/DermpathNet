Primary colonic liposarcomatosis: report of a case with review of literature.

Photomicrographs of the nodular mass. A and B – Thick septa separating the lobules of adipocytes. Note the septa with spindle-shaped singly scattered atypical stromal cells with hyperchromatic nuclei (H&E, 40X and 400X, respectively). C – Multivacuolated lipoblast (H&E, 400X). D – MDM2 immunohistochemistry showing strong nuclear positivity in the stromal cells as well as in the adipocytes (100X).

PMID: 30863730
