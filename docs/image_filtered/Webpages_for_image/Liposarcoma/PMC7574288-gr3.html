A case report of uncommon paratesticular liposarcoma of spermatic cord presenting as a scrotal mass.

Dedifferentiated liposarcoma: Histologically, the tumour is composed of fascicles of mildly to focally moderately atypical cells with spindle to ovoid nuclei and scanty mitoses with occasional interspersed mature adipocytes on 100X magnification.

PMID: 33102113

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Adipocytic
