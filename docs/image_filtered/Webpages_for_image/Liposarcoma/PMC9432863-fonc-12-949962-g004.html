Primary intrathoracic liposarcomas: A clinicopathologic and molecular study of 43 cases in one of the largest medical centers of China.

The histologic features of DDL with leiomyosarcomatous differentiation (case 33) and its corresponding immunohistochemical and fluorescence in situ hybridization (FISH) image. Sclerotic well-differentiated area outside the lung (A H&E; magnification: 200×). At low magnification, the growth of the spindle tumor cells showed cleft-like architecture, mimicking the pattern of pulmonary adenofibroma (B H&E; magnification: 200×). Spindle tumor cells display a fascicular arrangement, with hyperchromatic, cigar-shaped nuclei and mild to moderate atypia (C H&E; magnification: 200×). The tumor cells exhibited increased nuclear atypia with obvious pleomorphism (D H&E; magnification: 200×). The tumor cells showed SMA (E magnification: 200×), desmin (F magnification: 200×) and h-caldesmon (G magnification: 200×) positivity in well-differentiated areas and negativity in focal sarcoma-like areas. MDM2 amplification was identified in this case (H).
