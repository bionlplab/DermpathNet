Giant gluteal lipoma-like liposarcoma: a case report.

Photomicrograph of excised lesion showing a well differentiated lipomatous tumor growth which exhibits at low magnification adipocyte with significant variation in size and shape (A) as well as occasional atypical adipocyte with enlarged hyperchromatic nuclei seen with high magnification (arrow)(B & C) (H&E stained, 5 u thick paraffin sections).
