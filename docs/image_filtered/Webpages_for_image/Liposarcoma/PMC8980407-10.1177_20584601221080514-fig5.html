Inverted intercostal hernia of elastofibroma dorsi mimicking well-differentiated liposarcoma in the chest wall.

Pathological examination of the biopsy specimen. (a). Hematoxylin and eosin stain (HE, × 10). (b). Elastica van Gieson’s stain (EVG, × 10). The pathological examination of the biopsy specimen revealed increased collagen fibers, spindle-shaped mesenchymal cells, and adipose cells on HE staining (Figure 5(a), HE × 10). Multiple elastic fibers were dyed black with EVG staining (Figure 5(b), EVG stain × 10).

PMID: 35392629

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Adipocytic
