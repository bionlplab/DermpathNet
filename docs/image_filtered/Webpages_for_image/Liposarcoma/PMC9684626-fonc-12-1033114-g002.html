Case report: Atypical spindle cell/pleomorphic lipomatous tumor masquerading as a myxoid liposarcoma or intramuscular myxoma.

Histological findings of the needle biopsy specimen. Pathological examination showed mild proliferation of spindle cells with few atypical cells in a myxoid background (A, ×100 B, ×200).

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Adipocytic
