Liposarcomas of the mediastinum.

Prominent myxoid stroma in well-differentiated liposarcoma/atypical lipomatous tumor. (A) WDL/ALT with prominent myxoid stroma shows small islands of lipoma-like WDL admixed with areas containing abundant myxoid stroma (4× magnification, H&E); (B) higher magnification of WDL/ALT with myxoid stroma highlights the close resemblance of this tumor with myxoid liposarcoma, including a branching pattern of vessels (10× magnification, H&E); (C) immunohistochemical stain for MDM2 in WDL/ALT with myxoid stroma shows scattered nuclear positivity in the tumor cells, a pattern of staining that should not be seen in myxoid liposarcoma (10× magnification, MDM2 immunohistochemistry).

PMID: 35118295

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Adipocytic
