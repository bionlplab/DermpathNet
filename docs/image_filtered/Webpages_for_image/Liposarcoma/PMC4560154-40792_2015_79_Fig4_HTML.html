Successful resection of a huge metastatic liposarcoma in the pericardium resulting in improvement of diastolic heart failure: a case report.

Histological findings of the resected tumors. Histological findings showing that almost all of the tumors were myxoid liposarcomas (a, hematoxylin-eosin, ×100) and a part of the tumor adjacent to the right ventricle was round-cell liposarcoma (b, hematoxylin-eosin, ×200)
