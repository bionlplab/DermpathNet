Liposarcomas of the mediastinum.

Lipoleiomyosarcoma. (A) Well-differentiated smooth muscle component is present in an otherwise typical WDL/ALT (so-called “lipoleiomyosarcoma”) (20× magnification, H&E); (B) positive staining for SMA is seen in this example of lipoleiomyosarcoma [20× magnification, Smooth muscle actin (SMA) immunohistochemistry]; (C) the spindle tumor cells in the smooth muscle component of lipoleiomyosarcoma also show nuclear positivity for MDM2 (40× magnification, MDM2 immunohistochemistry).

PMID: 35118295

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Adipocytic
