Positron emission tomography/computed tomography manifestations of primary hepatic myxoid liposarcoma: A case report.

Photomicrograph shows a large area of loose myxedematous matrix (A) and focal short shuttle cells with heterogeneous nuclei (B). Immunohistochemistry (IHC) demonstrates S-100 as positive (C) and Ki-67 as 20% (D) (×100 A, B; ×200 C, D).
