Rare Case of Primary Pulmonary Pleomorphic Liposarcoma Treated With Multimodal Therapy.

(A) Pleomorphic liposarcoma tumor deposit shows admixture of pleomorphic lipoblasts with epithelioid cells (hematoxylin and eosin, ×100). (B) Pleomorphic liposarcoma tumor deposit shows multivacuolated lipoblasts with indented pleomorphic nuclei (hematoxylin and eosin, ×200). (C) Pleomorphic liposarcoma cells are positive for S-100 (immunohistochemistry, ×200). (D) Pleomorphic liposarcoma cells show diffuse p53 expression (immunohistochemistry, ×200).

PMID: 34984062

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Adipocytic
