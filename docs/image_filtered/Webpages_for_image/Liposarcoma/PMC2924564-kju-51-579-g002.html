Retroperitoneal giant liposarcoma.

(A) Computed tomography (CT) image showing a large fatty mass with an enhancing solid portion in the left renal subcapsular area. The kidney shows sprayed. (B) The gross specimen consisting of the left kidney and attached soft tissue, weighing 2,120 g. On bisection, a well-encapsulated, multilobulated, yellowish creamy white solid mass was noted, measuring 18×16×13 cm. Focal necrotic areas are seen, making up 10% of the tumor volume. (C) Microscopic findings showing a well-differentiated liposarcoma and mature-appearing adipose tissue with scattered lipoblasts (H&E, ×200).

PMID: 20733966

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Adipocytic
