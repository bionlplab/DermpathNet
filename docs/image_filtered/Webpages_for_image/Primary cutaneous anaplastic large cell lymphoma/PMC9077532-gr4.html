Master of mimicry: Rare primary cutaneous anaplastic large cell lymphoma presenting as fungating parotid tumor-case report and review.

Biopsy of the tumor shows intradermal tumor infiltration with absence of epidermatropism (H&E, 40×). The inset shows the hallmark cells (yellow arrow) scattered and other anaplastic large cells with the background of heavy infiltration of acute inflammatory cells (majority are neutrophils) (H&E, 400×).
