Master of mimicry: Rare primary cutaneous anaplastic large cell lymphoma presenting as fungating parotid tumor-case report and review.

The immunohistochemistry patterns of the fungating tumor. A: CD3 and B: CD20, only positive in the background reactive T and B cells respectively (×40);C: CD30, strong and homogenous in all of the tumor cells (×40); D: CD5, and E: CD4, T cell markers that are positive in the neoplastic cells (perforin was positive in some of these cells, not shown) (×100); F:CD8 background cytotoxic T cells (×40); G: ALK totally negative (×40); H:CD25 (×100); I:EMA, strong and highlighted the Golgi body (×100).
