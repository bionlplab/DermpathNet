Endocrine mucin-producing sweat gland carcinoma of the scalp treated with Mohs micrographic surgery.

A and B, Punch biopsy scalp lesion shows monomorphic epithelial cells surrounded by abundant mucin and separated by fibrous septa. C, Immunoreactivity for mucicarmine. D, Extensive immunoreactivity for synaptophysin, favoring EMPSGC of the scalp. E and F, Mohs stage I with proliferation of monomorphic small round cells surrounded by mucin.

PMID: 30306119

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Adnexal
