Recurrence after exenteration for canine orbital malignant schwannoma.

Tumor cells were arranged in an elongated shape or a polyhedral pattern (A) with high mitotic figures (arrows) (B), and demonstrated positive reactions for neuron-specific enolase (C). H&E stain (A and B) and immunohistochemistry (C). Scale bars = 50 µm (A), 20 µm (B and C).
