Neurilemmoma in the floor of the mouth: a case report.

Microscopic examination of the mass. A. Antoni B pattern with less cellularity, left. Antoni A pattern with well-organized, high cellularity, right (H&E staining, ×40). B. Streaming fascicles of spindle-shaped Schwann cells surround a central acellular, eosinophilic region of Verocay bodies (H&E staining, ×100). C. Magnification of the Antoni A area shows no evidence of cellular dysplasia in spite of high cellularity (H&E staining, ×100). D. Immunohistochemistry shows reactivity to S-100 protein (×100).

PMID: 26904498

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
