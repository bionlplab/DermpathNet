Charcot-Marie-Tooth 1A concurrent with schwannomas of the spinal cord and median nerve.

Histopathological feature of the intradural extramedullary mass at the T12-L1 spinal level is a tumor containing crowded spindle cells with palisading nuclei (Antoni A pattern, arrow) and loosely arranged spindle cells in abundant myxomatous areas (Antoni B pattern, arrowhead).
