Ancient Schwannoma of the hard palate. An uncommon case report and review.

Histopathological characteristics of the tumor. Spindle cell hypercelular areas (Antoni A) and hypocelular and myxoid areas (Antoni B). Degenerative changes can be observed. Hematoxylin and eosin stain 20x.

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
