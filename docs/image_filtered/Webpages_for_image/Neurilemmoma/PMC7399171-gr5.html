Laparoscopic resection of retroperitoneal intra-psoas muscle schwannoma: A case report and extensive literature review.

(5a) A photomicrograph showing the biphasic nature of the lesion (hypercellular Antoni A areas and hypocellular Antoni B areas) (H&E stain, x100 magnification). (5b) Areas of hyalinization and hemorrhage were present (H&E stain, x100 magnification). (5c) The cells showed elongated wavy nuclei with several Verocay bodies (H&E stain, x100 magnification). (5d) Mild nuclear atypia was present along with hyalinization of several blood vessels (H&E stain, x200 magnification).
