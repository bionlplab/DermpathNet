Eccrine Hidrocystoma of the Central Chest and Dermoscopic Findings.

Histopathology revealed a dermal-located cyst lined by two layers of cuboidal epithelium. The cyst is multiloculated and is arising in continuity with an eccrine duct (arrow). Hematoxylin-eosin stain; original magnification: 100x. 

PMID: 32528753

Type: Benign, Category: Cystic
