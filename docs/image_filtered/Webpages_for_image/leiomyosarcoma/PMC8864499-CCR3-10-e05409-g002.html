Primary leiomyosarcoma of the middle ureter: A rare case report with literature review.

(A). Immunohistochemical staining and microscopic study showing periureteric mass positive for smooth muscle actin (40 X, IHC). (B) was positive for vimentin confirmed the diagnosis of Leiomyosarcoma. (C) Interlacing bundles of pleomorphic spindle cells with storiform pattern in periureteric mass (10x, H & E) (1). (D) Periureteric mass showing elongated, pleomorphic spindle cells (40 x, H & E)

PMID: 35228875

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Smooth muscle
