Primary intracranial leiomyosarcoma in an immunocompetent patient: Case report with emphasis on imaging features.

The histology, H&E staining of the tumor showed characteristic spindle cell and moderate unclear polymorphism (A). The tumor cells expressed smooth muscle actin (SMA) (B) (original magnification, ×100). SMA = smooth muscle actin.
