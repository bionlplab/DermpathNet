Prognostic relevance of the hexosamine biosynthesis pathway activation in leiomyosarcoma.

Histologic appearance of three high grade LMS cases with different patterns of cytoplasmic expression of GFPT2 and nuclear expression of c-Myc.The first row shows representative H&E and immunohistochemical staining in LMS that lacks GFPT2 and c-Myc expression. The second row shows LMS with weak cytoplasmic expression of GFPT2 and a concordant weak nuclear expression of c-Myc in ~5% of cells. The third row demonstrates LMS with strong cytoplasmic expression of GFPT2 and a concordant strong nuclear expression of c-Myc in ~30% of cells. Total magnification Ã—600.
