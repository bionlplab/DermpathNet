Peritoneal dissemination complicating morcellation of uterine mesenchymal neoplasms.

A case of leiomyosarcoma (case #17) diagnosed following a uterine power morcellation, with subsequent diagnosis of dissemination throughout the peritoneum (all images 100x magnification).The primary and disseminated lesions are characterized by very high mitotic rates (all lesions greater than 50 mitoses per 10 high power fields) and significant nuclear atypia and pleomorphism; focal necrosis was also appreciated (not shown).

PMID: 23189178

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Smooth muscle
