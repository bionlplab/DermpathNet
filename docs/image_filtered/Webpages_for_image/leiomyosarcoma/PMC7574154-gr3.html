Leiomyosarcoma of the renal pelvis diagnosed by percutaneous endoscopic resection.

Histopathological examination revealed spindle-shaped tumor cells arranged in fascicles with moderate nuclear pleomorphism, nuclear hyperchromasia, and a mitotic figure; H&E, original magnification, x400 (3A). Immunohistochemical staining with peroxidase yielded strong and diffuse positivity for SMA; original magnification, x100 (3B).

PMID: 33102102

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Smooth muscle
