Primary leiomyosarcoma of the jugular vein in a dog.

The neoplasm was composed of spindle cells arranged in interlacing bundles and in a herringbone pattern. The cells have indistinct cell borders, a moderate amount of lightly eosinophilic cytoplasm, and oval or cigar-shaped nuclei. Several mitoses are evident (arrows) (H&E stain; bar = 100 Âµm).
