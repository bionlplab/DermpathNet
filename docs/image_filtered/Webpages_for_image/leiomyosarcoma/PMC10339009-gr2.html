Primary leiomyosarcoma of the pancreas with metastasis to the spleen.

Rapid on-site evaluation cytology of the mass in the pancreas (A) and the spleen (C) showing neoplastic spindle cells. Histology of both sites (B and D, respectively) revealed smooth-muscle pattern ([A andC] stain: diff quick. orig. mag. ×20; [B andD] stain: H&E. orig. mag. × 10).

PMID: 37456217

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Smooth muscle
