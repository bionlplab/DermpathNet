Lung metastases from sinonasal leiomyosarcoma masked by organizing pneumonia.

Microscopic section of lung tissue at (a) low power (hematoxylin and eosin [H&E], original magnification x40), and (b) intermediate power (H&E, original magnification x100) shows airspace filling by fibroblastic plugs (arrows), corresponding to foci of organizing pneumonia, adjacent to a cluster of atypical spindle cells (arrow head). (c) At high power (H&E, original magnification x400), the spindle cells are observed to be pleomorphic with an irregular nuclear membrane, vesicular nuclei, and prominent nucleoli, a morphology consistent with leiomyosarcoma. These malignant cells are immunohistochemically positive for desmin, confirming their smooth muscle origin (d)

PMID: 34453493

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Smooth muscle
