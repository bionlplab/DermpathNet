Skull Metastasis From Uterine Leiomyosarcoma, a Rare Presentation for a Rare Tumor: A Case Report and Review of the Literature.

Histological specimen: marked cellular pleomorphism, nuclear atypia, mitotic figures. Hematoxylin and eosin (H&E stain), X40 (A); Immunohistochemical findings: strong cell positivity for alfa-smooth muscle actin (a-SMA), X40 (B) and strong cell positivity for vimentin, X20 (C).

PMID: 32612948

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Smooth muscle
