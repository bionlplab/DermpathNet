Metastatic Vascular Pleomorphic Leiomyosarcoma in a Previously Treated Cervical Carcinoma Patient: A Diagnostic Dilemma.

Microscopic examination shows spindle neoplastic cells exhibiting moderate to marked pleomorphism, round to plump, blunt-ended nuclei, vesicular to hyperchromatic nuclei, some with prominent nucleoli and abundant eosinophilic cytoplasm. Bizarre cells with multinucleation (white arrow) and many mitoses (white asterisks) seen (H&E, 100x). The neoplastic cells are positive for SMA (inset).

PMID: 34367789

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Smooth muscle
