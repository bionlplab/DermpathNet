Successful robotic surgery for primary resection of a vaginal leiomyosarcoma: A case report.

(A) Tumor shows fascicular growth pattern and is composed of spindle cells with eosinophilic fibrillary cytoplasm and cigar shaped nuclei with moderate to severe atypia. Black arrow pointing to area of coagulative necrosis. (H&E, 10×). (B) The tumor contains frequent mitoses with black arrow showing atypical mitotic figure. (H&E, 40×).
