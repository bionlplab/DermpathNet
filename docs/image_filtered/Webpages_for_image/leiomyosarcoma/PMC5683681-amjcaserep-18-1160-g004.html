Leiomyosarcoma of the Inferior Vena Cava in an HIV-Positive Adult Patient: A Case Report and Review of the Literature.

Photomicrograph of the histology of the needle core biopsy of the leiomyosarcoma. Photomicrograph of the needle core biopsy shows a hypercellular spindle cell neoplasm, with cells resembling smooth muscle cells, but with moderate to severe cell pleomorphism, Hematoxylin and eosin (H&E) (Magnification ×400). Immunohistochemistry shows strong positive staining for vimentin, desmin, and smooth-muscle α-actin (SMA). A diagnosis of a moderately differentiated leiomyosarcoma was made.

PMID: 29097650

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Smooth muscle
