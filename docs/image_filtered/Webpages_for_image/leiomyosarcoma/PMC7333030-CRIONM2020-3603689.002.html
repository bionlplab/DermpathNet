Primary Leiomyosarcoma of the Gallbladder.

Pathohistochemical profile. (a) Ligand: normal gallbladder mucosa. (b, c) H&E stain shows proliferation of spindle cells arranged in interlacing bundles, pronounced nuclear atypia, pleomorphism, and many atypical mitotic figures (×200 and ×400, respectively). (d) SMA stain: the neoplastic cells show a strong cytoplasmic staining reaction (×200).

PMID: 32670649

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Smooth muscle
