Left distal ureter leiomyosarcoma: a case report.

(A–D) H&E resection ×12.5, ×40, ×100 and ×200—ureteric lumen (left of picture) lined by stratified, transitional epithelium, with high grade leiomyosarcoma extending from the wall. Note the spindled appearance of the lesional cells, with marked nuclear atypia and mitotic activity. In the lowest magnification (×12.5), the area of necrosis is the ill-defined pink region in the top left hand corner.

PMID: 36381984

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Smooth muscle
