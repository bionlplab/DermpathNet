Leiomyosarcoma arising from the blind end of a bifid renal pelvis.

Microphotography shows mitosis (a cell with dark nucleoli in the center) and spindle shaped cells with large nucleus in H-E staining (A)(× 400). Positive staining of the tumor cells for smooth muscle-specific actin reveals that the tumor is originated from smooth muscle tissue in actin staining (B)(× 100) and desmin staining (C)(× 200).

PMID: 17594170

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Smooth muscle
