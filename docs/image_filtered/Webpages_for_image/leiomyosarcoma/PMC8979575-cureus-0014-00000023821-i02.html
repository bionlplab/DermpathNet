Primary Pulmonary Leiomyosarcoma: An Unusual Cause of Pleural Effusion.

Leiomyosarcoma A. The pleural biopsy shows a neoplasm with sheets of hypercellular spindle cells (H&E, 100×). B. The tumor cells have marked nuclear atypia and brisk mitotic activity (H&E, 400×). They are positive for smooth muscle markers such as C. Caldesmon, and also D. ER immunostaining.H&E: hematoxylin and eosin, ER: estrogen receptor

PMID: 35392278

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Smooth muscle
