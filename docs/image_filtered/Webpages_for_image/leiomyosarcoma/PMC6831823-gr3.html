Retroperitoneal leiomyosarcoma mimicking gastric cancer recurrence: A case report.

Histological examination of biopsy. (A) pleomorphism with bizarre multinucleated tumor cells and high frequency mitosis (hematoxylin and eosin, ×100). (B) Tumor cells showed strong reactivity for desmin (desmin, ×200).

PMID: 31669956

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Smooth muscle
