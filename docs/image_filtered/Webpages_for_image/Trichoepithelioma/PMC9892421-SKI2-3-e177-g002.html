Trichoepithelioma presenting as leonine facies in a young female.

(a) Multiple aggregated skin‐coloured papules which are closely and firm. (b) Peripheral palisading in islands of basaloid cells with abortive hair papillae (black pointers) and trichilemmal keratinization (yellow pointer, ×100, H&E). (c) Abortive hair papilla (×400, H&E).

PMID: 36751311

Type: Benign, Category: Follicular
