Immunohistochemical analysis of the mechanistic target of rapamycin and hypoxia signalling pathways in basal cell carcinoma and trichoepithelioma.

Immunohistochemical staining for hypoxia inducible factor 1α (HIF1α) and phosphorylated mechanistic/mammalian target of rapamycin (pmTOR) and their target genes in trichoepithelioma (TE).BCL-2/adenovirus E1B 19 kDa-interacting protein 3 (BNIP3) (a); carbonic anhydrase IX (CAIX) (b); glucose transporter 1 (GLUT1) (c); HIF1α (d); phosphorylated protein kinase B (pAKT) (e); phosphorylated ribosomal protein S6 (pS6) (f); p-mTOR (g); prolyl hydroxylase domain protein 2 (PHD2) (h); vascular endothelial growth factor (VEGF-A)(i). Original magnification: (a–i) x 200.
