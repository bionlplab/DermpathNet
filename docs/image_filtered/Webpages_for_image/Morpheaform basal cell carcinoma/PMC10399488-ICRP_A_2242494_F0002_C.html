Morpheaform basal cell carcinoma of the nasal ala associated with multiple familial trichoepithelioma reconstructed by anterolateral thigh flap: a case report.

The lateral femoral circumflex artery and vein (LCFAV) was anastomosed with the facial artery and vein in an end-to-end manner, and the distal end of the descending branch was additionally anastomosed with the superficial temporal artery and vein.
