Extra ocular sebaceous adenocarcinoma in HIV-positive patient--case report.

Sebaceous adenocarcinoma: poorly delimitated lobules formed by stacks of basaloid cells with eosinophilic cytoplasm, centered by a population of cells with micro vacuolated cytoplasm, and hyperchromic, irregular nuclei. (HE 20X)
