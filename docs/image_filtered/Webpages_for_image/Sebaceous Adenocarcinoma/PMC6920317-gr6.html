A rare giant extraocular, anterior chest wall sebaceous carcinoma.

A: H/E section shows ulcerated skin with an invasive carcinoma with sebaceous differentiation. B: H/E (intermediate view) show basaloid tumour cells with cytological atypia and admixed with foamy/vacuolated cytoplasm in keeping with sebaceous cells.

PMID: 31835133

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Sebaceous
