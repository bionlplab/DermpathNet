Cutaneous Lymphadenoma: A Case of Recurrence after Shave Excision.

(a) Histopathological findings demonstrate ill-defined interconnected islands of epithelial cells within a sclerotic stroma (4x). (b) The epithelial cells have eosinophilic to clear cytoplasm and mild pleomorphic vesicular nuclei (10x). A peripheral rim of palisading basophilic cells surrounds the epithelial islands. A prominent lymphocytic infiltration and few plasma cells that spill over to the epithelial nests is also present (hematoxylin-eosin).

PMID: 33777457

Type: Benign, Category: Follicular
