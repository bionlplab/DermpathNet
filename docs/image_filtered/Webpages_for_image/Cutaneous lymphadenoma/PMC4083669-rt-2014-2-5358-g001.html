Cutaneous lymphadenoma: a rare case and brief review of a diagnostic pitfall.

A) A well-circumscribed lesion with basaloid nests in the dermis, rimmed by prominent lymphocytic inflammation at the deep margin (Hematoxylin & Eosin, 20×). B) Irregularly-shaped nests of basaloid and clear cells, without peripheral palisading or retraction artifact, in a collagenous stroma (Hematoxylin & Eosin, 100×). C) A thick rim of mature lymphocytes is present at the deep margin (Hematoxylin & Eosin, 100×). D) Mature lymphocytes are seen in the nests and stroma (Hematoxylin & Eosin, 100×).

PMID: 25002949

Type: Benign, Category: Follicular
