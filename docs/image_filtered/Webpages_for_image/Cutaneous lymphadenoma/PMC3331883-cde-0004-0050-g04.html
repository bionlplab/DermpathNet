Cutaneous lymphadenoma: a case report and immunohistochemical study.

Formalin-fixed paraffin-embedded tissue samples from patients with cutaneous lymphadenoma were sectioned at 4 μm and deparaffinized. Deparaffinized sections were treated with autoclave heating at 121°C for 5 min. After pretreatment, the sections were stained with an anti-Foxp3 Ab. The sections were developed with new fuchsin. Arrowheads indicate Foxp3+ cells (original magnification ×200).

PMID: 22529803

Type: Benign, Category: Follicular
