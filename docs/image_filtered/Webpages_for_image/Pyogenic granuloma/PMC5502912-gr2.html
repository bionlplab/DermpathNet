Oral Kaposi Sarcoma in HIV-seronegative Saudi patient: Literature review and case report.

Scanning electron microscopy with Hematoxylin and Eosin staining showing (A) lobulated vascular overgrowth enveloped by epithelial collarette mimicking a pyogenic granuloma (X100) and (B) spindle cell proliferation intermixed with numerous congested blood vessels. There are occasional mitotic figures (X200).
