Concurrent occurrence of mucocele and pyogenic granuloma.

(A) The tumor consisted of two components (H&E, ×40). (B) One component was a lobular structure surrounded by acanthotic epidermal collarette in the upper dermis. There were multiple dilated vessels surrounded by myxoid stroma in the lobular structure (H&E, ×100). (C) The other component was unilocular cyst in the lower dermis. There were infiltration of inflammatory cells and formation of granulation tissues were evident (H&E, ×100).

PMID: 22028553

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
