A Rare Case of Porocarcinoma and Trichoblastoma Arising in a Nevus Sebaceus of Jadassohn.

(a) Scanning micrograph of the porocarcinoma shows an expansile tumor arising from the epidermis with extension deep into the reticular dermis (hematoxylin and eosin, 4x). (b) Higher power shows focal necrosis of the tumor which displays cytologic atypia, including mitotic figures (hematoxylin and eosin, 20x).

PMID: 33763265

Type: Benign, Category: Sebaceous
