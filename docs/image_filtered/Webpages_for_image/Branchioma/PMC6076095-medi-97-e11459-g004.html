Ectopic hamartomatous thymoma (biphenotypic branchioma): A case report and review of the literature.

Immunohistochemistry findings of the tumor. (A–H: Original magnification ×100.). Spindle and epithelial islands were strongly positive for AE1/AE3 (A) and p63 (B). Spindle cells were positive for CD10 (C) and CD34 (D). Spindle cells were stained partly in SMA (E) and vimentin (F). Spindle and epithelial cells were negative for CK20 (G) and S-100 (H).

PMID: 29995801

Type: Benign, Category: Cystic
