Ectopic hamartomatous thymoma (biphenotypic branchioma): A case report and review of the literature.

Microscopic features of the tumor (H&E staining). The tumor consists of spindled cells, epithelial islands, and mature adipose tissue (A ×100). The spindle cells of the tissue are clearly arranged in a fascicular manner (B ×200) and in storiform manner (C ×100). In addition, keratinizing squamous nests (D ×40), nonkeratinizing squamous nests (E ×200) and gland-like structures (F ×100) can be seen in other areas.

PMID: 29995801

Type: Benign, Category: Cystic
