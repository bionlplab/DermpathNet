Development of dermatomyofibroma in a male infant.

Proliferation of bland-looking spindle cells with fascicular arrangement in the reticular dermis (A), extending focally into the subcutaneous tissue (B). There was no cytological atypia or mitotic figures (H&E; original magnification, A: ×40; B: ×200).

PMID: 22028578

Type: Benign, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
