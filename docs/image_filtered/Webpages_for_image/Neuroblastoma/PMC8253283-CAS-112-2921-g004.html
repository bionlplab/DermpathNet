Clonal evidence for the development of neuroblastoma with extensive copy-neutral loss of heterozygosity arising in a mature teratoma.

Histopathological evaluation of ovarian tumor. A‐C, H&E staining of the tumor. Neuroblastomatous tissue consisting of dense, solid proliferation of small round cells with increased chromatin. There are minor components with multilayered squamous epithelium with skin appendage‐like structures and choriocapillary epithelium
