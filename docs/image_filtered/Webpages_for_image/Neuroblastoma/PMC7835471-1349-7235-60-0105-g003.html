A Rare Case of Ectopic Adrenocorticotropic Hormone Syndrome with Recurrent Olfactory Neuroblastoma.

A left cervical lymph node biopsy of the tumor showing a histologically alveolar-like structure, accompanied by a neurofibril matrix-like image histologically similar to that of previous olfactory neuroblastoma specimens. Subsequently, this was diagnosed as lymph node metastasis of olfactory neuroblastoma. (A) Hematoxylin and Eosin (H&E) staining, 100× magnification. (B) H&E staining, 400× magnification.

PMID: 32921682

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
