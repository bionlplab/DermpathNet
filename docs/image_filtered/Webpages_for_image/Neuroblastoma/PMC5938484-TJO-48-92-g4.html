Olfactory Neuroblastoma: A Rare Cause of External Ophthalmoplegia, Proptosis and Compressive Optic Neuropathy.

(A) Relatively uniform, noncohesive tumor cells with inconspicuous nucleoli, indistinct nuclear membranes, and low cytoplasmic ratio (hematoxylin and eosin; x400). (B) Immunohistochemical analysis of the tumor cells shows CD56, chromogranin, and synaptophysin positivity and Ki-67 labeling index indicated high proliferation

PMID: 29755824

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
