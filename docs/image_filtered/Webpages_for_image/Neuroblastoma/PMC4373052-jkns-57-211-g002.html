A malignant transformation of a spinal epidural mass from ganglioneuroblastoma to neuroblastoma.

Photomicrograph showing tumor cells, which are round blastic in nature/appearance, and in between scattered mature ganglion cells (A : H&E, original magnification, ×200). The tumor cells were positive for CD56 (B : original magnification, ×100).
