Preclinical evaluation of the anti-tumor activity of pralatrexate in high-risk neuroblastoma cells.

Ex vivo tissue culture model recapitulated antitumor response to pralatrexate. Representative H&E and Ki67 immunohistochemistry staining sections were obtained from a neuroblastoma PDX, COG-N-415× treated ex vivo with 200 nM pralatrexate or vehicle control for 4 days, and demonstrated poorly differentiated neuroblastoma cells and decreased Ki67 staining in pralatrexate-treated tumor compared to vehicle control (20× magnification, scale bar, 100 μm).

PMID: 32850011

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
