Diagnostic dilemma in left flank mass in an infant: A rare case of intrarenal neuroblastoma.

(a-d) (a) H and E ×4. Sheets of tumour cells surrounded by areas of fibrosis. (b) H and E ×10- highly cellular tumour showing perivascular arrangement and pseudorosettes (Black arrow). Marked crushing is seen on the edges. (c) ×40 H and E- small-sized tumour cells showing high n:c ratio, moulding, hyperchromatic nucleus, irregular nuclear margin and scant cytoplasm. (d) ×40 Synaptophysin IHC. Shows cytoplasmic positivity in tumour cells. IHC: Immunohistochemistry
