Surgical Excision of an Adrenal Neuroblastoma in a Dog.

(A) Adrenal tumor. Low magnification image showing round to oval cells organized in ill-defined lobules, within a very poor stroma. (H&E, bar = 100 μm). (B) Adrenal tumor. High magnification image of the tumor cells. The cell limits are ill-defined, the cytoplasm is scant and pale, and the ovoid-shaped nucleus shows stippled chromatin distribution with no conspicuous nucleolus. (H&E, bar = 20 μm).

PMID: 31214604

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
