Neuroendocrine Carcinoma at the Sphenoid Sinus Misdiagnosed as an Olfactory Neuroblastoma and Resected Using High-Flow Bypass.

Histopathological findings of excised specimens. H&E staining shows a predominantly crushed artifact (A). Immunohistological staining is positive for synaptophysin (B) and CD56 (C). No S-100 protein-positive cells are observed (D).

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
