Involvement of the nuclear factor-κB signaling pathway in the regulation of CXC chemokine receptor-4 expression in neuroblastoma cells induced by tumor necrosis factor-α.

Immunohistochemical staining of NF-κB-p65 and CXCR4 in neuroblastoma and ganglioneuroma tissues. (A) Neuroblastoma tissues [hematoxylin and eosin (H&E) staining]. (B) Ganglioneuroma tissues (H&E staining). (C) High NF-κB-p65 expression in neuroblastoma tissues. (D) Low NF-κB-p65 expression in ganglioneuroma tissues. (E) High CXCR4 expression in neuroblastoma tissues. (F) Low CXCR4 expression in ganglioneuroma tissues. Original magnification, ×200. NF-κB, nuclear factor-κB; CXCR4, CXC chemokine receptor-4.
