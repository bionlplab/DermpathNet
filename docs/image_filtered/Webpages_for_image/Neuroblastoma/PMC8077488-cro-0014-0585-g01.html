5' ALK Amplification in Neuroblastoma: A Case Report.

Biopsy histology demonstrates sheets of round to spindled neoplastic cells with a high nuclear to cytoplasmic ratio and minimal cytoplasm. There is nuclear pleomorphism with variably sized nuclei and occasional prominent nucleoli, with scattered more prominently enlarged bizarre nuclei. There are frequent mitoses and apoptoses. H&E.
