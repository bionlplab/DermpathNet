Molecular Iodine/Cyclophosphamide Synergism on Chemoresistant Neuroblastoma Models.

Effect of I2 and CFF on the bladder morphology of the chemoresistant neuroblastoma model. The figure shows the urothelial thickness indicated by arrows in H&E representative micrographs (100 µM, bar graph) and the vasculature quantification (blood vessel number/field) of CD34 immunohistochemical staining (representative micrographs and bar graph). Three sections per tumor sample were analyzed. Data are expressed as the mean ± SD (n = 4). Different letters denote statistical differences (p < 0.05).
