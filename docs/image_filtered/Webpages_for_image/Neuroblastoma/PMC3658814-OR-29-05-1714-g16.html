Inhibition of the SK-N-MC human neuroblastoma cell line in vivo and in vitro by a novel nutrient mixture.

Effect of NM on the morphology of SK-N-MC cells as detected by H&E staining. Neuroblastoma cells exposed to various concentrations of NM indicated no morphological changes at concentrations <500 μg/ml. (A) Control, (B) NM 50 μg/ml, (C) NM 100 μg/ml, (D) NM 500 μg/ml and (E) NM 1,000 μg/ml.
