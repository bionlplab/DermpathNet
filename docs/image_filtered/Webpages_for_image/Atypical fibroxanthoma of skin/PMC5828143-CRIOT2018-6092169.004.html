An Unusual Case of Pinna Squamous Cell Carcinoma Arising Shortly after Resection of Atypical Fibroxanthoma.

Photomicrograph of excised nodular lesion (H&E stain) showing a dermal spindle cell lesion exhibiting marked pleomorphism and brisk mitoses. Immunohistochemically, focal staining with CD68 was seen. Morphologically and immunohistochemically, the features are consistent with AFX. (a) ×40 magnification. (b) ×20 magnification.

PMID: 29610695

Type: Benign, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
