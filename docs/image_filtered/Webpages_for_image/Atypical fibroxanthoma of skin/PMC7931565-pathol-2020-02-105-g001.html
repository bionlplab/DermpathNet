Atypical fibroxanthoma/pleomorphic dermal sarcoma of the scalp with aberrant expression of HMB-45: a pitfall in dermatopathology.

Dermal highly vascularized, ulcerated tumor composed of atypical spindle cells, arranged in fascicles, elongated nucleus, often in mitosis (H&E staining: 10x).

PMID: 32760054

Type: Benign, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
