Hidradenoma papilliferum of the vulva: a dermoscopic challenging diagnosis.

(A) Histopathological examination shows a dermal cystic neoplasm characterized by papillary projections (Hematoxylin & eosin, ×50). (B) Papillae are lined by myoepithelial cells (at the periphery) and tall columnar cells, showing decapitation secretion (Hematoxylin & eosin, ×100).

PMID: 36967274

Type: Benign, Category: Adnexal
