Hidradenoma papilliferum occurring on the nasal skin.

Variously shaped cystic and tubular structures were lined with a double or single layer of inner columnar and outer cuboidal cells. The cells of the lumina showed decapitation secretion similar to that of apocrine gland (A: H&E, ×40, B: H&E, ×200).

PMID: 22148064

Type: Benign, Category: Adnexal
