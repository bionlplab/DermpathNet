PTEN Methylation Dependent Sinonasal Mucosal Melanoma.

Pathologic findings of the biopsy specimen obtained from the nasal cavity mass. (A) Round tumor cells with atypia, showing pleomorphic and hyperchromatic nuclei with prominent nucleoli are seen (H&E staining, ×400). (B-D) Immunohistochemistry staining shows positive staining in the tumor cells for S-100 (B) and Melan A (C) (B and C, ×200), and decreased level of PTEN protein expression (D, ×200).

PMID: 25797573

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
