Near complete remission of a locally advanced giant melanoma of the vulva following hypo-fractionated radiotherapy and immune checkpoint inhibitors: A case report.

Histopathology and immunohistochemistry. (A) Pathology (magnification ×200; hematoxylin and eosin staining) shows pigmentation in the cytoplasm of melanoma cells (indicated by red arrows). (B-F) Immunohistochemistry (magnification ×100) was performed by immunoenzymatic cytochemistry and showed positive results for tumor markers (B) HMB45, (C) Melan-A, (D) S-100, (E) Ki-67 (30%) and (F) p53 (90%).

PMID: 36380876
