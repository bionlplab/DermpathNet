Tubulointerstitial Nephritis in an Advanced Melanoma Patient Treated with Encorafenib plus Binimetinib Combination Therapy.

PET-CT findings: left axillary lymph node (a) Histopathological findings of the renal biopsy: about 30% of glomeruli were sclerosing and collapsing but remaining glomeruli were intact (b), affected area: tubular atrophy (allow) and interstitial fibrosis with lymphocyte infiltration (allow) were observed in the tubular interstitial area (c, d). (H&E staining (b, c), Elastica Masson staining (d)). PET-CT, positron emission tomography CT.

PMID: 35702675

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
