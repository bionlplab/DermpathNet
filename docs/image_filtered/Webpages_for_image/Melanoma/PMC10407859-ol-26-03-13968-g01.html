Immunohistochemical detection of the chondroitin sulfate proteoglycan 4 protein in primary and metastatic melanoma.

H&E and CSPG4 staining of representative primary melanoma and melanoma metastases. (A) Primary melanoma sample (magnification ×100). (B) Positive immunohistochemical staining of a primary melanoma with a mAb specific for CSPG4 (magnification, ×20). (C) Sample of a melanoma metastasis (magnification ×100). (D) Negative immunohistochemical staining of a melanoma metastasis with a mAb specific for CSPG4 (magnification, ×20). CSPG4, chondroitin sulfate proteoglycan 4; H&E, hematoxylin and eosin; mAb, monoclonal antibody.
