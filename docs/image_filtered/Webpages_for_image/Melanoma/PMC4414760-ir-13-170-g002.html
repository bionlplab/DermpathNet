Primary anorectal malignant melanoma treated with endoscopic mucosal resection.

Pathologic findings. (A) A gross view of the resected specimen, measuring 15×12 mm, shows a dark, black-pigmented solid tumor with a short stalk. (B) Microscopic findings show diffuse infiltration of round or spindle-shaped tumor cells with lymphocytes (H&E, ×40). (C) Immunohistochemically, tumor cells are positive for Human Melanin Black-45 (HMB-45 staining, ×400).

PMID: 25932003

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
