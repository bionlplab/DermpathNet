Delayed recurrence of an iridociliary malignant melanoma 180° from the primary tumor.

H&E stained sections of the right eye enucleation specimen following iris melanoma recurrence. Figures A–C show the recurrent iris lesion at 3 o'clock at 4× (A), 20× (B), and 40× (C) magnification. The lesion is composed of mixed spindle B and epithelioid cells (C). Figures D–F show the treated 9 o'clock lesion at 4× (D), 20× (E), and 40× (F) magnification. The lesion is composed of predominantly epithelioid cells with spindle B cells (F). Tumor invades the angle and extends into the anterior ciliary body (B, E).

PMID: 36262689

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
