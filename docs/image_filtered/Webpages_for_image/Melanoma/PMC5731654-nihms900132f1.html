RAF Inhibitor Therapy Promotes Melanocytic Antigen Expression and Enhanced Anti-Tumor Immunity in Melanoma.

Induction of melanocyte antigen expression upon BRAFi treatment and loss at progression. Representative staining for MITF expression in a patient with metastatic melanoma at 10X and 40X shows an increase upon initiation of BRAFi and a decrease at disease progression on therapy.

PMID: 29250602

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
