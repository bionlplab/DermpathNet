Melanocytic Lesions of the Nail Unit.

Early in situ melanoma. a A 36-year-old male patient with a 6-year history of melanonychia located on his left hand thumbnail. See the black-brown pigmentation involving the whole nail plate area. b There was an increased density of solitary units of melanocytes displayed in a abnormal pattern at all layers of the subungual epithelium. Note the confluency of cells and pagetoid spread. Pigmentation was minimal. H&E, original magnification ×4. c There was an increased density of solitary units of atypical epithelioid melanocytes in the lower third of the subungual matrix epithelium. H&E, original magnification ×40. d Atypical melanocytes in the matrix epithelium were positive for HMB-45. Melan A, ×40.

PMID: 30197884

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
