Dirofilaria repens Infection Mimicking Lung Melanoma Metastasis.

Pulmonary nodule filled with necrotic material and worms, surrounded by inflammatory cells. Hematoxylin and eosin (H&E). Original magnification 2x (A). High-power view of worms with features consistent with Dirofilaria repens; note the thick cuticle and the presence of internal organs. H&E. Original magnification 10x (B).

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
