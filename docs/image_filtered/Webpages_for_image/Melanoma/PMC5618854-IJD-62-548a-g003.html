Lower Extremity Desmoplastic Malignant Melanoma in Egypt.

(a) The dermis is infiltrated by pleomorphic spindle-shaped cells within intervening marked desmoplastic stroma (H and E, ×100). (b) Dermal pleomorphic spindle-shaped cells with bizarre nuclei and retraction artifact admixed with mature collagen fibers (arrow) (H and E, ×400). (c) The tumor cells are strongly positive for Melan-A (×100). (d) The tumor cells are strongly positive for S100 immunostaining (×100)

PMID: 28979029

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
