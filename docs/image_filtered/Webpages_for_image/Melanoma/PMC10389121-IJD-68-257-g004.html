Cytomorphological Spectrum of Malignant Melanoma: A 3-Year Study in a Tertiary Care Centre in India.

(a) Cellular smears showing dispersed cells in low power. Intracellular pigment and few multinucleated forms also seen (HE X 10X). (b) Spindle cell variant comprising of mostly elongated cells arranged in clusters (HE X 10X). (c) Cells with mild to moderate nuclear pleomorphism and prominent nucleoli also noted (HE X 40X). (d) Tumour cells showing intranuclear inclusion (May Grunwald-Giemsa (MGG) X 40X)

PMID: 37529442

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
