The texture of collagen and immunoexpression of PRAME in dysplastic nevus syndrome lesions: relationship with melanoma.

Dysplastic nevus syndrome: (A) Classical Dysplastic Nevus (CDN): regular epidermal hyperplasia, small clusters of melanocytes at the dermoepidermal junction (yellow arrows) and papillary dermis fibroplasia (blue arrow); (B, C) DN with High-Grade histological atypia (DNHG): irregular epidermal hyperplasia, melanocyte aggregates of varying volumes, in varied distribution and with moderate to marked multifocal cytological atypia (yellow arrows) and papillary dermis fibroplasia (blue arrow); (D) DN with High-Grade histological atypia (DNHG): nuclear immunoexpression of the PRAME antigen in the melanocytes present at the dermoepidermal junction. (A‒C) Hematoxylin & eosin; (D) immunohistochemistry; ×100 (A, B) and ×400 (C, D).

PMID: 36369201

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
