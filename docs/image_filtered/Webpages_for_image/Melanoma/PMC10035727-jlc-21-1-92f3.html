A Case of Metastatic Melanoma in the Liver Mimicking Hepatocellular Carcinoma.

Pathologic findings of the liver biopsy specimen. The tumor cells show brown-pigment cytoplasmic pigmentation, which is consistent with malignant melanoma (A, hematoxylin and eosin ×100). Immunohistochemical staining for Melan-A (melanocyte antigen) is positive (B, ×40).

PMID: 37384272

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
