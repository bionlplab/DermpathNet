Lack of clinical efficacy of imatinib in metastatic melanoma.

Immunohistochemical staining of CD117/c-kit, PDGF-Rα and PDGF-Rβ in biopsy specimens of metastatic lesions obtained from six different melanoma patients prior to imatinib treatment. One representative example of each, positive and negative staining, is presented. Magnification × 100.
