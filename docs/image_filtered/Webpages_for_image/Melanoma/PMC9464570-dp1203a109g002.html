A Pink Nodule With White Halo: Dermoscopy of Halo Melanoma.

(A) Dermal proliferation of atypical achromic melanocytes with nodular dermal expansion and collarette formation at the periphery (H&E, 4x). (B) Immunohistochemistry supports the melanocytic phenotype and emphasizes the lack of junctional melanocytes in the epidermis straddling the collarette (HMB45 immunostaining with hematoxylin counterstain, 4x).

PMID: 36159120

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
