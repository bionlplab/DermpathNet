The curious incident of 3 melanomas and their possible origins-A case report and review of literature.

Hematoxylin and eosin stained sections of excision biopsy specimen of the left breast nodule showing a lobulated proliferation of sheets of epithelioid cells featuring enlarged vesicular nuclei with conspicuous nucleoli and ample eosinophilic cytoplasm (x5) (A), accompanied by scattered intracytoplasmic brownish pigment (X20) (B). Focally the tumor cells display eccentric nuclei with hyaline cytoplasmic inclusions (x40) (C). Immunohistochemistry showed strong and diffuse staining for S100 (D) and melan-A (E). Focal positivity for HMB45 was noted (F).

PMID: 27100953

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
