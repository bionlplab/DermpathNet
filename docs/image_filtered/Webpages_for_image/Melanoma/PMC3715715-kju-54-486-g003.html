Prostate metastasis of malignant melanoma.

(A) Microscopic picture of metastatic malignant melanoma in the prostate (H&E, ×400). (B) Immunohistochemical stains for S-100 protein showed an intense diffuse positivity (×200). (C) Immunohistochemical stains for HMB-45 protein showed an intense diffuse positivity (×200). (D) Melanin A was also diffusely positive (×200).
