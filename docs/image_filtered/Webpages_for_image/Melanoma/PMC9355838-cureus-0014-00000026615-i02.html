Acral Amelanotic Melanoma Mimicking a Foot Ulcer.

Nests of atypical melanocytes are arrayed in confluence along the basal layer underlying a papillated epidermal surface. Melanocytes extend deeply along adnexal epithelium and are present within the underlying dermis (hematoxylin & eosin, original magnification 100x).

PMID: 35936139

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
