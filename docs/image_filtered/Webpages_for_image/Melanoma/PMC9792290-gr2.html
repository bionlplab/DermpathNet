Amelanotic conjunctival melanoma in a child.

H&E stain. A. First biopsy showing polypoid neoplasm composed of nests of melanocytes cells (original magnification). B. Spindle cells with nuclear heterogeneity and cytologic atypia. Some nuclei contain nucleoli C. The subepithelial nests showed focal cyst formation at the depth of the lesion. Mitotic activity in submucosal melanocytes (black arrow) indicating proliferation (haematoxylin and eosin, original magnification X40).

PMID: 36582844

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
