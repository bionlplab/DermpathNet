Primary Clear Cell Sarcoma of the Dermis Mimicking Malignant Melanoma.

Histologic examination and immunohistochemical staining of the tumour. Higher magnification shows (a) nests and fascicles of spindle cells with clear cytoplasm and (b) wreath-like giant cells H&E x40 magnification. The tumour cells were positive for (c) S-100 and (d) HMB-45 (x400 magnification).

PMID: 29072181

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
