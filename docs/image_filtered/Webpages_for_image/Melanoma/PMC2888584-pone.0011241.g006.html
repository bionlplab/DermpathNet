Endothelin-1 inhibits prolyl hydroxylase domain 2 to activate hypoxia-inducible factor-1alpha in melanoma cells.

ETBR blockade results in vivo in neovascularization inhibition, associated with reduced HIF-α and increased PHD2 expression. A. Matrigel sections containing PBS (C), ET-1, or BQ788+ET-1 were immunostained with anti-CD31 (arrows; original magnification ×160). B. Expression of indicated proteins was analyzed in M10 tumor xenografts by Western blot analysis. C. Tumors removed from control and A-192621-treated M10 xenografts were analyzed for PHD2 expression (original magnification ×250). D. Human metastatic melanoma tissues were analyzed for ETBR, PHD2, and HIF-1α expression (original magnification ×250).
