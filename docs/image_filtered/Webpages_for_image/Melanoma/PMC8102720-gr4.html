Melanoma arising in a persistent nevus: Melanoma where 'pseudomelanoma' is expected.

Atypical melanocytes with enlarged and hyperchromatic nuclei were observed to invade into the dermis to a depth of 0.8 mm. Lymphocytes surrounded dermal melanocytes, and pigment was distributed in an irregular fashion. (Hematoxylin-eosin–stain; original magnification, ×100.)

PMID: 33997217

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
