Pathological morphology features and immunohistochemistry results of the patient. (A) Hematoxylin and eosin-stained section shows tumour cell pleomorphism and nuclear atypia. Immunohistochemical staining in the tumour cells for (B) S-100, showing nuclear and cytoplasmic positivity (streptavidin-peroxidase staining); (C) HMB-45, showing strong cytoplasmic positivity (streptavidin-peroxidase staining); and (D) melan-A, showing cytoplasmic positivy (streptavidin-peroxidase staining).
