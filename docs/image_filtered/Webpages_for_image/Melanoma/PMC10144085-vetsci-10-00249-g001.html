Expression and Prognostic Evaluation of the Receptor Tyrosine Kinase MET in Canine Malignant Melanoma.

Representative histologic samples of canine malignant melanoma tissue after H&E and c-MET immunostaining. A score of 0 is for negative immunopositivity, 1 for mild/moderate, and 2 for marked immunopositivity. Insets at high magnification for MET immunostaining highlight membranous staining (arrows).
