Primary malignant melanoma of the gastric antrum: A case report.

(A, B, and C) H&E at 4×, 10×, and 40× show infiltration of the antral mucosa by atypical cells with large irregular vesicular nuclei and prominent nucleoli. (D, E, and F) H&E at 4×: Neoplastic cells are positive for Melan A (D) and negative for AE 1/3 (E), and CD20 (F)

PMID: 34188931

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
