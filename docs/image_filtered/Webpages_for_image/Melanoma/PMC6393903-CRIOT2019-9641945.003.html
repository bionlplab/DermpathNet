A Case Report of Melanoma as Acute Mastoiditis in a 10-Month-Old Female Child.

Foetal metastases in the mastoid. The biopsy shows a tumour consisting of nests of small-sized and medium-sized cells, with oval to round nuclei and poorly defined eosinophilic cytoplasms (hematoxylin-eosin, original magnification 200x).

PMID: 30906611

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
