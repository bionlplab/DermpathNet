Choroidal Melanoma Metastatic to the Contralateral Medial Rectus After Orbital Exenteration.

Histopathological examination of muscle biopsy. (A) Hematoxylin and eosin stain, 200X; diffusely infiltrative nested malignant cells, some with intracytoplasmic brown pigment; (B) Melan A immunohistochemistry stain, 200X; diffusely positive staining consistent with melanoma; (C) hematoxylin and eosin stain, 600X; abnormal plump spindle and epithelioid cells showing nuclear pleomorphism with some nuclei bearing inclusions and others intracytoplasmic pigment consistent with melanoma cells (C).

PMID: 31650817

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
