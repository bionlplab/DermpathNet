Metastatic melanoma posing as a pelvic mass.

Melanoma immunohistochemical stain. High power image of the malignant melanoma showing sheets of malignant cells with large hyperchromatic nuclei, adjacent areas of tumor cell necrosis, and clumps of dark brown melanin pigment (H&E × 100). Inset shows diffuse positivity of the tumor cells for sox10 (red chromagen) confirming the diagnosis of melanoma. Clusters of necrotic tumor cells show lack of staining.

PMID: 36647550

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
