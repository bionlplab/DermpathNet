Malignant melanoma - cutaneous metastases.

Skin biopsy from nodule – showing epidermis and subepithelial stroma with sparse mononuclear cell collections. Note that the stratified squamous epithelium is unremarkable, × 10 H and E Stain (A) and × 20 H and E stain (B). A circumscribed nodule composed of round to oval cells with prominent nucleoli, × 20 H and E (C). Tumor nodule showing prominent nucleoli and pigment, × 40 H and E (D).

PMID: 19882041

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
