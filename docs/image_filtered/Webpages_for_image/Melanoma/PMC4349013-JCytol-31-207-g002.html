Cytodiagnosis of epithelioid malignant melanoma (amelanotic) and diagnostic dilemmas.

(a) Pleomorphic epithelioid cells with high N:C ratio (H and E, ×100) (b) Prominent nucleoli and occasional binucleated cells (H and E, ×400) (c) Tumor cells showing strong immunoreactivity with Melan-A (IHC, ×400), (d) Tumor cells showing strong nuclear and cytoplasmic reactivity with S-100 (IHC, ×400)
