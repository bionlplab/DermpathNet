PRIMARY MALIGNANT MELANOMA OF THE URINARY BLADDER: CASE REPORT.

Histologic appearance of the urinary bladder melanoma showing partly necrotic tumorous tissue composed of solid nests and groups of atypical, poorly differentiated, highly mitotically active cells with prominent nucleoli: HE x40 (A); HE x400 (B); immunohistochemistry indicating positive reaction to S-100 (x100) (C); HMB-45 (x100) (D); and MITF (x100) (E).

PMID: 31363341

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
