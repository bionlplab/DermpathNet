Uterine cervical melanoma presenting with rapid progression detected by PET/CT.

Excision biopsy of the cervical polyp showed sheets or solid nests of epithelioid tumor cells with pleomorphic nuclei and scattered mitotic figures. (a) The tumor cells spread along the basal layer of the squamous epithelium focally (H&E, ×400); (b) Some tumor cells were also positive for HMB45 (HMB45, ×400)

PMID: 23986839

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
