Lepromatous leprosy, melanoma, and basal cell carcinoma: clinical-histopathologic association.

(A) Basal cell carcinoma (BCC) – nasal; melanoma – right temporal region. (B) BCC – clusters of basaloid cells (Hematoxylin & eosin, ×10). (C) Peripheral palisading of cells (Hematoxylin & eosin, ×100). (D) Melanoma – nests of atypical melanocytes and pagetoid spread (Hematoxylin & eosin, ×200).
