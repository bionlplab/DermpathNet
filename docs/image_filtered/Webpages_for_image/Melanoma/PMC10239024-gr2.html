Large extraocular extension of a choroidal melanoma with orbital inflammation.

Histopathology showing a uveal melanoma with extraocular extension at low (A), 20x (B), and 200x (C) magnification. The central portion of the extraocular component demonstrates necrosis with the presence of prominent melanin-laden macrophages.
