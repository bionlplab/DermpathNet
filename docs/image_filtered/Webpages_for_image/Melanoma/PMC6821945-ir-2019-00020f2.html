Primary malignant melanoma without melanosis of the colon.

Pathologic findings. (A) Spindle cellular area and round nodular growing area were observed (H&E, ×40). (B) Spindle cellular area was observed (H&E, ×100). (C) Spindle tumor cells with frequent mitoses were observed (H&E, ×400). (D) Round nodular growing area was observed (H&E, ×100). (E) Round tumor cells with vesicular nuclei, prominent nucleoli, and frequent mitoses (H&E, ×400).

PMID: 31302994

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
