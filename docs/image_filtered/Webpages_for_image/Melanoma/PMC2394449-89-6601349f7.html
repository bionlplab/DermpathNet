Anti-inflammatory and anti-invasive effects of alpha-melanocyte-stimulating hormone in human melanoma cells.

Histology of melanoma invasion through reconstructed skin composites containing keratinocytes, fibroblasts and HBL cells±α-MSH/IBMX. Composites were cultured for 14 days in the absence (A, C and E) and presence (B, D and F) of α-MSH (1 × 10−9 M)/IBMX (50 μM). Composites have been labelled for HMB45 (for detection of HBL cells). Bar for (A)–(F)=40 μm; bar for (E)=20 μm.

PMID: 14612916

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
