Primary Clear Cell Sarcoma of the Dermis Mimicking Malignant Melanoma.

Histopathological examination of the skin. Sections show (a) diffuse infiltration of the dermis with sheets of neoplastic cells (b) without the involvement of the epidermis H&E x40 and x100 magnification respectively.

PMID: 29072181

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
