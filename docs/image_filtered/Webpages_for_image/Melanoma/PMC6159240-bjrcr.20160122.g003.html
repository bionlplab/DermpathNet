Perineural spread of recurrent cutaneous melanoma along cervical nerves into the spinal cord.

Photomicrographs. (a) Papanicolaou stain (original magnification x40)—fragments of tumour tissue containing spindle and epithelioid malignant cells with focal mitosis (arrow) morphologically consistent with melanoma. (b) Immunohistochemistry S100 (original magnification x40)—tumour cells exhibit nuclear and cytoplasmic staining. (c) Haematoxylin and eosin stain of cell block—desmoplastic stroma infiltrated by a single malignant cell (arrow).

PMID: 30363279

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
