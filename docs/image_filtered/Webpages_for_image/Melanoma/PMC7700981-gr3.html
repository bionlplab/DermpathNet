Lymphovascular invasion of malignant melanoma presenting as gray streaks on dermoscopy.

A and B, Biopsy of gray streak shows foci of atypical melanocytes within the lumens of multiple lymphatic channels, which show strong positive staining for D2-40 on immunohistochemistry. (Original magnifications: A, ×100; B, ×400.)
