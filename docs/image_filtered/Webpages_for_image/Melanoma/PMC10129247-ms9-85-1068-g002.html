Primary anorectal melanoma mimicking polyp in a scleroderma patient: a case report.

The histopathology examination of the excised polyp revealed: (A) pigmented polygonal and spindle cells infiltrate (arrows) [hematoxylin and eosin (H&E) ×40], (B) melanin deposits and nesting pattern of neoplastic cells (H&E ×100), (C) neoplastic melanocytes with higher magnification (H&E ×200) and (D) immunohistochemistry staining shows positivity for S100.

PMID: 37113947

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
