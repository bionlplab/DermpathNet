Case Report: Immune-Related Toxicity During Adjuvant Treatment With BRAF Plus MEK Inhibitors in a Melanoma Patient.

(A) The skin biopsy shows a sarcoidal granulomatous dermatitis within the dermis. The epidermis is normal. (Ematoxylin and Eosin staining; 10x). (B, C) The sarcoidal granulomas are discrete nodular aggregates of epithelioid histiocytes with very few lymphoid cells at the periphery (“naked granulomas”). Tattoo pigment is seen within the cytoplasm of histiocytes. Necrosis is not present. (Ematoxylin and Eosin staining; 40x).

PMID: 33312171

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
