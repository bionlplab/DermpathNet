Acral Melanocytic Neoplasms: A Comprehensive Review of Acral Nevus and Acral Melanoma in Asian Perspective.

Acral melanoma in situ in a 68-year-old woman. (A) Large irregular black patch on the outer area of the heel. (B) Dermoscopy showing parallel ridge pattern. (C) Skin biopsy showing a slight increase in basal pigmentation with mild lymphocytic infiltrate (H&E staining, original magnification, ×100). (D) Melan-A immunostaining highlights lentiginous proliferation of atypical melanocytes with prominent dendrites (×100).

PMID: 35997352

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
