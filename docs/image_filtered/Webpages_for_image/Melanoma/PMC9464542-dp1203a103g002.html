Cutaneous Spitzoid Melanoma in Childhood After Acute Lymphocytic Leukemia.

Melanocytic neoplasm characterized by proliferation of cells that are either epithelioid or fusiform with eosinophilic cytoplasm, whose nuclei have a moderate degree of polymorphism and prominent nucleoli, forming an extensive dermal lesion (H&Eeosin, 100x).

PMID: 36159140

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
