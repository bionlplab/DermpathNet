Ruptured epidermal cyst mimicking cutaneous melanoma on F-18 FDG PET/CT.

71-year-old man with ruptured epidermal cyst. Photomicrograph (H and E, x200) shows chronic active inflammation with foreign body type giant cells characteristic of granulomatous reaction to ruptured epidermal cyst.

PMID: 27303506

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
