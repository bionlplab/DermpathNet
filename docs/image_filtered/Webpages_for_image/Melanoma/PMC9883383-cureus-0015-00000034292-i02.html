Incipient Melanonychia: Benign Finding or Occult Malignancy? A Case Report of Subungual Melanoma.

(A) Hematoxylin and eosin stain, x4. Microscopy of the nail matrix tissue specimen shows epithelial acanthosis (black brackets) and pagetoid upward migration of individual epidermal cells with clear cytoplasm and groups of these cells in a spindle shape. (B) Hematoxylin and eosin stain, x10 and x40, respectively. Microscopy of the nail matrix tissue specimen with greater magnification shows the individual epidermal cells with clear cytoplasm (red arrows).

PMID: 36721707

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
