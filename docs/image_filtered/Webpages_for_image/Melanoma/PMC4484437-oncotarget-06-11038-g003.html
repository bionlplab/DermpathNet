Low thyroid hormone levels improve survival in murine model for ocular melanoma.

(A) Macro metastasis of B16F10 cells in the lung of a representative mice. (B) An aggregate of large epithelioid melanoma cells with expanded cytoplasm, large nuclei and prominent nucleoli within it (arrow), is surrounded by typical lung tissue (H&EX10). (C) An adjacent section of the lung is positive for S100 immunostaining confirming the presence of melanoma cells (X10). (D) Enucleated murine eye showing the intraocular tumor located behind the lens, between the pigment epithelium (arrow) and retina (arrowhead, H&EX2). (E) Tumor cells behind the lens (H&EX40).
