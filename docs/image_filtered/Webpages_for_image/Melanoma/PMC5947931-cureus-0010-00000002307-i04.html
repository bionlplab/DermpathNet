Subungual Amelanotic Melanoma Masquerading as Onychomycosis.

Microscopic examination of the melanoma-associated antigen recognized by T cells 1 (MART-1) and S100 immunoperoxidase stained nail bed biopsy from a subungual amelanotic melanoma.There is brown staining (arrows) of the subungual amelanotic melanoma tumor cells confirming melanocyte lineage (Melanoma-associated antigen recognized by T cells 1 immunoperoxidase: top x10; S100 immunoperoxidase, bottom x20). The bottom view demonstrates epidermis and underlying dermis; there is brown staining (arrows) of the subungual amelanotic melanoma tumor cells in the dermis, after staining with S100, which confirms melanocyte lineage.

PMID: 29755903

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
