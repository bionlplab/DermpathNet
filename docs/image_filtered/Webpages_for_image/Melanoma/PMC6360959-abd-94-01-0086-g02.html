Amelanotic acral melanoma misdiagnosed as verruca plantaris.

A and B: Histopathology: macular papule showed tumor cells arranged as nests in dermo-epidermal junction and dermis, and no obvious melanin was observed; C and D: histopathology: left inguinal lymph nodes revealed dense infiltration of tumor cells with rich melanin granules in cytoplasm (hematoxylin & eosin, A, x40; B, x200 ; C, x40 ; D, x200)

PMID: 30726470

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
