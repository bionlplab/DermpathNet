Amelanotic Melanoma: A Great Masquerader.

( A , B ) Tru-cut biopsy showing a cellular tumor present in cohesive sheets with a relatively plump, plasmacytoid morphology, and ovoid nuclei (hematoxylin and eosin [H&E] 100 × , 200 × ). ( C ) Tumor cells showing eccentrically placed nuclei having immature to mature chromatin, small to conspicuous nucleoli, and moderate amount of cytoplasm (H&E 400 × ). ( D ) The tumor interface with liver parenchyma ( arrow ) showed many mature plasma cells (H&E 200 × ).

PMID: 37323600

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
