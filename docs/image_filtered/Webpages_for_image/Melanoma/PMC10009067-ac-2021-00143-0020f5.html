Multiple gastrointestinal melanoma causing small bowel intussusception.

(A) Small bowel polyps showing malignant cells at the lamina propria and submucosa (H&E, × 4). (B) The malignant cells are arranged in diffuse sheets with presence of intracytoplasmic and extracellular pigments (H&E, × 20). (C) Small bowel polyps showing malignant cells at the lamina propria and submucosa (H&E, × 4). (D) These cells are strongly positive for melan-A immunohistochemistry stain (× 4).
