Advanced acral melanoma.

Pathology of acral melanoma. A, Histologically the lesion is densely cellular with minimal intervening stroma. B and C, Higher-power magnification demonstrates epithelioid tumor cells with marked cellular and nuclear atypia. D, An immunohistochemical study for S100 strongly and diffusely labels the tumor cells. (Original magnification: ×40.) (A to C, Hematoxylin-eosin stain; original magnifications: A, ×40; B, ×100; C, ×200.)
