Intense FDG uptake in an intra-articular localized giant-cell tumor of the tendon sheath (pigmented villonodular synovitis) mimics metastatic melanoma.

72-year-old man with giant-cell tumor of the tendon sheath. High-power view (20X) shows histiocyte-like tumor cells, lymphocytes, hemosiderin-laden macrophages and osteoclast-like giant cells consistent with localized GTTTS (PVNS).

PMID: 27307839

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
