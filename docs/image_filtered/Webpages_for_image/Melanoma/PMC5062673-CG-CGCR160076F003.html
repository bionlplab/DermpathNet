Primary Bile Duct Melanoma Causing Obstructive Jaundice.

(A) Smear of FNA specimen from bile duct showing pleomorphic malignant cells with enlarged nuclei and multiple nucleoli. (B) H&E stain 200x magnification showing pleomorphic malignant cells, some with pigmentation and atypical mitotic figures. These tumor cells are strongly and diffusely positive for S-100 and MART-1 stains as below. (C) Tumor cells showing positive staining for S-100. (D) Tumor cells showing positive stain for MART stain.

PMID: 27807580

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
