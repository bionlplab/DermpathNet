Late prostatic metastasis of an uveal melanoma in a miniature Schnauzer dog.

Prostate metastasis of the uveal melanoma. (A) The prostate glandular tissue was replaced by spindle cells, part of which were pigmented (H&E). (B) Melan A labeling positive for 80% of the cells present in the tumor. Intensity was considered moderate to strong (Novolink Polymer Detection System, Mayer's hematoxylin). The chromogen used for labeling is brown.

PMID: 27386120

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
