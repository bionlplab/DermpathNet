LASP1 expression in normal skin, melanocytic nevi, melanoma and metastases.(A) Representative immunohistochemical stainings of normal skin, melanocytic nevus, melanoma, and metastasis tissue samples for LASP1. Hematoxylin counterstaining; magnification 20x; Scale bars = 100 μm). (B) Gene expression patterns of LASP1 in skin tumor tissues: normal body atlas GSE3526; melanoma GSE10282, GSE15605, and GSE35640; normal skin GSE14905 and GSE13355. The number of samples is given in parentheses. p values < 0.05 were considered statistically significant (two-tailed student’s t-test with Welch’s correction).
