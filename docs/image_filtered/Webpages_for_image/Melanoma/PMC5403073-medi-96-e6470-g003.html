Diffuse melanosis cutis related to dermal micrometastases as the first clinical symptom of distant metastatic malignant melanoma: Case report.

Histological examination of a skin biopsy (A—H&E staining ×100 and B—H&E staining ×400) performed in the region of clinically observed melanosis showed intracellular and extracellular melanin deposition and focal dermal infiltration by collection of pigment—laden macrophages (C—immunostain for CD68, ×400) and dermal melanoma metastatic cells (D—immunostain for HMB45, ×400).

PMID: 28403076

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
