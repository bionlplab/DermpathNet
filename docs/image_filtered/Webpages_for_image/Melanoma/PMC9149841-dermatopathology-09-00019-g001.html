PRAME Expression as Helpful Immunohistochemical Marker in Rhabdoid Melanoma.

(a): Rhabdoid melanoma cells exhibit eosinophilic cytoplasm displaying a round eccentric vesicular nucleus with a prominent central nucleolus and large intracytoplasmic hyalin inclusions (Hematoxylin&Eosin (H&E), ×400). (b): Vimentin is expressed in the intracytoplasmic inclusions (Vimentin, ×400).

PMID: 35645230

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
