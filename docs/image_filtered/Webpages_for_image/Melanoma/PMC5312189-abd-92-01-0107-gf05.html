Synchronous melanomas arising within nevus spilus.

Histopathology of the nevus spilus showing mild acanthosis, elongation of rete ridges, and lentiginous proliferation of basal melanocytes at the dermoepidermal junction with hyperpigmentation of basal keratinocytes (Hematoxylin & eosin x200)
