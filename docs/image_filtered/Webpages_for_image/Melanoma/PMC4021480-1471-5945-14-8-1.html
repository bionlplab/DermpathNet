miR-125b induces cellular senescence in malignant melanoma.

Expression of miR-125b in primary cutaneous malignant melanoma and lymph node metastases shown with ISH. Primary cutaneous MM (stage T2; A, C) and a sentinel node with micrometastases (B) were hybridized with the probe for miR-125b (A, B) or with the control, scrambled probe (C). Arrows show the cells with prominent staining for miR-125b. (D) U6 probe (positive control). Magnification: A x60, B x40, C x20, D x20.
