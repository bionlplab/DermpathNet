Metastatic melanoma to the testis.

Histopathology sections of the testicular lesion. (a) Low-power image showing tumour cells almost completely obliterating the testicular parenchyma. (b) High-power image showing cells with large, epithelioid cytoplasm, and large nuclei and focal prominent nucleoli. (Haematoxylin and eosin, x4 and x40, original magnification).
