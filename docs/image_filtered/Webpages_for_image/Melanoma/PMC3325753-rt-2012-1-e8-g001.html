Malignant melanoma of the conjunctiva: a case report with examination of KIT and PDGFRA.

Histology of the conjunctival tumor. Malignant epitheloid cells are seen. Brown pigment was present. These features are suspicious of conjunctival melanoma. Haematoxylin & Eosin, x200.

PMID: 22532924

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
