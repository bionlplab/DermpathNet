Cutaneous chromoblastomycosis mimicking melanoma in a renal transplant recipient.

(a) In the epidermis there is pseudoepitheliomatous hyperplasia and in the dermis there is a granulomatous inflammatory infiltrate with suppuration, intermingled with numerous melanized fungi (HE,40x); (b) In detail, the presence of numerous yeasts of melanized fungi, grouped together permeating the areas with suppuration (HE,400x); (c) Fontana-Masson stain: the large amount of melanized fungi is highlighted (40x); (d) By the Fontana-Masson stain, spores of dematiaceous fungi are noted, and some are dichotomized (1000x).

PMID: 36393996

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
