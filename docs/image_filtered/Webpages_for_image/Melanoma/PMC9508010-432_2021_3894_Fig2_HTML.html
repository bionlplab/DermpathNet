Melanocytic nevi in sentinel lymph nodes: association with cutaneous nevi and clinical relevance in patients with cutaneous melanomas.

Histology and immunohistochemistry of melanocytic nevi in a sentinel lymph nodes: In the H&E staining, small groups of melanocytic cells in the nodal capsule (⇒) show bland cytomorphology. Localization of melanocytes within the capsule or trabeculae of the lymph node strongly supports the diagnosis of a benign NN (A HE × 100; B HE × 200). Positive cytoplasmatic staining for Melan A (C × 200) and positive nuclear staining for p16 (D × 200)

PMID: 35059868

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
