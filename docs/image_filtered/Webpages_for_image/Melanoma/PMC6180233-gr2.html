Tumoral melanosis associated with combined BRAF/MEK inhibition (dabrafenib/trametinib) in metastatic melanoma.

A, Large nodular collection of heavily pigmented melanophages in the dermis. No viable melanoma cells were identified. B, Immunohistochemistry shows strong positive staining for CD68 and C, negative staining for S100. (A, Hematoxylin-eosin stain; C, red chromagen; original magnifications: A, x20; B and C, x100.)

PMID: 30320196

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
