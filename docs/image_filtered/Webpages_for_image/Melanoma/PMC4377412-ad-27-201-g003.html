Amelanotic Acral Melanoma Associated with KIT Mutation and Vitiligo.

(A) Biopsy of a hypopigmented patch on the face shows the complete absence of melanocytes in the epidermis (H&E, ×100). (B) In immunohistochemical staining, the biopsy was negative for Melan-A (×100).

PMID: 25834362

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
