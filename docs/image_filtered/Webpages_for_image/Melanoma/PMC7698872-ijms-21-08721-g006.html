Mechanistic Insights into Synergy between Melanin-Targeting Radioimmunotherapy and Immunotherapy in Experimental Melanoma.

H&E staining and IHC staining of tumors. Panel (a): First row, H&E staining of tumors from cold h8C3, ICB, and RIT 177Lu (low) + ICB treatment groups. Second row, anti-CD3 IHC staining under 400X magnification; brown staining surrounds the nuclear indicate the CD3+ cells. Bottom row, anti-Ki67 IHC staining under 100X magnification, and brown staining overlaps with the nuclear indicate the Ki67+ cells. Panel (b): upper row—number of CD3-positive cells in cold h8C3, ICB, and RIT + ICB treatment group; lower row—number of Ki67-positive cells in cold h8C3, ICB, and RIT + ICB treatment group.
