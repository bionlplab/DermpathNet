Tumor Necrosis Factor Receptor-1 (p55) Deficiency Attenuates Tumor Growth and Intratumoral Angiogenesis and Stimulates CD8(+) T Cell Function in Melanoma.

Melanin content is similar in tumors developed in wild type (WT) and TNFR1 KO mice. (A) Quantification of cellular melanin normalized to protein concentration. Results are shown as the mean ± SEM of 3 independent experiments and are standardized per mg tumor protein. (B) Brown-blackish melanin granules typically observed in hematoxylin-eosin (H&E) stained tumor tissue. Scale bar: 25 µm.
