Optimization of an automated tumor-infiltrating lymphocyte algorithm for improved prognostication in primary melanoma.

The NN192 classifier assists in the visualization and quantification of multiple cell types.(a-b) Two representative melanoma cases are shown, with the 20x H&E digitized slide on the left and the same image with the NN192 classifier overlay applied on the right. Classification of cells are as follows: red denotes tumor cells, purple indicates TIL, green is stroma, and yellow signifies other components, such as false detections or background.
