Local depigmentation as a sign of local recurrence of a histologic complete regressed malignant melanoma.

A, Histopathologic image of the melanoma recurrence. Mostly spindle-shaped melanocytic cells with prominent melanin pigmentation located in the dermis. B, Histopathologic image of the melanoma recurrence. Melanocytic cells with polymorphic nuclei and large eosinophilic nucleoli, located in the dermis.
