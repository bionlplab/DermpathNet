A Disguising Fast-Growing Metachronous Melanoma and COVID-19.

Nodular melanoma 1.47 mm, vertical growth phase.A densely cellular melanocytic tumor, consisting of round/oval cells and rare, elongated, bulky cells. Tumor cells present pleiomorphic, atypical, hyperchromatic nuclei, with rare mitoses and brown pigment within the cytoplasm. They are organized in nests at the dermo-epidermal junction or in islands within the dermis. Predominantly, lymphocytic infiltrates are present. Hematoxylin and eosin, original magnification: Ã—100.
