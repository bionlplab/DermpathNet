Giant congenital melanocytic nevus with proliferative nodules mimicking a congenital malignant melanoma.

(A) Histopathology of the proliferative nodule showed a proliferative dermal nodule in a giant congenital nevus (H&E, ×40). (B) Congenital nevus cells formed nests at the dermo-epidermal junction and upper dermis (H&E, ×200). (C) Melanocytic cells within the dermal nodule were more cellularlarger, more heavily pigmented and predominantly epithelioid cells, showing low-grade cytologic atypia and minimal pleomorphism (H&E, ×400). The immunohistochemical stains showed a positivity for (D) HMB45 (×100), (E) Melan A (×100), (F) SOX-10 (×100), and (G) Ki-67 (×100).

PMID: 25143702

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
