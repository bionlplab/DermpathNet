Oral malignant melanoma of the lower jaw-a case report with immunohistochemical investigations.

H & E stained sections of oral malignant melanoma. (a): A-4× view of the lesion shows the lesional tissue which is composed of malignant melanocytes infiltrating into the connective tissue stroma. (b): B-10× melanocytes are infiltrating into the connective tissue stroma in the form of sheets, chords. (c): C-40× melanocytes are round in shape, exhibit nuclei of varying sizes which occupies nearly the entire cell cytoplasm.

PMID: 37396106

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
