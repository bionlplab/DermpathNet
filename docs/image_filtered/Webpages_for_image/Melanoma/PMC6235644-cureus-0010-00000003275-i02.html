Neurocutaneous Melanosis with Leptomeningeal Melanoma Involving Supratentorium and Infratentorium.

(A) Hematoxylin and Eosin (H&E) stained sections show sheets of medium-sized polygonal cells with moderately pleomorphic nuclei containing dispersed chromatin, macronucleoli, and moderate amounts of eosinophilic cytoplasm. Few cells contain intracytoplasmic melanin pigment (X400). (B) H&E-stained sections show tumor as described in A with an area of necrosis (X400). (C) H&E-stained sections show tumor as described in A (X200). (D) Immunoperoxidase staining for Melan A showing diffuse cytoplasmic staining in tumor cells (X200).

PMID: 30443446

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Melanocytic
