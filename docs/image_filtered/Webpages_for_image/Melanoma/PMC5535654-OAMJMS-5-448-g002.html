Interdigital Melanoma of the Foot Affecting Two Neighbouring Interdigital Spaces - Second Case Report.

Histopathology of amelanotic acrolentiginous melanoma. (a) Overview and (b) detail of an amelanotic epitheloid cell proliferation in the mid dermis and increased number of melanocytic cells in the basal cell layer of the epidermis (Hematoxylin-eosin x 2 and x 4, respectively). Immunoperoxidase stain for MART-1 demonstrating numerous positive tumour cells (x4)
