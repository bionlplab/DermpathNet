Extranodal NK/T Cell Lymphoma Causing Cardiorespiratory Failure.

Microscopic appearance of lymphoma infiltrate in the heart. (a) Hematoxylin and eosin stain shows lymphoid cells infiltrating the myocardium (×100). (b) Medium sized atypical lymphoid cells with abundant pale cytoplasm, irregular nuclear contour, granular chromatin, and inconspicuous nucleoli (×400). (c) Lymphoma cells are immunohistochemically positive for CD3 with cytoplasmic staining pattern (×400). (d) Lymphoma cells are immunohistochemically positive for granzyme B (×400).

PMID: 27493813

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Hematolymphoid
