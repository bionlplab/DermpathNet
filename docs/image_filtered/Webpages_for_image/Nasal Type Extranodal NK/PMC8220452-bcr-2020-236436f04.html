Extranodal NK/T cell lymphoma, nasal type: a rare diagnosis with common nasal presentation.

Immunohistochemistry and in situ hybridisation (ISH) staining pattern of extranodal NK/T cell lymphoma. The malignant lymphoid cells express: (A) CD3 (CD3×400). (B) CD7 (CD7×400). (C) TIA-1 (TIA-1×400). (D) B cell marker CD20 was negative (CD20×400). (E) Ki67 proliferative index was high, approximately 30% (Ki67×400). (F) Evidence of EBV-encoded small nuclear RNA-1 (EBER-1) (ISH x400). EBER, Epstein-Barr encoding region; EBV, Epstein-Barr virus.

PMID: 34158320

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Hematolymphoid
