Cutaneous Involvement of Extranodal NK/T Cell Lymphoma, Nasal Type, a Clinical and Histopathological Mimicker of Various Skin Diseases.

Interface dermatitis pattern: (A) multiple purplish plaques on right leg; (B) superficial and deep perivascular infiltration by small lymphocytes with overlying epidermal hyperkeratosis (H&E, original magnification ×100); (C) perieccrine infiltration by atypical small-to-medium lymphocytes (H&E, original magnification ×400); (D) basal vacuolar change and few necrotic keratinocytes (H&E, original magnification ×400); (E) presence of epidermotropism; (F) presence of folliculotropism (H&E, original magnification ×400).

PMID: 36135102

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Hematolymphoid
