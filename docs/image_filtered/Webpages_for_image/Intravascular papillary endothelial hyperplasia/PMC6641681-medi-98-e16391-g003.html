Intravascular papillary endothelial hyperplasia occurring in the foot: A case report and review of literature.

(A) A dilated vein with intraluminal organizing thrombus is seen. (H&E, ×12.5). (B–D) A detailed view of organizing thrombus shows papillary processes and anastomosing channels at the central portion. (H&E, (B): ×40, (C): ×100, (D): ×200). (E and F) Papillary processes are lined with benign endothelial cells without cellular atypia or mitotic figures. (H&E, (E) and (F): ×400).

PMID: 31305445

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
