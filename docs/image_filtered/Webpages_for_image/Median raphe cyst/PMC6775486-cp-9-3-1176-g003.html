Median raphe cyst: A clinically challenging diagnosis.

A) Transitional epithelium mixed with goblet cells and a prominent neutrophilic infiltration (H&E stain, ×200). B) Squamous epithelium lining with a prominent neutrophilic infiltration (H&E stain, ×200). C) Mucin producing goblet cells are highlighted in pink (Mucicarmine stain, ×400). D) Alcian blue/Periodic Acid-Shiff (PAS) also demonstrates the presence of goblet cells (Alcian Blue/PAS stain, ×400).

PMID: 31583066

Type: Benign, Category: Cystic
