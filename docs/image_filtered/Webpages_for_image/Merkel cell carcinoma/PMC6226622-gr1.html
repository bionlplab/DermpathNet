Neuroendocrine Key Regulator Gene Expression in Merkel Cell Carcinoma.

Immunohistochemistry staining of REST, ASCL1 and NeuroD1 in MCC ID 25.A, IHC reveals no expression of REST expression in the MCC tissue. In B, double staining of CK20 (red) and REST (brown) confirms that the REST positive cells are not located in the MCC tissue. ID25 is negative for ASCL1 and positive for NeuroD1 as shown in C and D. The microphotographs were taken at 20x magnification.

PMID: 30414538

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
