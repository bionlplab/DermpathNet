Merkel cell carcinoma metastatic to cervical lymph node in a patient with rheumatoid arthritis: a case report.

Histology of the resected cervical lymph node biopsy mass.Notes: (A, B) H&E staining found diffuse proliferation of atypical and pleomorphic cells (original magnification: A ×40; B ×200). (C–G) Immunohistochemical analysis found that the resected mass was positive for (C) CK20, (D) Syn, (E) CgA, (F) CD56 and (G) Ki67 (original magnification ×400).

PMID: 30863106

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
