Lambert-Eaton Myasthenic Syndrome and Paraneoplastic Cerebellar Degeneration Associated with Merkel Cell Carcinoma with Unknown Primary: A Case Report.

Histological appearance of the lymph node with positive immunohistochemical reactions with: (A) anti-synaptophysin antibodies (10x) and (B) cytokeratin 20 (20x). (C) Presence of numerous mitoses (15/10 high-power field) and areas of necrosis was reported (hematoxylin and eosin stain, 20x).
