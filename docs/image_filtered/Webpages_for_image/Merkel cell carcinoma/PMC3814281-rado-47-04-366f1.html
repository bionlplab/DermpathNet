Electrochemotherapy as a new therapeutic strategy in advanced Merkel cell carcinoma of head and neck region.

Tumour biopsy showing poorly differentiated cells with scarce cytoplasm and vesicular nuclei with inconspicuous nucleoli. High mitotic index and apoptotic figures were present. Immunohistochemical reactions were positive for cytokeratin 8 and 20 and neuroendocrine markers (chromogranin, synaptophysin and CD56/NCAM); TTF-1 was not expressed (H&E stain, 40Ã—).
