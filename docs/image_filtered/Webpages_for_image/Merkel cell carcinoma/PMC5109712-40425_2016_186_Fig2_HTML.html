Metastatic Merkel cell carcinoma response to nivolumab.

PD-1 and PD-L1 expression in Merkel cell carcinoma primary tumor from the right upper scapula. Legend: a, b: PD-1 shows patchy expression in tumor-associated lymphocytes. c, d: There is no significant PD-L1 expression in tumor cells.
