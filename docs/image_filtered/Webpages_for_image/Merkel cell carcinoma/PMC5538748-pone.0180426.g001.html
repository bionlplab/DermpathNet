Expression of Merkelcell polyomavirus (MCPyV) large T-antigen in Merkel cell carcinoma lymph node metastases predicts poor outcome.

Immunohistochemistry of the MCPyV large T-antigen (LTA) shows that the antigen is predominantly expressed in the nuclei of Merkel cell carcinoma cells.Panel (A) demonstrates no expression of the primary tumor whereas in panel (B) a positive expression of MCPyV LTA can be appreciated. In Panel (C) one can observe no LTA expression compared to positive expression in lymph node metastases (D). All photomicrographs are taken at x100 magnification.
