Burden of Disease, Early Diagnosis, and Treatment of Merkel Cell Carcinoma in Latin America.

Pathology of Merkel cell carcinoma: proliferation of uniform, small, round, blue undifferentiated cells with spherical or oval nuclei and scant cytoplasm. (Left) Sheets of small cells extending throughout the dermis (hematoxylin and eosin [H&E]). (Right) Small, round, blue undifferentiated cells at a higher magnification (H&E).
