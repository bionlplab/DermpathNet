Case of Merkel cell carcinoma in a patient with pre-existing ILD.

Right upper lobe wedge resection, July 2017. Usual interstitial pneumonia histology. (A–C) H&E stain (800, 300 and 300 µm). Fibrosing and minimally cellular interstitial pneumonitis. (D) Movat stain (300 µm) highlights the fibrosis (yellow) and fibroblastic foci (green).
