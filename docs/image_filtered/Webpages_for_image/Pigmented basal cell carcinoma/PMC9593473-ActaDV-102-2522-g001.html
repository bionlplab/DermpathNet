Capillaroscopy in Pigmented Basal Cell Carcinoma Smaller than 3-mm Diameter: A Report of Four Lesions.

Clinical, dermoscpoic and capillaroscopic features of (a–c) case 1 and (d–f) 2. (a) A 3×2-mm brownish nodule on the left cheek. (b) Dermoscopy showing brown macules, in-focus blue-grey dots (white arrowhead) with scales, and no vascular structures (polarized, magnification ×10). (c) Capillaroscopy showing homogeneous black or blue-whitish pigmentation and black globules (white arrowhead), arborizing vessels (white arrow) (magnification ×145). (d) A 2×1-mm nodule on the left cheek. (e) Dermoscopy showing homogeneous black-brownish pigmentation (polarized, magnification ×19). (f) Capillaroscopy showing tortuous and looped vessels (white arrow) in the pigmentation (magnification ×590).

PMID: 36000995

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
