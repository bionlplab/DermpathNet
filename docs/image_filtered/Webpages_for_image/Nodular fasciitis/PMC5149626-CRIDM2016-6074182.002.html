Nodular Fasciitis Complicating a Staged Surgical Excision of Dermatofibrosarcoma Protuberans.

(a) 20x magnification of original punch biopsy specimen: a diffuse dermal proliferation of spindle cells extending into the subcutaneous fat. (b) 200x magnification of original biopsy specimen: a hypercellular dermal proliferation of spindle cells in a storiform pattern. Infiltration of fat in a “honeycomb” pattern is noted. (c) 20x magnification of original biopsy specimen: spindle cells stain diffusely positive for CD34.

PMID: 28018683

Type: Benign, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
