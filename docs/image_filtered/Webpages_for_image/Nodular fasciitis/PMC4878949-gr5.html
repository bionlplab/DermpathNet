Nodular fasciitis mimicking recurrent lymphoma on positron emission tomography-computed tomography.

Cores in formalin at 10X reveals myofibroblasts, myxoid matrix, and loosely organized myofibroblasts. The spindle cells are positive for the smooth muscle actin and negative for cytokeratin, S100, Desmin, epithelial membrane antigen.

PMID: 27257463

Type: Benign, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
