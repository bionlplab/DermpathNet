Histomorphological evaluation, cell proliferation and endothelial immunostaining in oral and maxillofacial myofibroblastic lesions.

Ki67 immunostaining in MFL. a) Nodular fasciitis. Fusiform cell population with low reactivity. b) Myofibroma. Positively reacting MF scattered around immature-looking vessels, c) Desmoplastic fibroma. A MF with positive reaction, indicating the low proliferative capacity of this lesion, d) Inflammatory myofibroblastic tumor. Numerous reactive MF (it was avoided to count endothelial cells (arrow)) with positive reaction to Ki67, e) Myofibroblastic sarcoma. Large population of MF with positive reaction dispersed over a myxoid matrix (Ki67 400x).

PMID: 36243994

Type: Benign, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
