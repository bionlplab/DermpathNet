Concomitant spinal dural arteriovenous fistula and nodular fasciitis in an adolescent: case report.

The arteriovenous shunt was identified around the extradural nodular fasciitis (A), which was composed of fibrohistiocytic tissue with spindle cells (B, H&E) and positive on smooth muscle actin stain (C)
