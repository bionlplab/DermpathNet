Nodular fasciitis with cortical erosion of the hand.

A high-power view shows plump, randomly oriented spindle cells surrounded by myxoid stroma. Although cellularity of the lesion was high, hyperchromatism and variation in the size and shape of the nuclei were mild. Mitotic activity ranged from 5 to 10 mitotic figures per 10 high-power fields, with no atypical forms identified (H&E, Ã— 200).
