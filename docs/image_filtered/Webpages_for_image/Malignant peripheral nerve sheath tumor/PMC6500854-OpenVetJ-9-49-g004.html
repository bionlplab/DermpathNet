Central nervous system metastasis of an intradural malignant peripheral nerve sheath tumor in a dog.

The T2 spinal cord segment. The neoplastic cells infiltrate the nervous tissue from the leptomeninges through perivascular spaces, producing a perivascular whirl pattern (×10; H&E) (Bar: 120 μm).
