Sarcomas of the mediastinum with epithelioid morphology.

Malignant peripheral nerve sheath tumor with divergent glandular differentiation. (A) The tumor has a dual population of spindled and epithelioid cells (10× magnification, H&E); (B) higher magnification shows distinct nests of cuboidal epithelioid cells (20× magnification, H&E).

PMID: 35118310

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
