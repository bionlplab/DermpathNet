Primary malignant peripheral nerve sheath tumor of prostate in a young adult: A case report.

Microscopic findings of the prostatectomy specimen. (A) The tumor was composed of fascicles of spindle cells showing alternating hypercellular and hypocellular areas, and heterologous cartilaginous differentiation (center) (H&E, ×40). (B) Malignant spindle cells showed hyperchromatic nuclei and numerous mitoses (H&E, ×200). (C) Immunohistochemistry showed focal staining for S100 protein (×200).

PMID: 30278486

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
