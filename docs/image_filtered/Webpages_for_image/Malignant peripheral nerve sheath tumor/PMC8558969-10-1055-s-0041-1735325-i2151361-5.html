Cranial and Spinal Malignant Peripheral Nerve Sheath Tumor: A Pathological Enigma.

Histopathology of malignant peripheral nerve sheath tumor (images magnified at 100X). ( A ) Neoplastic spindle cells arranged in a fasciculated pattern. ( B ) Numerous thickened blood vessels in the stroma with a myxoid change. Insert shows cells exhibited high MiB-1 labeling. ( C ) The tumor cells exhibited patchy S-100 staining. ( D ) Loss of H3k27me3 in neoplastic cells and retained staining in blood vessels.

PMID: 34737514

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
