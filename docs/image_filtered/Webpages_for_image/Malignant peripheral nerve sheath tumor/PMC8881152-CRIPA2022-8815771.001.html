Epithelioid Malignant Peripheral Nerve Sheath Tumor in the Liver: Report of a Rare Unusual Case with Rhabdoid Morphology and Review of the Literature.

(a, b) Epithelioid malignant peripheral nerve sheath tumor demonstrating nests and cord-like architecture set in a myxoid background (H&E, 200x). (c, d) The individual neoplastic cells exhibit a rhabdoid morphology with eccentric nuclei and conspicuous nucleoli (H&E, 400x).
