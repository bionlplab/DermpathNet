Multiple large cysts arising from nevus comedonicus.

Histopathologic findings of cyst tissue(A) Histopathologic findings of cysts showed lamellated keratin content was washed out during H&E preparation (H&E, ×100). (B) Histophathologic findings of typical nevus comedonicus showed wide dilation and deep invagination with laminated keratin plugs and perifollicular hyperkeratosis, acanthosis, and papillomatosis (H&E, ×40).

PMID: 22783495

Type: Benign, Category: Keratinocytic
