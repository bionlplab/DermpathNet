An intravascular papillary endothelial hyperplasia of the hand radiologically mimicking a hemangiopericytoma: A case report and literature review.

(a) H&E 20x: the section shows a nodular, pseudoencapsulated lesion composed of papillary formations with hyaline or fibrous bundles and (b and c) H&E 100x, H&E 500x: at high magnification, it is possible to observe some vascular formations, with a labyrinthic structure, lined by plump endothelial cells. The underlying fibrous stroma appears oedematous; necrosis, cellular atypias, or atypical mitotic cannot be detected.

PMID: 29348916

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
