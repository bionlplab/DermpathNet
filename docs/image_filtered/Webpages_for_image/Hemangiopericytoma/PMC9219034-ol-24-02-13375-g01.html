Delayed pulmonary metastasis and recurrence of intracranial malignant solitary fibrous tumor/hemangiopericytoma: Case report and literature review.

Hematoxylin and eosin staining of different regions of the same solitary fibrous tumor/hemangiopericytoma specimen. (A) An abundance of blood vessels was observed in tumor tissue, with a typical ‘staghorn’ vascularization and a large number of fusiform tumor cells arranged in bundles between blood vessels (magnification, ×200). (B) HE staining indicated that sparse areas and dense areas coexisted (magnification, ×100). (C) Mitotic bodies were visible (black arrow; ≥5 mitoses per 10 high-power fields) (magnification, ×400).

PMID: 35765276

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
