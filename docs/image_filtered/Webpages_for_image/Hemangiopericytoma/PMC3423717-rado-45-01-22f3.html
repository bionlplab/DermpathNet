Pelvic hemangiopericytoma: the role of diffusion weighted imaging in targeting the biopsy site and in monitoring the tumour response to radiotherapy.

(A) CT guided biopsy of the lesion with an 18G true cut needle. (B) Power view (H&E stain, X100) of the biopsy, showing a mesenchymal tumour with a hemangiopericytoma-like pattern, composed of spindle cells arranged in short bundles and the characteristic vascular pattern with a partial “staghorn” configuration. (C) High power view of the spindle cell areas (H&A, X400). The spindle cells show no atypia, mitosis or necrosis.

PMID: 22933930

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
