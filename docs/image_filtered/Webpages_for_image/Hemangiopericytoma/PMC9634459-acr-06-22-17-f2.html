Superficial solitary fibrous tumor masquerading as a dermoid cyst: a case report.

Solitary fibrous tumor: histologic findings. Dermal neoplasm consisting of spindle cells arranged in a “patternless” pattern featuring hemangiopericytoma-like vessels (arrows) dispersed throughout shown at both low (A,B) and high power (C) (hematoxylin and eosin, H&E, ×2.5, ×10, and ×20). The spindle cell population exhibits diffuse nuclear STAT6 positivity (D) (STAT6 immunohistochemistry ×20). Note that the vascular endothelial cells do not stain positive for STAT6 (D). STAT6, signal transducer and activator of transcription 6.

PMID: 36339907

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
