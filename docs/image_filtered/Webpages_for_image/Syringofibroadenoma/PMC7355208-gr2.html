Recalcitrant erosive plaques on the palms and soles: A rare manifestation of eccrine syringofibroadenoma.

Biopsy of lesion on patient's lower leg shows multiple thin, branching strands of epithelium with ductal lumina consistent with eccrine ducts (arrows) extending from the epidermis into the dermis. The background inflammatory infiltrate includes plasma cells (arrowheads). (A and B, Hematoxylin-eosin stain; original magnifications: A, ×100; B, ×400.)

PMID: 32685646

Type: Benign, Category: Adnexal
