Perianal Eccrine Syringofibroadenoma.

(a) Thin anastomosing strands of uniform, small epithelial cells arising from the epidermis to the dermis were observed (H and E, ×10). (b) The cells were embedded in a cellular fibrous stroma and exhibited luminal structures within the lattice network (H and E, ×40). (c) Second biopsy shows suppurative neutrophilic dense inflammation in the upper and deep dermis (H and E, ×10). (d) Neutrophils in the dermal infiltrate (H and E, ×40)

PMID: 28979031

Type: Benign, Category: Adnexal
