The Rapidly Expanding Group of RB1-Deleted Soft Tissue Tumors: An Updated Review.

Atypical spindle cell/pleomorphic lipomatous tumor. (a) Strong, diffuse, cytoplasmatic CD34 expression. (b,c) Loss of nuclear Rb expression in the adipocytes and spindle cells, and intact nuclear Rb expression in the endothelial cells (red arrows) and macrophage (black arrow).
