Dermoscopy of Primary Cutaneous B-cell Lymphoma.

Histopathology of primary cutaneous B-cell lymphoma, follicle cell type. (a) Dense, diffuse lymphocytic infiltrate involving the dermis (hematoxylin-eosin, original magnification ×10). (b) The infiltrate was strongly positive for CD-20 immunostain (original magnification ×10). Bcl-2 and Bcl-6 immunostains were also positive (not shown)

PMID: 30984608

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Hematolymphoid
