Spindle cell lipoma of the floor of mouth.-DermPath

Spindle cell lipoma of the floor of mouth.

Histopathological findings. (A) H-E staining with weak enlargement (×40). Mature adipocytes proliferation and spindle-shaped cells against the background of “rope-like” collagen fibers (arrow) are observed. (B) Immunohistochemical staining of CD34 with strong enlargement (×100). Spindle-shaped cells are CD34 positive.

PMID: 36873044

Type: Benign, Category: Adipocytic
