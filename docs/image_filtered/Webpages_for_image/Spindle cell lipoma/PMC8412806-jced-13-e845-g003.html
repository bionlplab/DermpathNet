Large intraoral spindle cell lipoma.

Spindle cell lipoma. Five µm H&E stained histological section showing a detail of the spindle cells with elongated nuclei and bipolar cytoplasm (A – original magnification 400x). Immunohistochemical expression of CD34 in the spindle cells (B), mast cell tryptase in mast cells (C), and S100 protein in mature adipose cells (D) (B, C and D - Immunoperoxidase, original magnification 400x).

PMID: 34512925

Type: Benign, Category: Adipocytic
