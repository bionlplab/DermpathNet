Intramuscular spindle cell lipoma of the deltoid: a case report.

Formalin-fixed, paraffin-embedded specimens were stained with hematoxylin and eosin for analysis. The tumor consisted of spindle cells, collagen fibers and lipocytes. Original magnification, ×100 (a) and ×400 (b).

PMID: 25880875

Type: Benign, Category: Adipocytic
