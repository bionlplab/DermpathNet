Pelvic neurofibroma in a patient presenting with pelvic pain and urinary frequency: A case report.

Histopathology with hematoxylin and eosin (H&E) staining of the lesion (A: 20× and B: 40×) of the lesion: the hypocellular proliferation of nerve elements including Schwann cells with interposed collagen bundles. These cells are spindle in shape with wavy nuclei and pointed ends.

PMID: 37153482

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
