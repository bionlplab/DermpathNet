Solitary neurofibroma of eyelid masquerading as chalazion.

The histopathologic and histochemical studies of the solitary neurofibroma.Notes: (A) A well-circumscribed but unencapsulated collection of spindle cells within the eyelid dermis. Small bland wavy nuclei and small vascular channels are set within a myxoid background (H&E stain, ×100). (B) Diffuse immune-reactive areas were revealed when stained with additional immunohistochemistry staining S100, which indicates the presence of neural cells (×100).

PMID: 28579839

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
