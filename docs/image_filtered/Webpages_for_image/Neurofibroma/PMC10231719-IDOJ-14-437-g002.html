Diffuse Neurofibroma Presenting as Unilateral Facial Plaques with Overlying Hair Loss.

(a) Ill-defined spindle cell proliferation with a loose myxoid stroma in the mid- and deep dermis (40x). (b) Spindle cell proliferation encasing the hair follicles and arrector pili muscle (100x). (c) The nuclei of the spindle cells are short and wavy (200x). (d) Spindle cells showing positive immunohistochemistry for S-100 (200x)

PMID: 37266099

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
