Solitary testicular neurofibromatosis with testicular abscess: a case report.

Histopathological evaluation of the specimen. (A) The appearance of neural tissue in the testicular tumor made up of spindle cells (arrow) (HE, ×100); (B) tumor cells arranged in bundles, the individual cells are uniform in size and not aggressive, with no cells in mitotic phase (HE, ×200).
