A case of multiple solitary neurofibromas located in the hypopharynx and cervical esophagus.

Pathological findings of an endoscopic biopsy specimen obtained from a hypopharyngeal lesion 3 years after the first examination. Submucosal tumors without capsule formation are composed of spindle cells under hematoxylin–eosin (HE) staining, and they are positive for immunohistochemical staining with S‐100. (a) HE staining: ×40, (b) HE staining: ×100, and (c) S‐100 staining: ×40

PMID: 36036020

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
