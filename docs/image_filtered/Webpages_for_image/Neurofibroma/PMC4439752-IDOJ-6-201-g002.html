Giant cell tumor of the tendon sheath mimicking a plexiform neurofibroma.

(a) Histopathological examination from the lobulated mass (H and E, × 40) revealed a partially circumscribed tumor consisting of diffuse infiltration of mononuclear cells, epithelioid cells in a dense collagenous stroma. (b) Higher magnification (H and E, × 100) revealed diffuse infiltration of stroma with mononuclear cells, epithelioid cells and osteoclast like giant cells in a dense collagenous stroma with few mitotic figures

PMID: 26009718

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
