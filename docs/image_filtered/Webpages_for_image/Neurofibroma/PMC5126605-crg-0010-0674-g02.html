Neurofibroma of the Colon: A Diagnostic Mimicker of Gastrointestinal Stromal Tumor.

The tumor had its epicenter in the submucosa (a, HE, ×40) and was composed mainly of wavy spindle cells (b, HE, ×100). On immunohistochemistry, the tumor cells were positive for CD34 (c, ×40; please see the internal positive staining in the capillaries located in the mucosa and the internal negative staining in the smooth muscle fibers of the muscularis mucosa) and S-100 protein (d, ×40; please see the internal negative staining in the muscularis mucosa).

PMID: 27920660

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
