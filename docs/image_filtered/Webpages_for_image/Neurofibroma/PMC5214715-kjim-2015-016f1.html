Minimal change nephrotic syndrome showing complete remission after resection of a neurofibroma in a type I neurofibromatosis patient.

(A) Kidney light microscopy displays no abnormalities (H&E, ×200). (B) Electronic microscopy shows diffuse foot process effacement (arrow) without electron deposits (×4,000).
