Huge Steatocystoma Multiplex with New Point Mutation in the Exon 1 of KRT 17 Gene.

(A) A cyst covered by stratified squamous cells with adjacent sebaceous glands (H&E, ×100). (B) Immunohistochemistry was positive on the cyst wall and sebaceous glands (CK-17, ×200). (C) Missense mutation in exon 1 of KRT 17 gene (c. 425G>T).

PMID: 33911497

Type: Benign, Category: Cystic
