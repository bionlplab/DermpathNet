Former mucinous bronchioloalveolar carcinoma revisited.

(a) Mucinous adenocarcinoma with lepidic growth pattern (white arrows). Two foci of invasion are seen close to a vessel (black arrow) and around a bronchiole (arrow head). Mucin is present in the alveoli (H&E ×20). (b) The photograph depicts lepidic pattern (black arrows). Invasion of the peribronchiolar tissue is also evident at the center (between white arrows) (H&E ×100).

PMID: 24369471

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Adnexal
