Research on the Histological Features and Pathological Types of Gastric Adenocarcinoma With Mucinous Differentiation.

Signet ring cell type mucinous carcinoma. (A) It was composed of signet ring cell carcinoma cells and mucinous tissues. The cells accounted for 20-80% of the tumor; H&E, × 100. (B) The tumor's primary invasion feature was to grow diffusely, jumpily, and invasively and then invade the spaces among the surrounding tissues, lymphatic vessels, and blood vessels to destroy the tissues; H&E, × 100. (C) CKpan was positively expressed in the cancer cells; EnVision method™, × 400. (D) DX2 was positively expressed; EnVision method™, × 400.

PMID: 35308509

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Adnexal
