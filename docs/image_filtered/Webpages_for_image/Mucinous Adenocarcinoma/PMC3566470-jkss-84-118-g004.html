Two cases of mucinous adenocarcinoma of the stomach mistaken as submucosal tumor.

(A) The mucosal surface is slightly red in comparison with the surrounding gastric mucosa but no erosion is evident. (B) On cross section, the tumor was found to have infiltrated subserosa. The tumor had a brownish tan color and necrotic change was observed in its center.
