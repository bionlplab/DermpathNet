Mucinous Adenocarcinoma of Gallbladder Presenting As Acute Calculous Cholecystitis.

Microscopic examination of the gallbladder showed dysplastic glands (arrow) floating in the mucin pool (HE ×100) (A). The gallbladder mucosa showed dysplastic changes in the epithelium (HE ×400) (B). The tumor was seen infiltrating the peri-muscular connective tissue on the hepatic side (arrow) (HE ×100) (C).HE, hematoxylin and eosin

PMID: 34017663

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Adnexal
