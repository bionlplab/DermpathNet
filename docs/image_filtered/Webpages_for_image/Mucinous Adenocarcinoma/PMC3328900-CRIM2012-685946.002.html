Primary mucinous adenocarcinoma of the testis.

Microscopic findings (hematoxylin and eosin) showing mucinous adenocarcinoma. Single or multiple layers of columnar epithelium with mucin are visible. In some portions, papillary growth was also observed. (a) 40x; (b) 200x.

PMID: 22550498

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Adnexal
