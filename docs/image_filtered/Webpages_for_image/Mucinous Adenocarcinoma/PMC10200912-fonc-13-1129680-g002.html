Primary renal mucinous adenocarcinoma masquerading as a giant renal cyst: a case report.

Pathological features of the lesion. (A) Massive mucus was found in the fibrocyst wall, and the tumor cells were scattered or glandular in arrangement (HE, ×100). (B) A few mucinous adenocarcinoma cells with multiple mucinous foci and scattered calcifications were found in the cystic wall (HE, ×100). (C) Strong positivity for PCK (×100). (D) Strong positivity for Villin (×100). (E) Strong positivity for CDX2 (×100). (F) Partial positivity for SATB2 (×100). (G) Weak positivity for Syn (×100). (H) Fifty percent positivity for Ki67 (×100).

PMID: 37223683

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Adnexal
