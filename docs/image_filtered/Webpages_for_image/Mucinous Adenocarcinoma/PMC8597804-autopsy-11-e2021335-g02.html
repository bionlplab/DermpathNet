Mucinous adenocarcinoma of the appendix with inguinal node metastases.

Photomicrographs of the ileocecal appendix. Area of invasive adenocarcinoma, with desmoplastic stroma and focal presence of extracellular mucus (inferior right corner) - H&E stain 10x (A). Appendicular wall, with neoplastic glandular structures in the subserosa (superior left corner) with a lake of mucus accompanied by rare neoplastic cell clusters (inferior left corner) - H&E stain lower magnification (B). Cross-section of the appendicular surgical top - H&E stain (C). Detail of the mucosa of the appendicular surgical top, where low-grade dysplasia lesions are observed - H&E stain 20x (D).

PMID: 34805006

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Adnexal
