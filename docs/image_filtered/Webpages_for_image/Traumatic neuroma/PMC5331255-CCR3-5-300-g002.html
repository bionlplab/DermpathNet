Orbital rhabdomyosarcoma and traumatic neuroma following enucleation for a uveal schwannoma in a dog: a case report.

Histopathology OD, schwannoma of blue‐eyed dogs. (A) Subgross image revealing a basophilic mass expanding and distorting the iris leaflets, ciliary body, and iridocorneal angle. H&E. Bar = 1 mm (B) Photomicrograph, inset from (A), showing fusiform neoplastic cells with scant cytoplasm, forming variably dense, short interlacing bundles. H&E. Bar = 100 μm. (C) Photomicrograph showing varying degrees of positive cytoplasmic staining for GFAP. Bar = 100 μm.

PMID: 28265395

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
