Preventive Effect of Local Lidocaine Administration on the Formation of Traumatic Neuroma.

H&E staining results in the lidocaine and saline control groups at 4 weeks and 8 weeks after neurectomy. Traumatic neuroma formed in the saline control group (A,C). Observed in the lidocaine group was no obvious neuroma (B,D). Black arrows showed the position of the magnified picture.

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
