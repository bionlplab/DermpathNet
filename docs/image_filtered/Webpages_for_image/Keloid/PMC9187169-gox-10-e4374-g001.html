Hemodynamics and Vascular Histology of Keloid Tissues and Anatomy of Nearby Blood Vessels.

NIRS-determined rSO2 and T-HbI values of keloids and their surrounding healthy skin NIRS were conducted at 1- to 2-cm intervals at 10–20 sites on both the keloid and the adjacent skin rSO2, and T-HbI were measured three times. The average values at each site were expressed as % maximal rSO2/T-HbI × 100. A representative example is shown.
