Solitary fibrous tumor of the orbital region: report of a case with emphasis on the diagnostic utility of STAT-6.

Histological examination. (A) Low magnification showing an uniformly hypercellular tumor with pushing borders, circumscribed by a fibrous pseudo-capsule (H&E staining; 50x magnification). (B) Higher magnification showing moderately eosinophilic bland-looking spindle cells arranged in intersecting short fascicles with interspersed stellate-shaped, keloid-type collagen fibers (H&E staining; 100x magnification); (C,D) Neoplastic cells are positively stained with CD34 (C) and STAT-6 (D) (immunoperoxidase staining; 100x magnification).

PMID: 33393522

Type: Benign, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
