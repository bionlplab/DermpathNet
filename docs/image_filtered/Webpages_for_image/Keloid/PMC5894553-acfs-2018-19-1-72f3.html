Rare experience of keloidal dermatofibroma of forehead.

(A) A keloid-like dermatofibroma with atrophic epidermis in low power field (H&E, ×40). (B) A circumscribed, keloid-like area with irregularly oriented, thick, brightly eosinophilic collagen fibers (H&E, ×100).
