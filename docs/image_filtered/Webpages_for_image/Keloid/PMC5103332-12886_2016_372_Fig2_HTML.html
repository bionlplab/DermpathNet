Corneal keloid: four case reports of clinicopathological features and surgical outcome.

Hematoxylin-eosin staining of the excised keloid sections. a Case 1. The epithelium is hyperplastic and undulating, and Bowman’s layer is totally disrupted. The stromal collagen bundles are increased and irregularly-arranged in a whorl-like pattern. Original magnification × 100. b Case 2. A marked epithelial hyperplasia and focal disruption of Bowman’s layer are noted. Original magnification × 200. c Case 4. Thickened epithelial layer, increased stromal collagen, and numerous accumulation of prominent fibroblasts are observed. Original magnification × 100
