Multi-stage surgery combined with radiotherapy for treatment of giant anterior chest wall keloid: A case report.

A, A large number of coarse and uniform eosinophilic red-stained collagen bundles are visible in the dermis, which are disorderly arranged. The collagen bundles are rich in mucin. The fibroblasts are active in the periphery of the lesions, the central distribution is reduced, the skin appendages are atrophied, and the vascular hyperplasia is in the dermis [hematoxylin-eosin (H&E), 40]. B, Inflammatory cell infiltration is observed around the blood vessels [H&E, 100].

PMID: 31977896

Type: Benign, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
