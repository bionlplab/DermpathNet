Primary vaginal adenocarcinoma of intestinal type or occult metastatic colon cancer: a diagnostic dilemma from a vaginal skin tag.

Immunohistochemistry demonstrated on vaginal biopsy.Notes: This combination of stains is highly suggestive of a malignancy of gastrointestinal origin. (A) Moderately differentiated adenocarcinoma on H&E staining at 200× magnification. (B) Positive stain for CK 20 at 200× magnification. (C) Positive stain for CEA at 400× magnification. (D) Positive stain for CDX 2 at 200× magnification.Abbreviations: CEA, carcinoembryonic antigen; CDX, caudal-related homeobox 2; CK, cytokeratin.

PMID: 31015769

Type: Benign, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
