Adult T-cell lymphoma/leukemia presenting as isolated central nervous system T-cell lymphoma.

Microscopic findings of CNS T-cell lymphoma. Hematoxylin and eosin stain, 1000x. The lesions showed tissue necrosis and perivascular infiltration of atypical, medium-sized lymphoid cells (a). The lymphoid cells had strong membrane staining for CD4 (b), CD5 (c), focal positive for CD3 (d), and negative for CD20 (e).

PMID: 25587470

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Hematolymphoid
