Epidermal Inclusion Cysts following Dupuytren's Percutaneous Needle Fasciotomy: A Case Report.

Low power view showing the first epidermal inclusion cyst line by stratified squamous epithelium with granular layer, containing lamellated keratinous material (short arrow). In the vicinity, there are hypocellular nodules of uniform elongated spindled fibroblasts in long sweeping fascicles embedded within a densely collagenous stroma consistent with Dupuytren’s contracture (long arrows) [H&E ×20].

PMID: 34367852

Type: Benign, Category: Cystic
