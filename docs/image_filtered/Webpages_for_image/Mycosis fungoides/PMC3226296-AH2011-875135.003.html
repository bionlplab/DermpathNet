Expression of thymidine phosphorylase in lymph nodes involved with mycosis fungoides and sézary syndrome.

Immunohistochemical stain of thymidine phosphorylase (TP) expression in a gastrointestinal biopsy specimen from a patient with enteropathy-type T-cell lymphoma. (a) Hematoxylin and eosin stain. (b) TP stain highlights neoplastic T cells (arrow in inset; 400x). Images are from an Olympus BX41 microscope (Olympus Corp., Tokyo, Japan) processed with Qcapture Pro 5.1 (QImaging, Surrey, BC, Canada).
