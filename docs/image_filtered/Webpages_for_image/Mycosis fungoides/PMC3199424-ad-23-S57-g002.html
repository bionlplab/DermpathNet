The three dimensional conformal radiotherapy for hyperkeratotic plantar mycosis fungoides.-DermPath

The three dimensional conformal radiotherapy for hyperkeratotic plantar mycosis fungoides.

(A) A low magnification view shows compact hyperkaratois, acanthosis, and exocytosis with intraepidermal collection of mononuclear cells. The hyperkeratotic horny layer also shows many nuclear remnants (H&E, ×100). (B) The higher magnification of intraepidermal collections of mononuclear cells showed atypical hyperchromatic nuclei of cells compatible with Pautrier's microabscesses (H&E, ×400). (C) Papillary dermal and intraepidermal exocytic cells were CD4 positive (immunoperoxidase, ×100).

PMID: 22028574

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Hematolymphoid
