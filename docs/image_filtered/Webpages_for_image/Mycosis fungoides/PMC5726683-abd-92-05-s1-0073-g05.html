Folliculotropic mycosis fungoides: challenging clinical, histopathological and immunohistochemical diagnosis.

Predominance of CD4+ population (Immunohistochemistry, X40), mainly of large cells; CD30+ (Immunohistochemistry x100) of large cells with more atypia around vessels and appendages; CD8+ (Immunohistochemistry, X40) in some lymphocytes; CD3+ e CD5+ (Immunohistochemistry, X100) in most lymphoid cells; Ki-67+ (MIB1) in about 50% of nuclei (Immunohistochemistry, X400)

PMID: 29267452

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Hematolymphoid
