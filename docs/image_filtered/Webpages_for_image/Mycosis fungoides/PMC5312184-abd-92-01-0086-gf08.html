CD30-positive cutaneous lymphoma: report of four cases with an emphasis on clinicopathological correlations.

Large-cell transformation of mycosis fungoides. A) Dermis showing band-like infiltration in the papillary portion and around the skin appendages in depth (Hematoxylin & eosin x20). B) Dermis showing lymphocytes with large pleomorphic vesicular nuclei and abundant cytoplasm, interspersed with small lymphocytes and eosinophils (Hematoxylin & eosin x400)
