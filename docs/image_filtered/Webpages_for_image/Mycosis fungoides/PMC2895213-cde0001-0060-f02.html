A Rare Presentation of Mycosis Fungoides Mimicking Psoriasis Vulgaris.

a Histopathology of a skin biopsy showed a dense band-like dermal infiltrate (H&E, original magnification ×125). b Abnormal lymphocytes with moderately irregular nuclei showing several mitoses (H&E, original magnification ×640). c Immunoreactivity for CD45RO (original magnification ×160). d Immunoreactivity for CD45RO (original magnification ×500).
