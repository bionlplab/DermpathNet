Secondary Cutaneous Amyloidosis in a Patient with Mycosis Fungoides.

(A) Multiple nodular deposits of fissured faintly eosinophilic amorphous material in the papillary dermis and upper reticular dermis (H&E, ×100). (B) Large, fissured, homogenous, eosinophilic masses in the papillary dermis (H&E, ×400). (C) The deposits were birefringence positive on Congo red staining viewed under polarized light microscopy (Congo red, ×100). (D) Electron micrographs show acellular small nodules consisting of randomly oriented, non-branching, non-anastomosing, 6.67~12.7 nm thick (mean, 9.3 nm) amyloid fibrils.

PMID: 28223751

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Hematolymphoid
