Analysis of differential β variable region of T cell receptor expression and NAV3/TNFRSF1B gene mutation in mycosis fungoides.

Pathological features in the reported case(A) H & E staining (× 100) showing the lymphocytes distributing in superficial perivascular and variably interstitial, a few lymphocytes enter into the epidermis. (B) higher-power view of the image (200 ×) showing lymphocytes infiltrated into the epidermis. (C) H & E staining (× 100) showing dense infiltrating lymphocytes in the dermis and subcutaneous fat. (D) higher resolution (× 400) highlighting the atypical lymphocytes with more pleomorphism. Immunohistochemical staining showing CD4+ (E), CD8+ (F) expressed in MF neoplastic cells.

PMID: 26918607

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Hematolymphoid
