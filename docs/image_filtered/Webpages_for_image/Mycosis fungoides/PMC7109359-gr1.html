Demodex-induced follicular mucinosis of the head and neck mimicking folliculotropic mycosis fungoides.

Top panel shows necrosis and accompanying mixed inflammatory cell infiltrate within hair follicle, peripheral mucin deposition, and lymphocyte exocytosis. (Hematoxylin-eosin stain; original magnifications, left, ×100; right, ×200.) Bottom panel shows a colloidal iron stain highlighting mucin deposition in blue (left) and live Demodex mite seen on oil preparation microscopy (right).

PMID: 32258294

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Hematolymphoid
