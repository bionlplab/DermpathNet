Erythrodermic mycosis fungoides with hypereosinophilic syndrome: a rare presentation.

(a) A photomicrograph showing a dense diffuse lymphocytic infiltrate in the upper and mid dermis with no particular adnexotropism. The inset reveals these atypical lymphocytes mainly arranged in an interstitial pattern (H & E: 100× and 400×). (b) A photomicrograph showing lymphocytic infiltrate weakly positive for T-cell marker CD8 (IHC: 400×). (c) A photomicrograph showing lymphocytic infiltrate strongly positive for T-cell marker CD4 (IHC: 400×). (d) A photomicrograph showing negative expression of B-cell marker CD20 (IHC: 400×).

PMID: 23940492

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Hematolymphoid
