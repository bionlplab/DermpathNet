Mycosis fungoides and gastric T-cell lymphoma: A case report.

Skin biopsy. Histologic examination of a plaque stage lesion with neoplastic bands of dermal lymphocyte infiltrate. Marked epidermotropism is evident. H&E staining observed under (A) magnification, x100 and (B) x400. Immunophenotype is classically (C) CD3+ (magnification, x400), (D) CD4+ (magnification, x400), (E) CD8− (magnification, x400) and (F) CD20− (magnification, x100).

PMID: 32754329

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Hematolymphoid
