Folliculotropic Mycosis Fungoides: Clinical and Histologic Features in Five Patients.

Lesional biopsy specimens: (a) Prominent epidermotropism with the formation of small nests of atypical lymphocyte; (b and c) follicular mucinosis characterized by basophilic granular material (mucin) within hair follicles accompanied by atypical perifollicular and intrafollicular lymphocytes (a: H and E, ×100; b: H and E, ×40; c: H and E, ×400)

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Hematolymphoid
