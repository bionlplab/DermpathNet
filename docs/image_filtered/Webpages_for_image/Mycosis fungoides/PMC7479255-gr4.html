Collisional variant of CD8(+) mycosis fungoides and indolent CD8(+) lymphoid proliferation.

A and B, Hematoxylin-eosin stain shows that the tumefactive deeper seated proliferation of lymphocytes had a distinctly atypical noncerebriform appearance. C, Immunohistochemical analysis with positivity for CD8 (not illustrated) TIA and D, CD68 in a distinct perinuclear Golgi staining pattern. (Original magnifications: B and D, ×100; C, ×40.)
