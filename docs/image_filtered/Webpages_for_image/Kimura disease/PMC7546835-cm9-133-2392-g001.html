Kimura disease with a history of Budd-Chiari syndrome: a case report.

Histopathology of the case of Kimura disease with a history of Budd-Chiari syndrome. Lymph node specimen showed abundant eosinophils infiltration. A: H&E staining, original magnification ×80; B: H&E staining, original magnification ×200; C: H&E staining, original magnification ×400. Immunohistochemical staining (ElivisionTM Plus immunohistochemical staining, original magnification ×40): paracortical area CD3+ (D), lymphoid follicular CD20+ (E), germinal center CD10+ (F), CD21+ (G), Bcl-2− (H), Ki-67+ 80% (I). Bcl-2: B-cell lymphoma 2; CD: Cluster of differentiation; H&E: Hematoxylin and eosin.

PMID: 32868502

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
