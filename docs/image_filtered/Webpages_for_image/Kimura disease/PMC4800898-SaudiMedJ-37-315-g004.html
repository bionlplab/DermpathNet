Kimura disease: No age or ethnicity limit.

Histopathological slides stained with hematoxylin and eosin stain (H&E) under microscope: A) reactive germinal centers (arrow). Note adjacent adipose tissue, areas of fibrosis, and the infiltrates in between the reactive germinal centers (x50, H&E). B) severe mixed inflammation (arrow): eosinophils, lymphocytes, histiocytes, and plasma cells (x200, H&E), and C) bilobed nuclei with granular red cytoplasm (arrow) of infiltrating eosinophils (x400, H&E).
