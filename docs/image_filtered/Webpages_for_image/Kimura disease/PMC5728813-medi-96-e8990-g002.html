Kimura disease of buccal region in a pediatric patient with nephrotic syndrome: A case report.

Histopathological images. (A) Follicular hyperplasia with reactive germinal centers and intense eosinophilic infiltration with eosinophilic microabscesses are present (H&E stain, ×40). (B) Predominant eosinophils and proliferation of thin-walled capillaries are present (H&E stain, ×200).

PMID: 29310412
