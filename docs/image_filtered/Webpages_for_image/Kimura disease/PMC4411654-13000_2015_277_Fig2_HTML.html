Kimura disease accompanied with nephrotic syndrome in a 45-year-old male.

Histologic examination of a biopsy sample from the subcutaneous mass of the upper arm (H and E): (a) proliferation of lymphoid follicles with distinct germinal centres (arrow; × 40); (b) vascular proliferation (arrow; × 100); (c, d) formation of eosinophilic microabscess and intense eosinophilic infiltration (arrow; c × 200; d × 400).

PMID: 25928198

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
