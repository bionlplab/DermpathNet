Kimura disease of the upper lip: Case report.

Histopathological microphotographs of our case of Kimura disease. (A and B) Low-power microphotographs showing lymphoid hyperplasia with several small- and medium-sized lymphoid follicles dispersed among the muscle bundles (Hematoxylin and eosin stain or H&E; original magnification; A, 4×; B, 10×). (C, D and E) Medium- and high-power microphotographs demonstrating several lymphoid follicles composed mainly of a sheet of lymphocytes with many proliferative thin-walled capillaries and an infiltrate of eosinophils (H&E; original magnification; C, 20×; D, 40×; E, 40×). (F) Medium-power microphotograph exhibiting an infiltrate of eosinophils in the collagenous fibrous connective tissue stroma (H&E; original magnification; 20×).

PMID: 34484611

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
