Metastatic hemangiosarcoma of the liver in a young rat.

(a) Tumors were seen in the liver parenchyma as basophilic small foci located mainly in the perilobular region. Bar = 200 µm. (b) Tumor cells showed cellular atypia and formed small nests and sheet patterns in the sinusoid with no encapsulation. Hepatocellular necrosis was seen sparsely (see the arrows). Tumor cells showed a clear cell border and formed narrow spaces containing erythrocytes (upper left inset). Bar = 100 µm. (c) Local invasion into the adjacent blood vessel was seen (see the arrow). Bar = 100 µm. H&E.

PMID: 28190928

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Vascular
