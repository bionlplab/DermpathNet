Primary intrapelvic hemangiosarcoma in a dog.

Histopathological investigation of the intrapelvic mass. A. Hemorrhagic and necrotic areas along the border. Staining: hematoxylin and eosin (H&E; ×50; Scale bar=200 µm). B. Malignancy indices, including anisokaryosis, and multiple nucleoli (arrows) (H&E; ×400; Scale bar=50 µm). C. Positive staining of vimentin (vimentin immunohistochemical staining; ×200; Scale bar=50 µm). D. Positive staining of CD31 (CD31 immunohistochemical staining; ×200; Scale bar=50 µm).

PMID: 27746404
