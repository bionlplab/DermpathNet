Microscopic appearance of SB-HSA canine hemangiosarcoma xenografts. (A and B) Photomicrographs showing examples of tumors from two mice inoculated with 5 × 106 SB-HSA cells stained with H & E; (C) Immunostaining of the tumor in a for Ki-67 using antibody MIB-1 (recognizes canine Ki-67); and (D) antibody TEC-3 (recognizes murine Ki-67).
