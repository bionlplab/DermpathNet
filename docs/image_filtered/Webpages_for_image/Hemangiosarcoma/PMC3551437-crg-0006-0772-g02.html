Angiosarcoma involving solid organs and the gastrointestinal tract with life-threatening bleeding.

Pathological features of splenic angiosarcoma. Hematoxylin-eosin staining revealed clusters of tumor cells (a; ×20 magnification), and immunohistochemical staining identified them as positive for CD31 (b; ×10 magnification), CD34 (c; ×10 magnification) and factor VIII (d; ×10 magnification).

PMID: 23341800

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Vascular
