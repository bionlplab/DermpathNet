Primary intrapelvic hemangiosarcoma in a dog.

Histopathological examination of the metastatic organs. A. Lung metastasis. A thick and enlarged vascular channel with neoplastic endothelial cells (H&E; ×20; Scale bar=500 µm). B. Cerebellum metastasis. Significant focal hemorrhage and necrosis (H&E; ×10; Scale bar=1,000 µm). C. Cerebellum metastasis. Neoplastic endothelial cells forming irregular vascular channels (H&E; ×100; Scale bar=100 µm).
