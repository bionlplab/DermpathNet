The relevance of complete imaging investigations in lymphomatoid papulosis.

a, b) Irregular epidermal hyperplasia with pseudocarcinomatous features and dense nodular lymphoid infiltrate in the dermis (HE, 2x and 20x). c) Diffuse staining for CD30 (x20). d) medium-large atypical lymphocytes with partly pleomorphic, partly anaplastic morphology e)-f ) 18F-FDG PET/CT (axial images) showed intense tracer uptake (e, arrow) corresponding to a lesion of descending intestine and a small area of faint uptake (e, dotted arrow) corresponding to 10 mm locoregional lymph-node (f, dotted arrow) g) medium-large atypical lymphocytes stained for CD30 (x40).

PMID: 36483223

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Hematolymphoid
