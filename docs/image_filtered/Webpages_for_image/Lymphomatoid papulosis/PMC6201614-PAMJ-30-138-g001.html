Type B lymphomatoid papulosis.

A) papular lesions with irregular pigmentation on the axilla; B) erythematous papules on the flanks; C) large lymphocytes, with CD30 expression confined to the cell membrane and Golgi region (H&E, original magnification x100); D) atypical large lymphocytes, with CD30 expression confined to the cell membrane and Golgi region (H&E, original magnification x400)

PMID: 30374384

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Hematolymphoid
