Pityriasis Lichenoides Et Varioliformis Acuta and Lymphomatoid Papulosis Type F: A Case Report of Two Entities in One Patient.

Immunohistochemistry of lymphomatoid papulosis type F. Positive (A) CD3, (B) CD4, and (C) CD8 staining of small lymphocytes in perivascular area. (D) Perifollicular and follicular infiltrate of positive CD30 large atypical mononuclear cells (x40 original magnification).

PMID: 36065341

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Hematolymphoid
