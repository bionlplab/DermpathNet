Mycosis Fungoides, Lymphomatoid Papulosis and Hodgkin's Lymphoma in the Same Patient: Apropos of a Possible Monoclonal Origin.

Lymphomatoid papulosis: (a) Panoramic view of skin biopsy showing superficial and deep nodular lymphoid infiltrates with small and large cells (H and E, ×100); (b) Immunohistochemical staining showing predominance of CD3+ lymphocytes and numerous CD30+ large cells. A small percentage of cells also shows positive staining for CD25 (×100)
