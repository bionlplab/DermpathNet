Dermoscopy of eccrine angiomatous hamartoma: The popcorn pattern.

The sections show in a low-power view the hamartomatous presence of 5 to 6 lobules of eccrine glands that are accompanied by some dilated vascular lumina. (Original magnifications: A, ×4; B, ×10; C, ×20.)
