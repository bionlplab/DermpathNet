A spindle cell squamous cell carcinoma on the cheek presenting with in-transit metastases and a satellite lesion.

Histopathological examination showing the biphasic malignant nature of tumor. The tumor had superficial epithelial and deep sarcomatous components. Squamous cell carcinoma of the epidermis infiltrated the upper dermis. The spindle tumor cells in the dermis and subcutis demonstrated atypical nuclei and frequent mitoses without any specific pattern of growth and cellular differentiation (H&E, Ã—10).
