Evaluation of immunohistochemical expression of TWIST in oral epithelial dysplasia and squamous cell carcinoma.

Expression of TWIST in oral squamous cell carcinoma (OSCC). Tumor cells with strong positive staining observed in the periphery of cancer nests of well-differentiated OSCC (a) and moderately differentiated OSCC (b). Diffuse localization of the positive cells in poorly differentiated OSCC (c) (streptavidin-biotin (a) ×400, (b) ×100, (c) ×400)

PMID: 32206058

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
