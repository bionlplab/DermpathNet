Portal Vein Thrombosis Associated with Trousseau Syndrome due to Urinary Bladder Squamous Cell Carcinoma in a Liver Cirrhosis Patient.

Histological findings of squamous cell carcinoma arising from the urinary bladder. (a) Low-magnification image of the borderline area between the urinary bladder and the transplanted small intestine. (b) Squamous cell carcinoma of the urinary bladder. (c) The transplanted small intestine. (d) Metaplastic squamous cell epithelium of the bladder. Hematoxylin and Eosin staining (a-d). Original magnifications: ×5 (a), ×40 (b, c), and ×100 (d). Arrow: muscularis propria of the bladder. Arrowheads: muscularis propria of the transplanted intestine.
