A20 as a Potential New Tool in Predicting Recurrence and Patient's Survival in Oral Squamous Cell Carcinoma.

Histomorphology (H&E; (A,D,G)) and CD3 and A20 IHC stainings at central and peripheral tumor areas and the associated non-neoplastic mucosa of a representative keratinizing OSCC case of the TMA cohort. Whereas only few or no stromal CD3+ T cells within the tumor center (B,C) and non-neoplastic mucosa (H,I), respectively, show A20 IHC expression, high A20 expression was observed within the stromal CD3+ T cells at the tumor periphery (E,F).

PMID: 36765630

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
