Rigid Tissue Increases Cytoplasmic pYAP Expression in Pre-Malignant Stage of Lung Squamous Cell Carcinoma (SCC) In Vivo.

H&E staining of lung tissue from the VC (A,B) and PM (C,D) group at 10× and 40× magnification. (A) shows normal bronchial epithelial cells and (B) shows a single layer of cells contacting the basement membrane, whereas (C) shows a thickened bronchial epithelium and (D) shows hyperplasia lesions around the bronchial. (E) shows the bar chart of the bronchial epithelium thickness (µm) for the VC and PM groups. The arrow points to the epithelial cells. ** in (E) indicates p < 0.01 using the student’s t-test.
