Infundibulocystic Squamous Cell Carcinoma.

Histological findings of the excised mass. (A) Infiltrating infundibular canals and radiating cords with multiple cysts in the dermis (H&E, ×40). (B) Multiple cysts filled with laminated keratin in the dermis (H&E, ×40). (C) Neoplasm cords or nests composed of atypical keratinocytes with hyperchromatism, pleomorphism, dyskeratosis, and minimal mitosis (H&E, ×200).

PMID: 26082591

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
