Leiomyosarcoma and Squamous Cell Carcinoma Arising in Mature Cystic Teratoma of the Ovary.

Leiomyosarcoma and squamous cell carcinoma. (a) Squamous cell carcinoma at the upper part of leiomyosarcoma. Note an abrupt demarcation between two components (H&E, 4x). (b) Squamous cell carcinoma was highlighted by positive staining for AE1/AE3, whereas leiomyosarcoma was completely negative (IHC stain, 4x). (c) Infiltrative nests of squamous cell carcinoma surrounded by desmoplastic stromal reaction (H&E, 10x).

PMID: 28751996

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
