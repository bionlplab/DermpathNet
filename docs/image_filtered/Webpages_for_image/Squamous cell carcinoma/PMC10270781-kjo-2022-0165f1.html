Second Primary Conjunctival Squamous Cell Carcinoma on Multiple Myeloma: A Case Report.

(A) Slit-lamp photography of preoperative conjunctival mass. (B) Slit-lamp photography at 2.5 years after surgery showing no recurrence. (C) Hematoxylin-eosin–staining (×100) shows nests of squamous epithelial cells arising from epidermis and extending into the dermis. (D) Hematoxylin-eosin–staining (×200) shows malignant squamous cells with pleomorphic nucleus and abundant eosinophilic cytoplasm.

PMID: 37068837

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
