A rare case of renal metastasis from squamous cell carcinoma of the cervix.

(a) Hydronephrotic kidney with capsular thickening and cortical atrophy. (b) Pyelonephritis (H and E, ×100). (c) Capsular thickening with multiple tumor deposits (H and E, ×100). (d) Capsular thickening with multiple tumor deposits (H and E, ×100). (e) Extension of tumor in renal parenchyma (H and E, ×100). (f) Flattened pelvic lining without dysplasia (H and E, ×100)

PMID: 27499599

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
