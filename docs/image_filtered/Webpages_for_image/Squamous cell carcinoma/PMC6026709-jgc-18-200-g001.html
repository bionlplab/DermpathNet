Multiregion Comprehensive Genomic Profiling of a Gastric Mixed Neuroendocrine-Nonneuroendocrine Neoplasm with Trilineage Differentiation.

Case pathology. (A) MiNEN with large-cell NEC, SCC, and AC in a collision tumor pattern (H&E, 20× magnification). (B) Synaptophysin immunohistochemical stain showing positive staining of the neuroendocrine component (20× magnification). (C) P63 immunohisotochemical stain showing positive staining of the squamous component (20× magnification). (D) Ki-67 immunohisotochemical stain demonstrating an overall high proliferation index (20× magnification; 152×116 mm [144×144 DPI]).NEC = neuroendocrine carcinoma; SCC = squamous cell carcinoma; AC = adenocarcinoma; H&E = hematoxylin and eosin.

PMID: 29984070

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
