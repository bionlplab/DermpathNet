Podoplanin is an efficient predictor of neck lymph node metastasis in tongue squamous cell carcinoma with low tumor budding grade.

Immunohistochemical analysis for TBG by Pan-Cytokeratin AE1/3 staining. Budding is recognized as a single cancer cell or cancer clusters consisting of four cells or less at the invasive front shown by the arrows. (A) TBG 1: budding counts 0–4. (B) TBG 2: budding counts 5–9. (C) TBG 3: budding counts more than nine. Each field is magnification, 20×10. Scale bar, 100 µm. TBG, tumor budding grade; HE, hematoxylin and eosin.
