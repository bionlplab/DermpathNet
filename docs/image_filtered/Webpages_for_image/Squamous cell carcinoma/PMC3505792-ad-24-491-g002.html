Tripartite motif-containing 29 expression in squamous cell carcinoma.

Immunohistochemical analysis of tripartite motif-containing 29 (TRIM29) expression in (A) malignant melanoma, (B) squamous cell carcinoma, (C) basal cell carcinoma, and (D) normal skin tissue. Only squamous cell carcinoma displayed TRIM29 expression. (A, B, C: ×200, D: ×100).

PMID: 23197927

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
