Fibroblasts in Head and Neck Squamous Cell Carcinoma Associated With Perineural Invasion Have High-Level Nuclear Yes-Associated Protein (YAP) Expression.

A, Squamous cell carcinoma with perineural invasion, hematoxylin and eosin (H&E) section, ×200. B, Immunostain of YAP, fibroblasts with strong nuclear YAP expression and tumor cells with weak cytoplasmic expression, ×200. C, Immunostain of phosphorylated at Ser127 (pYAP) in the fibroblasts with perineural invasion and fibroblasts and tumor cells with mainly cytoplasmic expression, ×200.
