Reactive Eccrine Syringofibroadenoma on the Heel, Clinically Mimicking Squamous Cell Carcinoma.

(a) Hyperkeratosis with thin anastomosing strands of cuboidal cells extending from the epidermis to the upper dermis (H&E stain, original magnification × 2). (b) Hematomas in the intracorneal region and upper dermis (H&E stain, original magnification × 4). (c) The cells of the ductal and cystic structures (d) were positive for CEA (original magnification × 20).

PMID: 31885941

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
