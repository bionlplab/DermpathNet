Giant squamous cell carcinoma of penis with rapid progression.

(A) Postoperative image after total penectomy, scrotal extended resection, perineal urethrostomy and lymph node biopsy. (B) Appearance of the mass measuring 11 cm × 9.5 cm × 6.5cm invading the entire penile shaft. (C-D) Pathology revealed moderate-to poor-differentiated squamous cell carcinoma was noted (H&E, original magnification × 40 and × 200).
