Co-expression of fibroblast growth factor receptor 3 with mutant p53, and its association with worse outcome in oropharyngeal squamous cell carcinoma.

mp53 and FGFR3 staining in samples from patients with SCCHN.IHC staining of mp53 in SCCHN samples shows mainly nuclear and some cytoplasmic expression patterns. IHC staining of FGFR3 shows membrane and cytoplasmic expression. Four representative images are presented: mp53+/FGFR3+, mp53+/FGFR3-, mp53-/FGFR3+, mp53-/FGFR3- and negative control. (200 x magnification).
