Primary and metastatic squamous cell carcinoma of the thyroid gland: Two case reports.

HE images of case 1: (a) these cells were cohesive and arranged in nest and sheet patterns in the low-power field, HE ×100 and (b) presence of keratinization was noted, and the tumor cells exhibited hyper-chromatic nuclei, prominent eosinophilic nucleoli, and abundant eosinophilic cytoplasm in the high-power field, HE ×400.
