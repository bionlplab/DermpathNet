Glut-1 as a prognostic biomarker in oral squamous cell carcinoma.

Immunoexpression of Glut 1 in OSCC. (a) Glut 1 expression in well differentiated (Grade I) OSCC, (IHC stain, ×100) (b) Glut 1 expression in well-differentiated OSCC showing predominant basal cell staining and loss of Glut 1 expression with keratinization at the core of the tumor nest, (IHC stain, ×400). (c) and (d) Glut 1 immunoexpressionin in moderately differentiated (Grade II) OSCC, (IHC stain, ×100 and ×400 respectively. (e) and (f) Glut 1 immunoexpression in poorly differentiated (Grade III) OSCC showing diffuse immunopositivity (IHC stain, ×100 and x400 respectively)
