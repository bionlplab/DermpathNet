Human Papillomavirus (HPV69/HPV73) Coinfection associated with Simultaneous Squamous Cell Carcinoma of the Anus and Presumed Lung Metastasis.

Microscopic findings of the anal lesion excision (A,B) and lung nodule biopsy (C,D). A, There is a notable distinction between the condylomatous area on the top/left and the carcinomatous area on the bottom/right (H&E; x40). B, The squamous cell carcinoma on the left demonstrates full-thickness dysplasia, keratinization, and invasive growth pattern (H&E; x200). C, The majority of the specimen is involved by squamous cell carcinoma (H&E; x40). D, The squamous cell carcinoma demonstrates marked cytologic atypia, keratinization, and invasive growth pattern (H&E; x200).
