Evaluation of e-cadherin and vimentin expression for different grades of oral epithelial dysplasia and oral squamous cell carcinoma - An immunohistochemical study.

(a) Clinical picture of normal buccal mucosa; (b) Histopathology showing the normal buccal mucosa (H&E, ×40); (c) E-cadherin showing intense membranous staining in normal lower 1/3rd mucosal epithelium; (d) Vimentin showing negative expression in normal mucosal epithelium and intense positivity in the connective tissue (IHC, ×20)
