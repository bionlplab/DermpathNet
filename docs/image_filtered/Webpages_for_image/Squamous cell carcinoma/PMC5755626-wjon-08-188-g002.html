Basaloid Squamous Cell Carcinoma of the Lung Associated With Syndrome of Inappropriate Antidiuretic Hormone Secretion.

(a) H&E stain of lung biopsy showing small basophilic cells with a high nuclear to cytoplasmic ratio resembling small cell carcinoma. (b) Positive immunohistochemical stain for cytokeratin 5/6 supporting squamous derivation.

PMID: 29317964

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
