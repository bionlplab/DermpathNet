WAVE3 upregulation in esophageal squamous cell carcinoma and its effect on the migration of human esophageal cancer cell lines in vitro.

Immunohistochemical staining of WAVE3 in ESCC tissues and adjacent normal tissues. (A) Low WAVE3 expression in adjacent normal tissues (magnification, ×100). (B) High WAVE3 expression in ESCC tissues (magnification, ×100). (C) WAVE3 expression levels were compared between ESCC tissues and adjacent normal tissues. *P<0.05. WAVE3, Wiskott-Aldrich syndrome verprolin-homologous protein 3; ESCC, esophageal squamous cell carcinoma.
