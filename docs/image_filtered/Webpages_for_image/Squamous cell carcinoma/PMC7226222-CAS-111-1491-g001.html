Prognostic value of HLA class I expression in patients with oral squamous cell carcinoma.

Representative H&E staining and immunohistochemical pan‐cytokeratin labeling of oral squamous cell carcinoma sections for the assessment of 2 distinct tumor compartments. Tumor center (TCe) and invasive front (IF) were evaluated. Pan‐cytokeratin expression was detected using a primary mAb (clone AE1/AE3; Abcam)

PMID: 32167621

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
