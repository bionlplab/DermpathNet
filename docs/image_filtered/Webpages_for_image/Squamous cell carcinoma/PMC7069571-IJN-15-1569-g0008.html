Efficient miRNA Inhibitor Delivery with Graphene Oxide-Polyethylenimine to Inhibit Oral Squamous Cell Carcinoma.

Histological analysis of organs after GO-PEI-inhibitor treatment. H&E staining assay of lung, liver, spleen and kidney tissues after an intratumoral injection of saline, the miR-214 inhibitor, GO-PEI or GO-PEI-inhibitor complexes (30 μL) for 20 days. Experiments were repeated 3 times. Scale bar: 100 μm.
