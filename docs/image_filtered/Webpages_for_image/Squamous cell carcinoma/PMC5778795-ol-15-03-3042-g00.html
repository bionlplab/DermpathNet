Expression of proteins associated with epithelial-mesenchymal transition in esophageal squamous cell carcinoma.

Immunohistochemical photomicrographs of CD147, PTTG and CD44v6 in tissue samples from patients with esophageal squamous cell carcinoma. The expression of CD147, PTTG and CD44v6 was classified as - (negative staining), + (weak staining), ++ (moderate staining) or +++ (strong staining); magnifications, ×100 (main image) and ×400 (inset). CD147, cluster of differentiation 147; PTTG, pituitary tumor transforming gene.

PMID: 29435035

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
