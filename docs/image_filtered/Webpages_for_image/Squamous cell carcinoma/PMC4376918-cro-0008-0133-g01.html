Testicular metastasis in a case of squamous cell carcinoma of the lung.

Representative images of histopathology from the right radical orchiectomy. a The testis contained a well-circumscribed tumor measuring 1.5 cm, no involvement of the spermatic cord, and negative surgical margins. The tumor showed anastomosing nests of large cells infiltrating between benign seminiferous tubules. HE. ×100. b A higher-magnification image illustrates large cells with vesicular chromatin, large nucleoli, and abundant cytoplasm. HE. ×400.
