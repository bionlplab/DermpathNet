BRCA2 mutation in advanced lung squamous cell carcinoma treated with Olaparib and a PD-1 inhibitor: a case report.

(A) Fiberoptic bronchoscopy shows cauliflower-like new organisms blocking the left main bronchus and oozing blood on the surface of the new organisms. The pathological classification of lung squamous cell carcinoma was confirmed by multi-biomarker immunohistochemistry. (B) HE×100, (C) HE×400, (D) ki-67, (E) P40, (F) TTF-1.

PMID: 37260982

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
