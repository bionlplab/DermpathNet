A rare cause of small bowel perforation: A metastatic lesion from squamous cell carcinoma of the tongue.

Histopathology examination of the resected segment (a) Microscopic examination revealed infiltration of the small bowel muscular wall by diffuse sheets of neoplastic cells (Star mark), H&E, 4x, (b) Higher magnification showing poorly differentiated neoplastic cells with some intra-tumor neutrophilic infiltrate, H&E, 40x, (c) Immunohistochemical staining showing the neoplastic cells staining positive for P63 and (d) CK 5/6.

PMID: 32155586

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
