Sex-dependent development of Kras-induced anal squamous cell carcinoma in mice.

Tdtomato expression indicates Pdx1-Cre expression and mutant Kras expression in both male and female mice.Anal tissue was excised from 3 month old Ai14; LSL-KrasG12D; Pdx1-Cre (AiKC) female (A, B, C) and male (D, E, F) mice, fixed and frozen embedded and sectioned for H&E analysis (A and D). Additional adjacent sections were prepared for immunohistochemistry (IHC) to identify tdTomato protein at 4x and 10x (B, C, E and F). Scale bars equal 100 Î¼m. Positive IHC signal for tdTomato reveals the location of Cre expression (Pdx1-Cre), which serves as a marker for the location of mutant KrasG12D expression.
