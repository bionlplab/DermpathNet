A Primary Barrett's Adenocarcinoma with a Squamous Cell Carcinoma Component.

SCC component covered by adenocarcinoma. In this tumor, the entire SCC component was covered by adenocarcinoma [Hematoxylin and Eosin (H&E) staining (a, b), P40 (c), a marker for squamous epithelial cells, and CK18 [d], a marker for columnar epithelial cells]. The adenocarcinoma extended underneath the normal squamous epithelium (a, c, d). In the high-magnification image of H&E staining (b), the blue star corresponds to the SCC component, whereas the yellow stars correspond to the adenocarcinoma component.
