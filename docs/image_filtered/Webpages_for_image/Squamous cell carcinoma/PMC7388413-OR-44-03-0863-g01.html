Selective inhibition of PI3K110α as a novel therapeutic strategy for cetuximab‑resistant oral squamous cell carcinoma.

Representative immunohistochemical staining of PI3Kp110α. (A) Negative PI3Kp110α staining is observed in normal epithelium. (B) Weak staining for PI3Kp110α in OSCC with a Bryne's score of 3. (C) OSCC with Bryne's score of 3, demonstrates strong PI3Kp110α cytoplasmic and nuclear expression (staining index of 12). Magnification ×100.
