Evaluation of immunohistochemical expression of TWIST in oral epithelial dysplasia and squamous cell carcinoma.

Expression of TWIST in epithelial dysplasia: (a) Mild; (b) moderate; (c and d) severe. Weak staining in the basal and parabasal layers in mild dysplasia (a). Nuclear expression of TWIST increase in moderate epithelial dysplasia in the basal and parabasal layers (b). Strong expression of TWIST in severe epithelial dysplasia, in the basal and parabasal layers (c and d) (streptavidin-biotin) (a and c, ×100; b, ×200; d, ×400)

PMID: 32206058

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
