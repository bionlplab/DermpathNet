Podoplanin is an efficient predictor of neck lymph node metastasis in tongue squamous cell carcinoma with low tumor budding grade.

Scoring of PDPN in tumor budding cells by immunohistochemical stain analysis with D2-40. After confirming budding cells by Pan-Cytokeratin AE1/3 staining (upper figures), the PDPN score was judged in the same budding cells (lower figures). (A) was judged to be score 0 and (B), (C), and (D) were judged to be scores 1, 2, and 3, respectively. For statistical analysis (A) is determined to be negative. (B), (C), and (D) are determined to be positive. The arrows indicate each bud and each field at magnification, 60×10. Scale bar, 40 µm. PDPN, podoplanin.
