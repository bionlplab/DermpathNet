Cutaneous squamous cell carcinoma arising within a mycosis fungoides patch: Case report and review of the literature.

Histologic and immunohistochemical findings. A, Specimens from the erythematous patch demonstrate heavy infiltrate of atypical lymphocytes surrounding the keratinizing squamous cell neoplasm and marked epidermotropism. B and C, Immunohistochemical studies of atypical lymphocytes show stronger CD4 positivity as compared with that of CD8. (Hematoxylin-eosin and other indicated stains; original magnifications: A, ×20; B and C, ×10.)

PMID: 33294566

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
