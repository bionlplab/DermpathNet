Post-radiotherapy recurrence of conventional oral squamous cell carcinoma showing sarcomatoid components: an immunohistochemical study.

Histopathological features of SpSCC: Biphasic tumor consisting of infiltrating neoplastic epithelium in close relationship to spindle cells (H&E, A, x10; B, x20). Dysplastic epithelium and overt pleomorphism of the spindle cells in the sarcomatoid component (H&E, C, x20; D, x20).

PMID: 34277487

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
