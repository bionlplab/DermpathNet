Differential Regulation of Innate Lymphoid Cells in Human and Murine Oral Squamous Cell Carcinoma.

Representative images of hematoxylin and eosin (H & E) stained oral squamous cell carcinoma at x40, x100, x200, and x400 magnification. Well-differentiated squamous cell carcinoma (WDSCC) and moderately differentiated squamous cell carcinoma (MDSCC) showing large and small keratin pearls (P), respectively. Poorly differentiated squamous cell carcinoma (PDSCC) showing mitosis (M) and pleomorphism (Pl).
