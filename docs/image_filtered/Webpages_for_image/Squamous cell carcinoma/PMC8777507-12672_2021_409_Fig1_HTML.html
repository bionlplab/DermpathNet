Involvement of transcribed lncRNA uc.291 and SWI/SNF complex in cutaneous squamous cell carcinoma.

Expression of BRG1, ACTL6A, uc.291 and the differentiation genes loricrin and LCE1C in normal epidermis and NMSCs. a H&E staining and IHC analysis of Actl6a, Brg1 and Loricrin in normal skin. One representative image of 4 is shown. b BRG1 and ACTL6A expression at the mRNA level. Relative quantification of clinical samples of normal skin (n = 21), cBCC (n = 26) and cSCC (n = 13) by RTqPCR. The p-value was obtained using one-way ANOVA; ns not significant. c uc291 expression at the mRNA level. Relative quantification of clinical samples of normal skin (n = 21), cBCC (n = 26) and cSCC (n = 13) by RTqPCR. The p-value was obtained by one-way ANOVA; ns not significant. d LORICRIN and LCE1C expression at the mRNA level. Relative quantification of clinical samples of normal skin (n = 21), cBCC (n = 26) and cSCC (n = 13) by RTqPCR. The p-value was obtained by one-way ANOVA; ns not significant
