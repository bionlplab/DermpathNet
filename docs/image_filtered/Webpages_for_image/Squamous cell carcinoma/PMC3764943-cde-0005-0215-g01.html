Signet-ring cutaneous squamous cell carcinoma arising on the back of the finger.

Clinical and histological findings. a Reddish-brown, erosive macule on the back of the left fourth finger. b The biopsy specimen showed a thickened epidermis with parakeratosis, but no granular layer. An abnormal, stratified arrangement and cytologically atypical keratinocytes were also observed (HE staining, ×40). c Numerous signet-ring cells with nuclei that are crescentically compressed to the cellular border (HE staining, ×250). d The border of the skin lesion also shows focal parakeratosis, loss of the normal stratified arrangement of the epidermis and the presence of signet-ring cells (HE staining, ×100).

PMID: 24019773

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
