High Expression of MDM2 and the p53 Protein is Predictive Biomarkers for Poor Prognosis of Oesophageal Squamous Cell Carcinoma.

Immunohistochemical staining for MDM2 and p53 in human oesophageal carcinoma. The data show expression levels of MDM2 (A×100, B×400) and p53 (C×100, D×400) compared with the negative control (E×100, F×400) in tumour tissues from patients with OSCC. Amplification of the MDM2 gene (G×1000) compared with the negative control (H×1000) according to FISH in tumour tissues from patients with OSCC.

PMID: 33790647
