Evaluation of e-cadherin and vimentin expression for different grades of oral epithelial dysplasia and oral squamous cell carcinoma - An immunohistochemical study.

(a) Clinical picture of patient with verrucous lesion extending all over the tongue surface; (b) Histopathology with features of well-differentiated squamous cell carcinoma (H&E, ×40); (c) E-cadherin showing intense membranous and cytoplasmic positivity in >50% of epithelial cells; (d) Vimentin showing mild to moderate expression with infiltrative pattern (IHC, ×40)

PMID: 35968190

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
