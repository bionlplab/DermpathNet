Papillary squamous cell carcinoma of the cervix: Two cases and a review of the literature.

In this variant of squamous cell carcinoma, the cells can exhibit eosinophilic cytoplasm and full thickness hyperchromasia. No invasion was identified in this specimen (H&E stain, 40 ×).
