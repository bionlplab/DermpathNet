Unusual recurrent tongue spindle cell carcinoma with marked anaplasia occurring at the site of glossectomy for a well-differentiated squamous cell carcinoma: A case report.

Surgically removed secondary tumor. (A) Storiform patterns of spindle cells (arrows) and residual SCC nests were observed (magnification, ×200; hematoxylin and eosin stain). (B) Residual SCC and nearby scattered cells were positive for cytokeratin AE1/AE3 (magnification, ×100; immunohistochemistry). SCC, squamous cell carcinoma.

PMID: 28781811

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
