An Entirely Atypical Presentation of Esophageal Squamous Cell Cancer with Pancreatic and Bone Metastases.

a Fine needle aspirate of the pancreatic head showing a group of markedly atypical epithelial cells in the background of extensive necrosis, consistent with carcinoma. b Biopsy of distal esophagus showing infiltrating cords of malignant squamous cells with associated desmoplastic reaction, consistent with moderately differentiated invasive squamous cell carcinoma (×10). c Biopsy of L3 vertebral body showing metastatic carcinoma, positive for p40 and p63, which is morphologically and phenotypically consistent with squamous cell carcinoma (×20).

PMID: 31762730

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
