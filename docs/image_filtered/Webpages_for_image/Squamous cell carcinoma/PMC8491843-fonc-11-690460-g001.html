Cathepsins B, D, and G Are Expressed in Metastatic Head and Neck Cutaneous Squamous Cell Carcinoma.

Representative hematoxylin and eosin (A) and immunohistochemical-stained (B–D) images of metastatic head and neck cutaneous squamous cell carcinoma (mHNcSCC) tissue samples, demonstrating tumor nests (TNs) surrounded by the peritumoral stroma (PTS) (A). Cathepsin B (B, brown) and cathepsin D (C, brown) were expressed on the cytoplasm of cells within the TNs (arrows), and occasional cells within the PTS (arrowheads). Cathepsin G (D, brown) was expressed by few cells within the PTS (arrowheads). Nuclei were counter-stained with hematoxylin. Original magnification: 400x. Scale bars: 20µm. n = 20.

PMID: 34621666

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
