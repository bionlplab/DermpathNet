Cutaneous metastasis from esophageal basaloid squamous cell carcinoma: A case report.

Microscopic findings of the resected cutaneous tumor.(a) There is growth of tumor cells with solid nest and without continuity with the epidermis (HE, ×40). (b) The tumor cells have round or short spindle-shaped nuclei (HE, ×200). Immunohistochemical staining is positive for (c) cytokeratin 19 (×100) and (d) Bcl-2 (×100).HE: hematoxylin–eosin.

PMID: 33596520

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
