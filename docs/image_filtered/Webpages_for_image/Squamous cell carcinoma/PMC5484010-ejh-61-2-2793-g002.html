Fibroblast activating protein-α expression in squamous cell carcinoma of the esophagus in primary and irradiated tumors: the use of archival FFPE material for molecular techniques.

Examples of IHC staining patterns for FAP-α in the normal and tumorous tissues of the esophagus in three randomly chosen patients (P. I-III) untreated before surgery at a total magnification of 200x. Weak and locally moderate FAP-α immunoreactivity in normal epithelium (C, F, I). Strong FAP-α immunoreactivity in esophageal squamous cell carcinomas (A, B, D, E, G, H). Scale bars: 100 µm.
