Malignant transformation of tailgut cyst to squamous cell carcinoma, a rare case with poor outcome.

(A) Cyst wall containing smooth muscle bundles and lined by squamous and columnar epithelium (H&E, ×100). (B) The stroma is infiltrated by nests of neoplastic cells with vesicular nuclei and abundant eosinophilic cytoplasm with foci of keratin‐like material.
