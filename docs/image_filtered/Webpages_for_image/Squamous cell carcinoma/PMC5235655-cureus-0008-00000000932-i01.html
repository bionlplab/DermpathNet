Recurrent Squamous Cell Carcinoma of the Eyelid Presenting as Trigeminal Neuralgia.

(A) A 79-year-old man presenting with left-sided eyelid pain and an ulcerative brow lesion. (B) Excisional biopsy showing the histological appearance of SCC with perineural invasion (H&E 100x) of the brow lesion. (C) Axial MRI revealing an abnormal enhancing mass (red arrow) of the left cavernous sinus surrounding the left cavernous carotid artery extending into the left orbit anteriorly encasing the left optic nerve.SCC: squamous cell carcinoma

PMID: 28097083

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
