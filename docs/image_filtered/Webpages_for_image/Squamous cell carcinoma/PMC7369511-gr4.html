A rare case of anal squamous cell carcinoma metastasizing to the scrotum.

Histopathology of metastatic lesions at dermatology presentation. Atypical epithelial cells with areas of necrosis and degenerative change are visible at ×100 (A), whereas nuclear pleomorphism and atypical mitoses are more clearly seen at ×400 (B).
