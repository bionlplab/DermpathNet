Combined large cell neuroendocrine carcinoma and squamous cell carcinoma of the oropharynx: A collision course of tumors.

Endobronchial ultrasound guided FNA cell block showing high‐grade large‐cell neuroendocrine carcinoma: (A) These cells are composed of medium‐large atypical, pleomorphic cells with large, hyperchromatic nuclei and small nucleoli, H&E x200. Immunohistochemical staining shows (B) rare positive cells for synaptophysin, (C) strongly positive for CD56, and (D) negative for p40
