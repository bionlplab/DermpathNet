Squamous cell carcinoma arising from Warthin's tumor in the parotid gland.

Pathologic findings of the mass in the left parotid gland. (a) Gross specimen of resected left parotid gland shows a 6 cm-sized firm mass with cystic change. (b) Microscopic specimens of the left parotid tumor. The upper panel shows squamous cell-lined cyst (H&E staining, x 40). And the lower panel shows Warthin’s tumor with infiltration of stroma by malignant squamous cells (arrows) (H&E staining, x 100).

PMID: 31938564

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
