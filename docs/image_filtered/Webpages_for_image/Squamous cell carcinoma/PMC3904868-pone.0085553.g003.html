Background coloration of squamous epithelium in esophago-pharyngeal squamous cell carcinoma: what causes the color change?

BC-negative case.a. The color of the epithelia between each dilated IPCL was identical to surrounding mucosa. b. H&E staining histologically confirmed the lesion as intraepithelial neoplasia. c. Immunohistology using anti-Hb Ab showed that the lesion was negative for Hb.

PMID: 24489662

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
