Collision tumor of small cell carcinoma and squamous cell carcinoma of the maxillary sinus: Case report.

Histopathologic examination revealed a high amount of chromatin and non-uniform cells on H&E staining. Immunostaining indicated the presence of 2 of 3 neuroendocrine tumor markers, CD56 and Chromogranin. (A) A high N/C ratio and atypical nuclei (H&E) suggested a high amount of chromatin and non-uniform cells revealed small cell carcinoma. (B) Immunostaining: CD56 (+); (C) Immunostaining: Chromogranin A (+) (magnification, x400).

PMID: 35400122

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
