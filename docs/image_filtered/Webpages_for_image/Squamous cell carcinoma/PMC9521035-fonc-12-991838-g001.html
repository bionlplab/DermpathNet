Updates in the management of unknown primary of the head and neck.

These slides demonstrate a small 3 mm tumor in the glossotonsillar sulcus that was identified through TORS. The H&E stained image of this tumor shows irregular nests of non-keratinizing squamous cell carcinoma underlying normal squamous mucosa in a background of tonsillar lymphoid tissue (A). P16 immunostain is diffusely positive in ~100% of tumor cells (B).

PMID: 36185196

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
