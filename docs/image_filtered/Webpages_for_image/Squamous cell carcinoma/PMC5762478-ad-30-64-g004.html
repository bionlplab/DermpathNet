Features Causing Confusion between Basal Cell Carcinoma and Squamous Cell Carcinoma in Clinical Diagnosis.

Dermoscopic and histopathologic findings of basosquamous cell carcinoma. Basosquamous cell carcinoma appeared as basal cell carcinoma or squamous cell carcinoma according to histopathologic findings. Arborizing vessels with hyperkeratosis (A, C, E) and ulceration with telangiectasia (B, D, F) are seen (H&E; C, D: ×40, E, F: ×100).

PMID: 29386834

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
