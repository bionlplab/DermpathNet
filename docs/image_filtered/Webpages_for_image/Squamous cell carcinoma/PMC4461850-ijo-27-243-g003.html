Basaloid Squamous Cell Carcinoma: An Unusual Ball-Valve Laryngeal Obstruction.

(a) Low power view of the cell block showing clusters of malignant cells with basaloid appearance (Haematoxylin eosin, original magnification x 40). (b) High power magnification showing malignant basaloid cells with coarse chromatin pattern, numerous mitosis, and apoptotic bodies. Some of the cells show intercellular bridges denoting squamous differentiation (Haematoxylin eosin, original magnification x 400).

PMID: 26082908

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
