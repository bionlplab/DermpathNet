Loss of MMP-27 Predicts Mandibular Bone Invasion in Oral Squamous Cell Carcinoma.

H&E-stained sections of OSCC (100× and 400× magnification). (A,B) Primary tumors classified with a score of 3, thus exhibiting a non-aggressive invasion pattern characterized by broad, finger-like pushing fronts invading surrounding tissue. (C,D) Primary tumor with a score of 4 (aggressive pattern of invasion). Here, small cell islets are detached from the main tumor, infiltrating the healthy tissue.

PMID: 36011038

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
