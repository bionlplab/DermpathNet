Programmed cell death protein-1 inhibitor-induced granuloma annulare and hypertrophic lichen planus masquerading as squamous cell carcinoma.

A, Biopsy findings show a hyperkeratotic, papillomatous proliferation of pale pink keratinizing squamous cells projecting down into the dermis with a dense infiltrate. There is focal loss of the granular layer. B, On higher power, the infiltrate is better appreciated as lymphocytic. C, Biopsy findings of an annular plaque show palisading lymphocytes and histiocytes around a core of degenerating collagen. D, Rebiopsy findings show again an acanthosis and papillomatosis of the epidermis with a dense lymphocytic infiltrate at the dermoepidermal junction. There is no atypia in this specimen. (Original magnifications: A, ×4; B to D, ×10.)

PMID: 30094305

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
