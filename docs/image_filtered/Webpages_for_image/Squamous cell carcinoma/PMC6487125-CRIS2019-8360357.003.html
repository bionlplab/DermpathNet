An Unusual Case of Mandibular Squamous Cell Carcinoma in Intimacy with an Impacted Wisdom Tooth.

Histopathology from initial biopsy of lesion and final resected specimen. (a) High-power (200x, H&E stain) magnification illustrating fragments of keratinizing conventional SCC from incisional biopsy of lesion. (b) Low-power (40x, H&E stain) magnification of tissue from final resected specimen showing SCC invasion into mandibular bone (lower left) with central tumor necrosis.

PMID: 31110834

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
