Primary breast squamous cell carcinoma: A case report at a tertiary hospital in Northern Tanzania.

(A) Photomicroscopy demonstrating dermal based invasive breast tumor composed of sheets or cords of squamous cells, H&E 20× original magnification. (B) Tumor is dissecting subcutis and fat tissue but with negative free margins, H&E 20× original magnification. (C) Sheets and trabeculi of tumor cells with the tendency to form keratin pearls, H&E 40× original magnification. (D) Immuno‐expression of the tumor cells with CK5/6, IHC 40× original magnification.

PMID: 37346880

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
