Pure basaloid squamous cell carcinoma of the uterine cervix: a case report.

Histopathologic findings. The tumor is mainly composed of immature basaloid squamous cells with scanty cytoplasm and peripheral palisading (arrow). Keratinization foci are seen in the center of the nests (arrowhead). Both tumor components show p63 immunopositivity (A: H&E, ×200; B: p63, ×200).

PMID: 19543425

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
