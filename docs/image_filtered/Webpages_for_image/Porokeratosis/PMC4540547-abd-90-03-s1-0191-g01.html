Porokeratosis ptychotropica of the scrotum: dermoscopic evaluation of an atypical presentation.

A - A single, annular plaque with hyperkeratotic, ridge-like border is observed in the right scrotum; B - Digital dermoscopy revealed a sharply demarcated annular lesion with a thick, peripheral light brown rim, limiting an erythematous nonatrophic centre with regular dotted vessels; C - Histopathology evidenced the presence of multiple cornoid lamellae (haematoxylin and eosin, original magnification x100)

PMID: 26312713

Type: Benign, Category: Keratinocytic
