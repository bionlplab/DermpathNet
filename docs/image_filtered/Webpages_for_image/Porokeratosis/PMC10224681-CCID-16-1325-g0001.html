Case Report: A Novel MVK Missense Mutation in the Sporadic Porokeratosis Ptychotropica in China.

Clinical manifestation and histopathological findings of Porokeratosis Ptychotropica. (A) Clinical features show “butterfly shaped” verrucous plaque on buttocks with multiple satellite papules. (B) Dermoscopic view (X10) of the skin lesion, similar to keratotic ridge: annular, brown-yellowish hyperkeratotic ridge (arrows) demarcating a central area with whitish scales and some globular vessels. (C) A histological hallmark of all variants of porokeratosis, cornoid lamella (CL) as a “column” overlying a zone of the epidermis (cornoid lamella, marked as red asterisk). Dyskeratotic cells and focal hypogranulosis were present in the epidermis underlying the CL. Here, localized subcutaneous inflammatory cells infiltration suggested an eruptive pruritic PPt (H&E staining, X100).

Type: Benign, Category: Keratinocytic
