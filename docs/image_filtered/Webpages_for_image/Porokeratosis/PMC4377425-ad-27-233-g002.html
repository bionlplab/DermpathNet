A case of solitary porokeratosis mimicking circumscribed palmar hypokeratosis.

(A) Histological analysis of the 4-mm punch biopsy specimen obtained from the patient shows an abrupt decrease in stratum corneum (H&E, ×40), (B) cornoid lamella on the margin of the lesion. And hypogranulosis and edematous changes of keratinocytes on the underlying epidermis (H&E, ×200).

PMID: 25834375

Type: Benign, Category: Keratinocytic
