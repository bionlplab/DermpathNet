Linear porokeratosis of the foot with dermoscopic manifestations: A case report.

Pathological images of skin lesions of porokeratosis. A: HE staining, ×100, B: HE staining, ×200, C: HE staining, ×400. Epidermis was hyperkeratosis with a high degree of hyperkeratosis, with columns of parakeratosis, thinning of the granular layer, thickening of the spinous layer, and a small amount of lymphocyte infiltration in the superficial dermis.

PMID: 36387824

Type: Benign, Category: Keratinocytic
