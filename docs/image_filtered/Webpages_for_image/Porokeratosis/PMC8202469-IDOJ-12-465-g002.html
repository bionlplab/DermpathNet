Linear Systematized Porokeratosis-A Rare Case and Dermoscopic Clues to Diagnosis.

(a) Dermoscopy of the periphery of lesion using DermLite II hybrid m; 3Gen; polarized mode, ×10 magnification showed a track made by a collarette of scale (black arrow) with two free edges broken at some places over a brown background. (b) Dermoscopy using DermLite II hybrid m; 3Gen; polarized mode, ×10 magnification showed hyperpigmented blackish rim inner to track made of collarette of scale (black circle), and a perifollicular cast (black arrow) near the centre

PMID: 34211921

Type: Benign, Category: Keratinocytic
