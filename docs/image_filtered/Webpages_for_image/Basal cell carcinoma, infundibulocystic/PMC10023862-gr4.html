A case of Gorlin syndrome like phenotype with multiple infundibulocystic basal cell carcinomas in a moniliform blepharosis arrangement.

Biopsy of right eyelid margin showing features consistent with infundibulocystic basal cell carcinoma. A well-circumscribed basaloid neoplasm composed of anastomosing buds and cords with peripheral palisading surrounded by fibromyxoid stroma. Few small keratinizing cystic structures are also present. (A and B, Hematoxylin-eosin stain; original magnifications: A, ×100; B, ×200). (single, 1.5 column).

PMID: 36941877

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
