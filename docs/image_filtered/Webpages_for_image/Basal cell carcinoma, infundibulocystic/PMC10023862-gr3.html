A case of Gorlin syndrome like phenotype with multiple infundibulocystic basal cell carcinomas in a moniliform blepharosis arrangement.

Dermoscopy images of eyelid margin. Black solid arrow points to yellow-white globule, which is visible with polarized and nonpolarized light. Blue solid arrow points to arborizing vessels. Yellow solid arrow points to brown globules. Black dashed arrow points to the healed biopsy site. Dermoscopic images were captured with a Canon EOS M100 Mirrorless Camera (Canon USA Inc) using Dermlite DL3N (3Gen) with Genteal (Alcon) lubricant eye gel for contact polarized (A) and contact nonpolarized (B) images at 10 × magnification.
