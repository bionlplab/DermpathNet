Keratoacanthoma, committed stem cells and neoplastic aberrant infundibulogenesis integral to formulating a conceptual model for an infundibulocystic pathway to squamous cell carcinoma.

A, Crateriform nonulcerated squamous cell carcinoma with papillomatous and keratotic cavity without prominent infundibular or infundibulocystic differentiation (H&E, x12.5). B, Detail of base with invasive cords with full‐thickness atypia and minimal maturation through enlarged keratinocytes (H&E, x50). C, Broad base deeply and widely invasive well‐differentiated infundibulocystic squamous cell carcinoma with fibrosis and lichenoid inflammation (H&E, x12.5)
