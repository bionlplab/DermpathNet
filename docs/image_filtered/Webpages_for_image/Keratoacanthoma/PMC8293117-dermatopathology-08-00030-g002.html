A Wolf in Sheep's Clothing: Collision of Melanoma and Keratoacanthoma.

Collision tumor consisting of keratoacanthoma and melanoma, Hematoxylin and Eosin stain (H&E). The small insert on the upper right side shows the edge of the keratoacanthoma with characteristic architecture and large pale keratinocytes.

PMID: 34287287

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
