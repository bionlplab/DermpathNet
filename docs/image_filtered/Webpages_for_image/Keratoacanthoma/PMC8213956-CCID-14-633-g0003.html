Expression of Heat Shock Protein 105 in Cutaneous Squamous Cell Carcinoma: Correlation with Clinicopathological Characteristics.

Clinical and histopathological appearances of Bowen (A, B; HE×10), verrucous carcinoma (D, E; HE×10), keratoacanthoma (G, H; HE×10) and acantholytic SCC (J, K; HE×10). HSP105 is highly expressed in Bowen, while shows a trend of lower expression in verrucous carcinoma, keratoacanthoma and acantholytic SCC (C, F, I, L; IHC×10).

PMID: 34163202

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
