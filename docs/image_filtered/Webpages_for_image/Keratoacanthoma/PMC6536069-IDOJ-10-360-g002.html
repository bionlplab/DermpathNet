Dermoscopy of Keratoacanthoma Centrifugum Marginatum.

Non-contact dermoscopy of scaly nodule under polarized mode using DermLite™ DL3 shows thick yellowish-white keratin (black stars) and multiple grayish-white circles (blue arrows), lines (red arrows), and structureless zones (red stars) on a pinkish background. The grayish-white circles are seen surrounding central whitish structure (yellow stars). Also seen are red globules (blue stars) in between the whitish zones (a). Dermoscopy of the non-scaly nodule shows multiple whitish globules (yellow stars) and lines (blue arrows) on an erythematous background (b). (original magnification ×10)

PMID: 31149601

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
