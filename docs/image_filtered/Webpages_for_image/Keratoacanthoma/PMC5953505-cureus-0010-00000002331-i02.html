Solitary Palmar Keratoacanthoma: Case Report.

Pathology features of keratoacanthomaA well-defined nodular invasive squamous neoplasm with a cup-shaped invagination on the periphery. Well-differentiated neoplastic glassy squamous epithelioid cells arranged in a haphazard pattern surrounding multiple islands of keratin, consistent with keratoacanthoma, depicted in 40x (A) and 100x (B) magnification views

PMID: 29770284

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
