Recurrence of a Cellular Blue Nevus with Satellitosis-A Diagnostic Pitfall with Clinical Consequences.

(a) Recurrent cellular blue cell nevus with strongly pigmented oval-shaped, spindle-shaped, and dendritic melanocytes and melanophages, occupying upper and mid dermis. (H&E, 200×). The tumor cells are diffusely positive for Melan-A (b) (200×), SOX10 (c) (200×), and BAP1 (d) (200×).

PMID: 36412623

Type: Benign, Category: Melanocytic
