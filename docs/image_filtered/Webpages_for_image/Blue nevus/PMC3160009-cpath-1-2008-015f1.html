Sclerosing 'mucinous' blue nevus: a clinical simulator of dermatofibroma.

(A) Fibro-mucinous stroma with spindle-shaped and dendritic melanocytes, which sometimes were arranged in whorls. Melanophages were present (20x Hematoxylin and Eosin). (B) Alcian blue staining confirmed the mucin deposition within the tumor (20x Mucin). (C) No reaction was found against MIB excluding a high proliferation rate (20x MIB-1). (D) MART-1 staining confirmed both the nature and the distribution of the melanocytes (20x MART-1).

PMID: 21876647

Type: Benign, Category: Melanocytic
