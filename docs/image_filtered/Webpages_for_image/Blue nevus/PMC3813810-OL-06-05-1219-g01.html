Endometrioid adenocarcinoma concurrent with a blue nevus of the endometrium and uterine cervix: A case report.

(A) Endometrial tumor. Proliferation of atypical columnar cells containing large round-to-oval nuclei that formed irregularly-shaped tubular and fused/cribriform glands [hematoxylin and eosin (HE) staining; magnification, ×100]. (B) Short spindle-shaped melanocytes in the endometrial stroma (arrows; HE staining; magnification, ×100). (C) Short spindle-shaped or polygonal melanocytes are also present in the stroma of the endocervix. These melanocytes are without atypia (inset) [HE staining; magnification, ×40, (inset) ×200].

PMID: 24179498

Type: Benign, Category: Melanocytic
