Atypical Spitz Tumor Arising on a Congenital Linear Plaque-Type Blue Nevus: A Case Report With a Review of the Literature on Plaque-Type Blue Nevus.

Immunohistochemical panel. The Ki67 labeling index was below 2% (immunoperoxidase, ×100). P16 expression was positive with some focal loss in Spitz lesion (immunoperoxidase, ×100). The melanocytes of both lesions were positive for HMB45 (immunoperoxidase, ×40 and ×100), positive for pS100 (immunoperoxidase, ×40), and positive for Melan-A (immunoperoxidase, ×40).

PMID: 25943242

Type: Benign, Category: Melanocytic
