Incomplete Urethral Duplication Associated with a Dermoid Cyst within a Vascular Hamartoma in a Female Dog.

Cross section of entire mass (20x; hematoxylin and eosin). The mass is composed of a moderately dense fibrovascular stroma containing numerous blood-filled channels surrounding a lumen lined primarily by urothelium with multifocal aggregates of well-differentiated hair follicles and associated adnexal structures; bar = 2 mm.

PMID: 31151175
