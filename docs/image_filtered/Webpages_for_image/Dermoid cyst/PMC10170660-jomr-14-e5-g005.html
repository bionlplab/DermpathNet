Co-existence of Congenital Epidermoid Cyst and Ranula in a Newborn. Report of a Unique Case.

Histopathologic examination (haematoxylin and eosin stain, original magnification x20). A = two large cystic spaces with epithelial lining; B = anteriorly, a keratin-filled cystic cavity with squamous epithelial lining; C = posteriorly, a cystic cavity with columnar or cuboidal cells in the epithelial lining.
