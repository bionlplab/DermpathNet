Coexisting sublingual dermoid cyst and heterotopic gastrointestinal cyst: Case report.

a: Heterotopic gastrointestinal cyst. The part shown consisted of gastrointestinal epithelium. The cystic lining cells included gastrointestinal cells such as mucus, parietal or chief cells (hematoxylin and eosin) (H&E stain) ×100. b: Dermoid cyst. The cyst is lined by a stratified squamous epithelium with a granular layer, and the cyst-filled space contains laminated keratin (hematoxylin and eosin) (H&E stain) ×100.

PMID: 29670741

Type: Benign, Category: Cystic
