Intradural intramedullary dermoid cyst in a 42-year-old man at the L1-L2 region.

Pathology slides with Hematoxylin and Eosin stain (H&E Stain) (A) - A single hair shaft is seen microscopically, corresponding to hairs seen intraoperatively (B) - Only keratotic debris were recovered histologically, with no lining epithelium identified (C)- A keratin pearl seen within the keratin debris.

PMID: 35634129

Type: Benign, Category: Cystic
