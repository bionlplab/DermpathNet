Dermoid cyst in the subcutaneous tissues of the back: A rare case with multimodal imaging and pathologic correlation.

(A) Grossly, the dermoid cyst was about 4.5 cm sized, thick walled, grayish-white unilocular cyst with focal rupture. (B) The cyst wall is lined by stratified squamous epithelium associated with sebaceous glands (H&E, ×10). (C) The cyst wall also contains foreign body type giant cells and inflammation (H&E, ×100). (D) In the cyst lumen, keratin debris is observed (H&E, ×200).

PMID: 33732406

Type: Benign, Category: Cystic
