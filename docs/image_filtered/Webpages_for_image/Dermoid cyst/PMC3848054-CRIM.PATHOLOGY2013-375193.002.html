Dermoid cyst of the pancreas: a report of an unusual case and a review of the literature.

Microscopic examination showed mature squamous epithelium with formation of a granular cell layer with underlying sebaceous glands (arrow), filling the cyst with laminated keratin (hematoxylin and eosin (H&E), 100x magnification).
