Beyond the Norm: Unveiling a Dermoid Cyst in an Inguinal Hernia Case.

Histopathological examination of the excised tissueA: A cystic space is lined by keratinizing stratified squamous epithelium, and the lumen is filled with loose keratin flakes. H&E stain, 10X magnification. B: The wall of the cyst harbors variable skin appendages, including pilosebaceous units and apocrine ducts, consistent with a dermoid cyst. H&E stain, 10X magnification.
