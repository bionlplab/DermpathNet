The Value of Ultrasonography in the Diagnosis of a Rare Congenital Dermoid Cyst of the Anterior Fontanelle in an Infant.

(a) Histological section of the cyst shows a benign cyst filled with keratin flakes (arrows) and lined by stratified squamous epithelium (arrowheads) with associated underlying mature skin adnexal structures, H&E ×40.
