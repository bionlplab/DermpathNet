Occurrence of dermoid cyst in the floor of the mouth: the importance of differential diagnosis in pediatric patients.

Surgical procedure to remove the dermoid cyst. A - Intraoral sublingual approach. The cyst lumen is generally filled with a mixture of desquamated keratin and sebum. B - The cyst is lined by orthokeratinized squamous epithelium. Underlying connective tissue stroma contains fibroblast and collagen fibers. The connective tissue exhibits variable numbers of sebaceous glands (black arrows) and blood vessels. Keratinous debris could also be seen in the lumen. Hematoxylin & eosin, original magnification 100x

PMID: 28678954

Type: Benign, Category: Cystic
