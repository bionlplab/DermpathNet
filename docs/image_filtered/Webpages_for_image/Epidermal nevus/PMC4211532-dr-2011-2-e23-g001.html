Patient with giant Becker's nevus and epidermal nevus.

Hyperpigmented scaly plaque on left lower extremity consistent with epidermal nevus (A). Biopsy of (A) showing increased basilar pigmentation, acanthosis, and hyper-keratosis(B, H&E 5X). Large hyperpigmented patch with irregular borders and satellite macules (C & D) consistent with Becker's nevus. Increased basilar pigmentation (E, H&E 10X) and smooth muscle proliferation (F, H&E 20X) in biopsies from lesion (C).

PMID: 25386275

Type: Benign, Category: Keratinocytic
