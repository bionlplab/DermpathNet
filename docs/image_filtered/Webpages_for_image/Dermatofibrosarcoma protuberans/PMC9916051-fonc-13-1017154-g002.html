Pediatric dermatofibrosarcoma protuberans: A clinicopathologic and genetic analysis of 66 cases in the largest institution in Southwest China.

Histologic findings of DFSP and corresponding immunohistochemical images. (A, B) The neoplastic cells infiltrated into subcutaneous fat tissues forming a characteristic “honeycomb” pattern (A, H&E × 200). The corresponding component showed diffuse positivity for CD34 (B, × 200). (C, D) The myxoid component of DFSP (case 40) could show patchy or focal staining for CD34 (C, H&E × 200; D, × 200). (E, F) The fibrosarcomatous component of DFSP (case 1) could exhibit loss for CD34 staining (E, H&E × 200; F, × 200).
