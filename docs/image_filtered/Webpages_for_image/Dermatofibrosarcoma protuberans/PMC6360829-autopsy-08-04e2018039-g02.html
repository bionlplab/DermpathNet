Fibrosarcomatous dermatofibrosarcoma protuberans from scar following trauma.

Photomicrograph of resected mass. A – demonstrates fibrosarcomatous differentiation of DFSP with cellular atypia with abundant mitotic activity (H&E, 40X); B – Immunohistochemistry staining for CD34 marker is diffusely expressed in the spindle cells and highlights the fascicular pattern of this tumor (20x).
