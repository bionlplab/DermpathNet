Dermatofibrosarcoma protuberans with pulmonary metastasis in the absence of local recurrence.

Histopathologic finding of the lung nodule. (A) The specimen of the lung biopsy shows infiltration of multiple spindle cells arranged in a storiform pattern (black arrows), identical to the tumor previously resected from the thigh (H&E, ×100). (B) The diffuse brown staining of the tumor cells in the lung nodule[s] shows the presence of CD34 (CD34, ×100).

PMID: 22783540

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
