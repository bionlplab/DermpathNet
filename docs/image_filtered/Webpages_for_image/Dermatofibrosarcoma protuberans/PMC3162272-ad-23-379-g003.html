A case of myxoid dermatofibrosarcoma protuberans.

(A) The tumor showed the characteristic storiform pattern in some parts of the lesion (H&E, ×100). (B) In addition to the typical storiform pattern, prominent myxoid stromal changes were demonstrated (H&E, ×100). (C) Bland spindle cells with oval nuclei were randomly embedded in the loose myxoid stroma (H&E, ×400).

PMID: 21909213

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
