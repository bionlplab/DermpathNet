Dermatofibrosarcoma protuberans on the chest with a variety of clinical features masquerading as a keloid: is the disease really protuberant?

Clinical features of the 3 different lesions: shiny atrophic plaque (8.0×10.0 cm), indurated erythema, and painful, keloid-like red nodules (largest nodule: 2.0×1.5 cm) on the anterior chest.

PMID: 25324663

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
