Recurrent Dermatofibrosarcoma Protuberans of Scalp in a Distant Location 10 Years after Primary Excision.

(A) Densely packed spindle cells in storiform pattern (H&E, ×40). (B) Spindle cells with elongated nuclei infiltrates to fat layer and shows honeycomb appearance (H&E, ×100). (C) Immunohistochemistry results shows positivity for CD34 (CD34, ×100).
