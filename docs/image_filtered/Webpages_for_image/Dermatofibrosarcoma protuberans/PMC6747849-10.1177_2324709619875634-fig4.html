Dermatofibrosarcoma Protuberans of the Breast in Man: An Extremely Rare Entity With a Review of the Literature.

Progressive magnification in hematoxylin-eosin staining: (A) ×50, (B) ×100, (C) ×200, and (D) ×400. The tumor showed densely packed spindle cells arranged in focal storiform as well as herringbone pattern with myxoid areas in between and infiltration of tumor cells into subcutaneous fatty tissue, with rare figure of mitosis.

PMID: 31522558

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
