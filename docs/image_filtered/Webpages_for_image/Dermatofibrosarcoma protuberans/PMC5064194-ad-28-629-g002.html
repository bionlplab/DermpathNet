Pedunculated Nodules as a Variant of Dermatofibrosarcoma Protuberans.

(A) Densely packed, uniform spindle cells with a storiform and cartwheel pattern (H&E, ×40). (B) The spindle cells spread into subcutis layer with honeycomb-like appearance (H&E, ×100). (C) Immunohistochemistry results showing uniform positivity for CD34 (CD34, ×200).

PMID: 27746644

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
