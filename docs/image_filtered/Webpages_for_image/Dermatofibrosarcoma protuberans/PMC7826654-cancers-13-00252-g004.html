Wilms' Tumor 1 (WT1): A Novel Immunomarker of Dermatofibrosarcoma Protuberans-An Immunohistochemical Study on a Series of 114 Cases of Bland-Looking Mesenchymal Spindle Cell Lesions of the Dermis/Subcutaneous Tissues.

(A) Typical nodular dermal neurofibroma (H&E; original magnification 100×), showing a heterogeneous/focal and weak positivity for WT1; (B) (immunoperoxidase; original magnification 150×); (C) low magnification showing morphological details of a deep fibrous histiocytoma, containing foci of skeletal muscle infiltration (H&E; original magnification 50×); immunohistochemical analysis showed no detectable staining for WT1 in the neoplastic cells; (D) cytoplasmic staining of blood vessels and skeletal muscle cells (immunoperoxidase; original magnification 50×).

PMID: 33445443

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
