Multifocal oral melanoacanthoma associated with Addison's disease and hyperthyroidism: a case report.

Oral melanoacanthoma. (A) Stratified squamous epithelium with hyperparakeratinized areas showing acanthosis, substantial deposition of melanin in basal and suprabasal layers (yellow arrows); (B) dendritic melanocytes (white arrow) in all epithelial layers (hematoxylin-eosin [H&E] stain, original magnification 200X and 400X, respectively); (C) and (D) positive HMB-45 expression in dendritic melanocytes extending across the epithelium (immunohistochemical reaction, original magnification 100X and 400X, respectively).

PMID: 28658350

Type: Benign, Category: Keratinocytic
