A rare presentation of multiple eruptive vellus hair cysts and dystrophic nails in a pediatric patient with Stüve-Wiedemann syndrome.

Histopathologic result of the biopsy of a vellus hair cyst in a child with SWS. A middermal cyst lined with a stratified squamous epithelium and within the cystic lumen are multiple vellus hair shafts and laminated keratinaceous material. (hematoxylin-eosin stain; original magnification: ×40.)

PMID: 37484655

Type: Benign, Category: Cystic
