A Rare Occurrence of Sebaceous Carcinoma, Sebaceoma, Syringocystadenoma Papilliferum, and Trichoblastoma in a Single Nevus Sebaceous Lesion.-DermPath

A Rare Occurrence of Sebaceous Carcinoma, Sebaceoma, Syringocystadenoma Papilliferum, and Trichoblastoma in a Single Nevus Sebaceous Lesion.

a Sebaceous carcinoma irregular lobules of basaloid cells in the dermis (H&E. ×20). b Mature sebocytes with vacuolated cytoplasm and scalloped nuclei (H&E. ×400). c Tumor cells show nuclear atypia, and several mitotic figures and necrosis are also seen (H&E. ×400). d Tumor cells showed positive reaction for adipophilin immunohistochemical stain (×400).

PMID: 34177517

Type: Benign, Category: Adnexal
