Syringocystadenoma papilliferum of the bony external auditory canal: a rare tumor in a rare location.

Histological appearance of the tumor, H&E stain. (a) Fragment of dermis with small irregular glands lined by a double-layered epithelium with micropapillary structures and focal squamous metaplasia. (b) Higher magnification of the tumor showing intraglandular micropapillary structures.

PMID: 23762705

Type: Benign, Category: Adnexal
