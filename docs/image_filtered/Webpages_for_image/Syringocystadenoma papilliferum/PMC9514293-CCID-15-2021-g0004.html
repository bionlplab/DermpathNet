Syringocystadenoma Papilliferum and Basal Cell Carcinoma Arising in Nevus Sebaceous.

Histological features. (A) Cystic invaginations extending into the dermis, with numerous papillary projections (black arrow), hyperplasia sebaceous glands in the dermis (red arrow), and stroma rich in plasma cells (HE ×20). (B) Columnar cells making up the luminal layer, and smaller cuboidal cells with larger nuclei and comparatively meager cytoplasm making up the outer layer (HE ×100). (C) Multiple basaloid cells in the dermis in the form of lump or cord, peripheral palisading, focal retraction from the stroma, and increased pigment. A lymphocytic infiltrate in the stroma (HE ×100).

PMID: 36176943

Type: Benign, Category: Adnexal
