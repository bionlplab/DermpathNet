Syringocystadenoma papilliferum in an unusual location.

A and B: Anatomopathological examination of lesion biopsy stained with hematoxilyn and eosin showed papillomatous epidermis presenting multiple papillary cystic invaginations which extended to the dermis (HEx10). C: Detail of glandular epithelium constituted by an external layer of cuboid cells, with round nuclei and scarce cytoplasm, and an internal layer of cylindrical cells with decapitation secretion (HEx40) D: Detail of decapitation secretion and a plasmocyte-rich inflammatory infiltrate (HE x40)

PMID: 26734876

Type: Benign, Category: Adnexal
