Epithelioid Hemangioendothelioma and Epithelioid Hemangioma: Pazopanib as a Potential Salvage Therapy.

A, B Tumor composed of medium size, round-to-oval epithelioid cells with a myxoid matrix resembling cartilage. Numerous multinucleated giant cells are also present. Original magnification, ×10 and ×20. C, D Immunostain for CD31 is positive. The remaining immunoprofile showed strong reactivity for other vascular markers (CD34, Factor VIII, and FLI-1) as well as some staining for CK7 and CD10. Original magnification, ×20.

PMID: 33776722

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
