Cutaneous verrucous carcinoma: A clinicopathological study of 21 cases with long-term clinical follow-up.

Histopathological changes in CVC. (A) Low magnification showed an exo-endophytic growth pattern (H&E, ×10). (B) Exophytic component showed prominent parakeratosis and exudation without koilocytotic cells (H&E, ×100). (C) Intraepithelial microabscesses in severe keratinization tumor cells (H&E, ×400). (D) Basal cell atypia and mitosis (arrow) can be observed (H&E, ×400). (E) Blunted rete ridge with pushing margins (H&E, ×200). (F) Severe dermal inflammation of lymphocytes, plasma cells, and eosinophils (H&E, ×400). CVC, cutaneous verrucous carcinoma.

PMID: 36313691

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
