A Case Report of an Early Response to Definitive Chemoradiation for Esophageal Carcinoma Cuniculatum.

Findings posttreatment. (a) PET/CT noting persistent hypermetabolism and wall thickening in the distal esophagus. (b) Endoscopic finding of severe stenosis in the distal esophagus. (c) H&E stains demonstrating an invasive squamous cell carcinoma (200x and 400x, respectively). This is a higher grade than seen on initial biopsy and may represent a hybrid verrucous carcinoma. There is evidence of radiation treatment effect in the invasive nests.

PMID: 32373377

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
