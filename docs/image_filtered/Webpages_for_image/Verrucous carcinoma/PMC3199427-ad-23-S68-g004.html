Treatment of verrucous carcinoma of the lower lip with topical imiquimod (aldara®) and debulking therapy.

A biopsy specimen revealed marked parakeratosis, neoplastic proliferation, and downward invasion of epidermal keratinocytes with a blunt border and a pushed-down, bulldozing appearance (H&E, ×20).

PMID: 22028577

Type: Premalignant/Malignant, Category: Keratinocytic
