Axillary Hibernoma in woman with Lobular breast cancer and MEN1 syndrome: A case report.

H&E stain 20x: Hematoxylin-eosin staining revealed the presence of a cluster of small neoplastic cells in a fibrous septum of the axillary hybernoma (original magnification: × 20). E-Cadhein 20x: Neoplastic cells resulted totally negative for the immunostaining for E- cadherin (original magnification: × 20).CK 8-18 20x: Cytokeratins 8–18 are strongly expressed by neoplastic cells, confirming their epithelial nature (original magnification: × 20).GATA-3 20x: Epithelial neoplastic cells stained positively for GATA-3, indicative of their mammary origin (original magnification: × 20).

PMID: 33395907

Type: Benign, Category: Adipocytic
