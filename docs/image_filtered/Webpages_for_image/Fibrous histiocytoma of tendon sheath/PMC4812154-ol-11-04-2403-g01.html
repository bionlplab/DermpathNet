An unresectable retroperitoneal malignant fibrous histiocytoma: A case report.

Microscopy and photomicrography of the tumor prior to chemotherapy. (A) Microscopic examination revealed the tumor was composed of a mixture of spindle cells in a storiform pattern and polygonal or rounded cells (hematoxylin and eosin staining; magnification, x100). (B) Immunohistochemical staining revealed the tumor cells were positive for vimentin (magnification, x400).

PMID: 27073487

Type: Benign, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
