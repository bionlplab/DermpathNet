CyberKnife radiotherapy for malignant fibrous histiocytoma of the chest wall: A case report and review of the literature.

Pathological features. Histopathological examination revealed (A and B) the presence of pleomorphic spindle cells arranged in sheets and fascicles with a prominent storiform pattern (stain, hematoxylin and eosin; magnification, ×100 for A and ×400 for B) and (C and D) immunostaining for vimentin in the majority of tumor cells (stain, immunoperoxidase; magnification, ×100 for C and ×400 for D).

PMID: 24932251

Type: Benign, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
