Malignant fibrous histiocytoma of chest wall.

Microscopic findings. A. The mass adhered to and infiltrated the inner surface of the rib (arrow). One segment of the mass (arrowhead) situated within the bony defect (H-E stain, × 40). B. Storiform arrangement of malignant cells, including a plump cytoplasm and a few mitotic figures, was suggestive of storiform-pleomorphic malignant fibrous histiocytoma (H-E stain, × 100).

PMID: 15744825

Type: Benign, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
