Giant cell malignant fibrous histiocytoma of the breast: a case report.

Microscopic findings of the tumor. Low power (A, H&E, ×100) and high power (B, H&E, ×400) examinations show many multinucleated osteoclast-like giant cells and hyperchromatic mononuclear tumor cells.

PMID: 15201521

Type: Benign, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
