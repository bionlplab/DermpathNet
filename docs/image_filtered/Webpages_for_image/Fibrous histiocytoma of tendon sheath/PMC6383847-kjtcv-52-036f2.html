Long-Term Survival after Wide Resection of Malignant Fibrous Histiocytoma of the Chest Wall.

Pathologic findings of the lesion. (A) Storiform arrangement of malignant cells with plump cytoplasm and the presence of a few mitotic figures suggesting storiform-pleomorphic malignant fibrous histiocytoma (H&E stain, ×200). (B) Typical features with areas of varying cellularity, thick-walled blood vessels, and Verocay bodies (H&E stain, ×200).

PMID: 30834216

Type: Benign, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
