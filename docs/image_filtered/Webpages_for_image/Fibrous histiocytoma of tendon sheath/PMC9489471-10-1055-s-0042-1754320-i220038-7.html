Operative Technique: Angiomatoid Fibrous Histiocytoma-Unique Case and Management.

Histological slides of this intracranial angiomatoid fibrous histiocytoma. ( A ) The lesion demonstrates a nodular proliferation with blood-filled pseudoangiomatous spaces and abundant hemosiderin deposition. The characteristic lymphoplasmacytic cuff often seen in this entity is absent in this case (x40 magnification). ( B ) Syncytial growth of bland spindled to epithelioid tumor cells; cytologic atypia is minimal, and mitotic figures are inconspicuous (x200 magnification). ( C ) The lesional cells show focal positivity for CD99 (x200 magnification) and ( D ) strong, diffuse staining for desmin (x200 magnification).

PMID: 36148089

Type: Benign, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
