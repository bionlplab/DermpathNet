(a) Presence of foamy histiocytes (H and E, ×10); (b) foamy histiocytes and giant cells (H and E, ×20); (c) spindle-shaped fibroblasts and histiocytes seen in storiform and fascicular pattern (H and E, ×4); (d) CD68 positive cells (×40)
