A case of radiation-induced sternal malignant fibrous histiocytoma treated with neoadjuvant chemotherapy and surgical resection.

A) The tumor consisted of cells with spindle character forming non-marked whirlpool-like structures at certain places and fewer oval cells (H&E.200×). B) It has been observed that tumor cells were strongly stained with CD68 immunohistochemically (CD68 200×).

PMID: 19116008

Type: Benign, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
