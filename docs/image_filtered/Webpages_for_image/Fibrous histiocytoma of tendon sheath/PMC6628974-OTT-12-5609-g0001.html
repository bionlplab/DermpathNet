Endoscopic submucosal dissection of tracheal deep benign fibrous histiocytoma using hybrid knife.

(A) Chest CT revealed an endotracheal mass of 11 mm in diameter. (B) Bronchoscopy showed a well circumscribed posterior soft mass occluding over 95% of the tracheal lumen. (C) The endotracheal mass was treated with electrocautery snare and APC via bronchoscope.
