Cytopathologic features of an unusual case of multiple eccrine spiradenomas misdiagnosed as a malignant round cell tumor.

(a) Post-contrast MRI displaying multiple, discrete-to-confluent lesions (arrow) over the left shoulder. (b) FNAC smears showing cohesive cluster of cells with round-to-oval nuclei and scant cytoplasm, including relatively darker, smaller nuclei at the periphery, along with interspersed metachromatic/pink hyaline droplets (MGG, ×200). (c) Higher magnification displaying rosetting arrangement of tumor cells around hyaline droplet (MGG, ×400). (d) Cluster of bland cells, including cells with pale and dense chromatin, forming rosette-like structures (H&E, ×400)

PMID: 28182081

Type: Benign, Category: Adnexal
