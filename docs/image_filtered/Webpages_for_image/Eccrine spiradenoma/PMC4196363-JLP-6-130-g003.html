Fine needle aspiration cytology of eccrine spiradenoma of back: report of a rare case.

Fine-needle aspiration cytology showing (a) admixture of pale large epithelial cells and darker spindle to oval myoepithelial cells and occasional lymphocytes (Papanicolaou, ×100); (b) tumor cells arranged around perivascular space forming a rosette-like structure (arrow)(Leishman– Giemsa, ×400)
