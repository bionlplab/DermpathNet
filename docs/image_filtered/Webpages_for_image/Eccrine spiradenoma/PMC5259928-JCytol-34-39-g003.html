Cytopathologic features of an unusual case of multiple eccrine spiradenomas misdiagnosed as a malignant round cell tumor.

Immunohistochemical results. (a) CK7 highlighting the luminal cells. (DAB, x400) (b) S100-P positivity within tumor cells (DAB, ×400). (c). EMA highlighting the lumens within tumor cell clusters (DAB, ×400). (d) Ki-67/MIB1 highlighting 5–10% tumor cell nuclei (DAB, ×200). (e) Tumor cells displaying weak CD117/C-KIT positivity (DAB, ×400)

PMID: 28182081

Type: Benign, Category: Adnexal
