Multiple Segmental Eccrine Spiradenoma with a Zosteriform Pattern: A Case Report and Literature Review.-DermPath

Multiple Segmental Eccrine Spiradenoma with a Zosteriform Pattern: A Case Report and Literature Review.

(A) Multiple well-circumscribed dermal nodules comprising basophilic cells in the deep dermis. The basaloid cells are arranged in a trabecular pattern with eosinophilic fibrous strands (H&E, ×40). (B) High-powered view demonstrating small dark basaloid cells with hyperchromatic nuclei, larger cells with pale nuclei in the center of the clusters, and numerous lymphocytes scattered throughout (H&E, ×400).

PMID: 26273161

Type: Benign, Category: Adnexal
