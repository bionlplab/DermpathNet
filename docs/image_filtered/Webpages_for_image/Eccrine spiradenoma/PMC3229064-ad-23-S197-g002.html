Giant vascular eccrine spiradenoma.

(A) Well-demarcated, encapsulated, multilobulated tumor in the dermis with many blood-filled vessels and extensive hemorrhage (H&E, ×25). (B) The tumor was composed of two types of cells: cells with small, dark nuclei at the periphery of the lobules and cells with large, pale nuclei in the center of the lobules (H&E, ×200).

PMID: 22148049

Type: Benign, Category: Adnexal
