Eccrine Spiradenoma of the Scalp.

Microscopic findings of the tumor. (A) Well-circumscribed and encapsulated tumor nodule arising from the dermis (H&E, ×40). (B) Tumor lobules composed of two cell types. Peripherally, smaller dark blue cells and centrally, pale larger cells. Large cells showing luminal differentiation containing eosinophilic material at the center (H&E, ×200).

PMID: 29090205

Type: Benign, Category: Adnexal
