Infantile Fibromatosis: A Rare Cause of Anterior Mediastinal Mass in a Child.-DermPath

Infantile Fibromatosis: A Rare Cause of Anterior Mediastinal Mass in a Child.

3-year-old female with noisy breathing and exertional dyspnoea with suspected mediastinal mass. Histological examination of the specimen; Photomicrograph with the hematoxylin and eosin stained tissue (a) at magnification of ×100 shows hypocellular tumor (circle) consisting of bland spindle cells, (b) at magnification of ×20 shows tumor adherent to thymic tissue (black arrow).

PMID: 26180657

Type: Benign, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
