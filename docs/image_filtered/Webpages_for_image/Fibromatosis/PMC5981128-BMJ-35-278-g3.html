Successful Management of Aggressive Fibromatosis of the Neck: A Case Report.

Histopathologic examination of the tumor under low (a, x20) and high (b, x100) power magnification reveals a proliferation of well-differentiated fibroblasts within a collagenous stroma, with infiltration of (H&E stain). Immunohistochemistry shows focally positive cytoplasmic reaction for smooth muscle actin [immune-staining against smooth muscle actin (c, x40), (d, x400)].

PMID: 29843498

Type: Benign, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
