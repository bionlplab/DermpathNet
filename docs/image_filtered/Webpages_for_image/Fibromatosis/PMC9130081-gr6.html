A desmoid-type fibromatosis in the retroperitoneum of the gastro-pancreatic region that was resected with a distal pancreatomy: a case report.

Hematoxylin and eosin stain with 4x revealed abnormal proliferation of spindle-shaped cells (A). Immunohistochemical staining with beta-catenin was positive (B). Desmoid-type fibromatosis attached to pancreas (C, yellow arow) and splenic artery (D, yellow arow).

PMID: 35634017

Type: Benign, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
