Generalized hereditary gingival fibromatosis in a child: clinical, histopathological and therapeutic aspects.

Photomicrographs of the gingiva. A and B - Gingival connective tissue poorly vascularized and exhibiting marked deposition of collagen fibers underlying an epithelium with acanthosis and elongated rete ridges (H&E, original magnification 100X and 200X for A and B, respectively); C and D - Picro-sirius red staining reveals that the enlarged gingival tissue is primarily the result of an increased amount of collagen fiber bundles (picro-sirius stain, original magnification 200X for C and D).

PMID: 32039068

Type: Benign, Category: Fibrohistiocytic
