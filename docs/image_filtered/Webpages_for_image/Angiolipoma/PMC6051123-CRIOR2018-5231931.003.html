Hemorrhagic Sudden Onset of Spinal Epidural Angiolipoma.

Histopathological analysis of the resected tumors. The spinal angiolipoma consists of both mature fatty tissue and abnormal proliferating vascular elements with thin or expanded walls, which usually contain fibrin thrombi (hematoxylin and eosin stained; magnification: (a) ×40 and (b) ×100).

PMID: 30057841

Type: Benign, Category: Adipocytic
