MRI of intraneural perineurioma of the brachial plexus.

18-year-old female with intraneural perineurioma. Hematoxylin-eosin (H&E) stain displays foci of hypercellularity arranged in a whorl-like formation (onion bulbs).

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
