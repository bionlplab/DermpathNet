Oral intraneural perineurioma. Report of two cases.

A - Onion-bulb-like structures composed of central Schwann cells and axons surrounded by circumferential proliferation of spindle-shaped perineurial neoplastic cells (hematoxylin and eosin, original magnification x200). B - Perineurial cells immunoreactive for epithelial membrane antigen encircle negative Schwann cells and axons (original magnification x200). C - Immunoexpression of S-100 protein in the myelin sheath and Schwann cells, surrounded by perineurial cells that were negative for S-100 protein(original magnification x200)

PMID: 19841714

Type: Benign, Category: Neural or neuroendocrine
