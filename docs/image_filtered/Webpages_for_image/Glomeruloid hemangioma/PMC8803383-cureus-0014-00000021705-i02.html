A Case of Oral Glomeruloid Hemangioma Without Systemic Conditions.

Histochemical and immunohistochemical features: (a) PAS staining highlights cytoplasmic granules (black arrows) in the cytoplasm; (b) Double immunofluorescence staining shows CD31-positive endothelial cells (green) and yellow cytoplasmic IgGκ deposition (white arrows); (c) αSMA is faintly positive in stromal cells but positive in normal vascular smooth muscle cells (arrowheads); Stromal cells are positive for (d) CD 146, (e) nestin, and (f) type IV-collagenScale bars: 10 µm in (a) and (b), and 20 µm in (c)-(f)PAS: periodic acid-Schiff; αSMA; α-smooth muscle actin

PMID: 35145824

Type: Benign, Category: Vascular
