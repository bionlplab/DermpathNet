Malignant pilomatricoma in a dog with local and distant metastases treated with chemotherapy and bisphosphonates.

Bone metastasis of malignant pilomatricoma (biopsy from left scapula). Epithelial cells (arrows) with high nuclear/cytoplasmic ratio and hyperchromatic nuclei infiltrate the space between trabeculae of remodeled woven bone. Haematoxylin and eosin, 10x magnification.

PMID: 28795016

Type: Benign, Category: Follicular
