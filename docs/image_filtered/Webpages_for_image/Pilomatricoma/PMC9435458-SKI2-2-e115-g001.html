Pseudobullous pilomatricoma: A rare variant of pilomatricoma.

(a) A single, pink‐colored bullous‐like lesion on the right shoulder. Histopathological findings of the excised tissue; (b) histopathology showed oedema in the superficial dermis, tumour nests with fibrous capsule in the deep dermis. (H&E × 0.45); (c) dilated thin‐walled vascular structures within the upper dermis, representing dilated lymphatics, with marked oedema leading to disruption and separation of collagen bundles. (H&E × 5.0); (d) deeper in the dermis, there was an admixture of basaloid and ghost cells with focal calcifications, consistent with pilomatricoma. (H&E × 2.5); (e) lymphatic vessels were staining positive for D2‐40 in the upper dermis. (D2‐40 × 10); (f) near total loss of elastic fibres, and mild collagen disruption. (Verhoeff–Van Gieson × 20)

PMID: 36092264

Type: Benign, Category: Follicular
