Rapidly growing giant pilomatricoma in the right parotid region of a pregnant woman.

Microscopic examination revealing typical features of pilomatricoma. (A) A nodular, encapsulated tumor was found in the subcutaneous tissue with central necrosis (asterisk) and peritumoral mild lymphocytic infiltration (H&E, ×40). (B) The tumor was composed of outer basaloid and basophilic cells (black arrow) and central eosinophilic cells (white arrow) under the thick fibrous capsule, with anucleated ghost cells (arrowheads) characteristic of pilomatricoma (H&E, ×100).

PMID: 32630990

Type: Benign, Category: Follicular
