Ossifying pilomatricoma and a novel hypothesis for its pathogenesis: A case report and comprehensive literature review.

(A) Histopathological study shows a well-circumscribed nodular tumor with extensive ossification (H&E, 15×). (B) Histopathological study shows shadow cell clusters, laminated trabecular bones, osteoclast-like multinucleated giant cells, and bone marrow structures enclosed by bony trabeculae (H&E, 100×). (C) Immunohistochemical study shows a few cells (arrows) positive for antimyeloperoxidase antibodies within the bone marrow structure (400×).

PMID: 35147098

Type: Benign, Category: Follicular
