Fibrous hamartoma of infancy with sarcomatous transformation: an unusual morphology.

Photomicrographs of the tumor. A - Diffuse sheets of immature mesenchyme with intervening blood vessels (H&E x400); B - Fascicles of fibroblasts observed in between immature mesenchyme with lobules of adipocytes (H&E x400); C - Dilated blood vessels at the periphery of the lesion (H&E x400); D - Neoplastic cells constituting the immature mesenchyme depicting round to elongated cells with conspicuous nucleoli and scant cytoplasm (H&E x1000).
